# Health and Safety Incident Management OSHA Content Pack release notes

Version history for the Health and Safety Incident Management OSHA Content Pack on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 11.3.0 - June 2026**

    Changed: Out-of-the-box role inheritance and ACL review and adjustments for subscription alignment

-   **Version 11.2.0 - May 2026**

    New: Validates role existence and ACL references, fixing ACL role mismatches caused by invalid roles.

-   **Version 11.1.0 - January 2026**
    -   New: CSV format for OSHA 300A export
    -   Changed: CSV is now the default export file type
-   **Version 11.0.0 - December 2025**

    Fixed: Fixed issue with success message.

-   **Version 10.0.0 - August 2025**

    Changed: Minor ACL updates.

-   **Version 9.0.0 - May 2025**

    Minor bug fixes.

-   **Version 8.0.0 - November 2024**
    -   New: OSHA reports can be exported as a CSV file to align with the latest OSHA reporting rules
    -   Changed: Export modal now provides an option of exporting to CSV or PDF
-   **Version 7.0.0 - August 2024**

    Fixed: Incorrect tooltip shown on "Generate OSHA form" button and "Cancel" button.

-   **Version 6.0.0 - May 2024**
    -   Fixed:
    -   -   Missing OSHA buttons on the incident playbook for a few roles
-   Empty OSHA 300 and 300A forms when generating for the previous year
-   Date time discrepancy issues
-   **Version 5.0.0 - February 2024**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Includes all previous fixes included in the v4 patch releases:
            -   Fixes for OSHA compliance updates
            -   Free text entry for hospital address entry
-   **Version 4.0.3 - December 2023**

    Changed: Enabled free text entry for hospital address on injury illness reports in Health and Safety incidents.

-   **Version 4.0.2 - September 2023**

    Fixed: Fixes for OSHA compliance updates.

-   **Version 4.0.0 - August 2023**

    Changed: Updated the OSHA functionality to integrate with the incident playbook.

-   **Version 3.0.0 - May 2023**
    -   New: Added support for internationalization
    -   Fixed:
        -   ACL issues
        -   Issues related to incident occurrence date and time
        -   Workbench loading issues
        -   SQL related exceptions
        -   Localization issues
        -   RCA UI issues
        -   Affected employee issues
-   **Version 2.0.2 - March 2023**

    Fixed: Fixes for health and safety incident management.

-   **Version 2.0.0 - February 2023**

    This application enables autofill and export of OSHA 301, 300, and 300A reports with the click of a button from the ServiceNow Health and Safety application. The Health and Safety includes a dedicated role for managing these tasks, as well as an establishment list where your annual reports are saved per establishment.
