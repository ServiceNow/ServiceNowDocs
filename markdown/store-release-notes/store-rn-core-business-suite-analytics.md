# Core Business Suite Analytics release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Core Business Suite Analytics application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.1.0 - June 2026**

    Core Business Suite Analytics gives a consolidated dashboard, cross-business-unit view of service performance- including open and unassigned cases, mean time to resolve, and self-service resolution.
