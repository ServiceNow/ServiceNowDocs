# Total Cost of Ownership release notes

Version history for the Total Cost of Ownership application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.0 - June 2026**

    New: Added support for query\_range access control lists \(ACLs\).

-   **Version 1.0.5 - March 2026**

    Changed: Made certain fields read-only.

-   **Version 1.0.3 - November 2024**

    Fixed: Access control fixes.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - May 2024**

    Fixed: Fiscal period issue is fixed.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - February 2024**

    Use Total Cost of Ownership to evaluate the cost of your business applications and leverage the application costs to prioritize the application portfolio. Rationalize business applications by their cost and align them with the organization's business strategy.
