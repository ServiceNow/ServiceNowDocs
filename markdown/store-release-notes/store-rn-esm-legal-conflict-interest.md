# Legal Conflict of Interest release notes

Version history for the Legal Conflict of Interest application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 4.11.0 - June 2026**

    Changed: Security enhancements

-   **Version 4.10.3 - April 2026**

    Fixed: Risk assessments in Conflict of Interest now only run for conflict types that have applicable risk factors, and no longer trigger incorrectly for Personal Relationship disclosures.

-   **Version 4.10.2 - March 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Conversational intake to submit disclosures using natural language from the Now Assist in Virtual Agent.
        -   Risk assessment utility to evaluate the request against predefined risk factors and calculate risk score. Based on the score, requests are routed to the appropriate approval workflow for faster resolution.
-   **Version 4.7.0 - December 2025**

    Changed: Security enhancements.

-   **Version 4.6.0 - May 2025**

    Changed: Improved Legal Conflict of Interest icon for improved space utilization in the intake form.

-   **Version 4.5.4 - March 2025**

    New: Legal Conflict of Interest application is also available as part of the Legal Simple Compliance suite.

-   **Version 4.5.3 - February 2025**

    Legal Conflict of Interest application has been aligned for Yokohama release.

-   **Version 4.3.0 - November 2024**

    Fixed: In the Employee Center, when you open a draft legal request, the form was not displaying previously entered information. This is now fixed.

-   **Version 4.2.3 - August 2024**

    Fixed: Values in Conflict type field are now displayed in the Conflict of Interest Disclosure intake form.

-   **Version 4.1.6 - August 2023**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Addressed an issue while transferring a General Legal Request to Conflict of Interest type.
        -   Addressed intake form configuration when rest of the compliance apps are installed or not to show up category.
-   **Version 4.1.2 - February 2023**
    -   Changed:
        -   Added dependency on Legal Practice Apps Core
        -   Added the dark theme and improved icon look when submitting request through Intake form
-   **Version 5.2.1 - November 2022**
    -   New: Added new widgets for the delegated legal requests and legal matters.
    -   Changed:
        -   Updated titles of existing widgets that had open legal requests or open legal matters.
        -   Reordered the Add and Remove buttons in the Legal Matter Tasks tab.
-   **Version 4.0.0 - August 2022**
    -   New:
        -   Changes made by requesters to their existing Conflict of Interest records appear as additional comments in the Activities stream of the records.
-   **Version 3.2.1 - February 2022**
    -   New: Get insight, analyze trends, and drill down into the details of Conflict of Interest requests from the new Legal Executive dashboard, which is installed via Performance Analytics Content Pack for Legal Service Delivery app.
    -   Changed: Legal: LPA: Conflict of Interest has been renamed Legal Conflict of Interest
-   **Version 3.1.1 - December 2021**
    -   New: Added new email notifications for various conflict of interest requests.
-   **Version 3.0.3 - September 2021**
    -   New: Request legal services related to conflict of interest from Employee Center portal
    -   Fixed: Minor fixes
-   **Version 2.2.0 - June 2021**
    -   New:
        -   Intake form for various conflict types
        -   Conflict of Interest workflow
        -   Database of all disclosed conflicts
