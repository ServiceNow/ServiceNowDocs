# Now Assist for Enterprise Asset Management \(EAM\) release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Now Assist for Enterprise Asset Management \(EAM\) application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - April 2026**
    -   AI-powered agentic flows, agents, and skills to manage enterprise assets throughout the full asset life cycle, across planning, procurement, deployment, maintenance, and retirement.
    -   This app is designed to work with Enterprise Asset Management, OT Asset Management, Enterprise Asset Management for Healthcare, and Enterprise Asset Management for DCNAM.
