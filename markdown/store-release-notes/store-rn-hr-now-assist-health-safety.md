# Now Assist for Health and Safety release notes

Version history for the Now Assist for Health and Safety application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.4.1 - June 2026**

    New: Contextual Action Planner sidebar in the Health and Safety Workspace: Create actions manually or from AI suggestions, and review them in context across incidents, observations, investigations, cases, audits, work permits, risk assessments, meetings, etc.

-   **Version 1.3.2 - May 2026**

    Fixed: Updated orders for new third party models for incident summarization

-   **Version 1.3.1 - April 2026**

    Changed: Subscription entries for compatibility with AI-native SKU versions

-   **Version 1.3.0 - March 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Introduced AI gradient styling updates for the incident summarization experience, aligning the Now Assist UI with updated design standards across the Health &amp; Safety workspace.
        -   Applied the AI gradient visual treatment to the incident summarization Gen AI skill output panel, improving visual consistency and branding alignment.
        -   Delivered an AI-powered Health &amp; Safety Incident Pattern Analysis capability that automatically identifies recurring incident patterns across multiple dimensions \(location, time, people, assets, injuries, and categories\). The feature surfaces pattern clusters with confidence-scored tiers \(Exploratory, Moderate, Strong\), provides natural-language summaries via Now Assist, recommends preventive action plans, and enforces role-based Access Control \(RBAC\) to mask sensitive incidents for unauthorized users.
        -   Added a Gen AI-powered Pattern Analysis dashboard to the Health &amp; Safety Workspace, enabling safety investigators to visualize and interact with detected incident patterns and AI-generated summaries directly within their workspace.
    -   Fixed: Fixed an issue where AI security ACL rules were incorrectly triggering role masking warnings for users.
-   **Version 1.2.0 - January 2026**
    -   New: Improved summarization role security
    -   Fixed: Added missing mapping record to allow full 3P model support
-   **Version 1.1.2 - October 2025**

    Fixed: Issue with prompt title for summarization.

-   **Version 1.1.1 - October 2025**

    Fixed: Issue with summarization prompt title.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - September 2025**

    Fixed: Improved security of AI agent flows and ACLs.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - August 2025**

    Changed: True up to Zurich.

-   **Version 1.0.3 - June 2025**

    Xanadu Patch 9 and Now Assist for Platform 6.3.0 support.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - May 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Updated to be provided with AI Agents for Health and Safety application.
        -   Verified new LLMs provided by ServiceNow AI Platform work with existing skills.
-   **Version 1.0.0 - November 2024**

    Now Assist for Health and Safety enhances your ServiceNow Health and Safety Incident Management experience. It includes incident summarization which makes it easy to summarize the details of safety incidents. The summarization feature enhances the incident management experience by summarizing the incident record details, along with any root cause analyses and any corrective or preventive actions that were assigned when working on safety incidents in the Health and Safety Workspace.
