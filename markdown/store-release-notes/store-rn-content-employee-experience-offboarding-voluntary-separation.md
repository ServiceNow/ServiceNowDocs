# Employee Experience Pack: Offboarding - Voluntary Separation release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Offboarding - Voluntary Separation application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - November 2022**
    -   Voluntary Separation is when an employee willingly leaves the organization \(for example resignation, quit without notice, completion of a contract\). The Voluntary Separation Employee Experience Pack serves as a blueprint for the process of offboarding an employee of an organization during a voluntary separation \(resignation\).
    -   Problem it solves:
        -   More customers are looking to ServiceNow to deliver best-practice guidance and leadership on how to create great experiences during complex moments that matter.
    -   Employees and their managers need guidance through the offboarding process to ensure the efficiency and thoroughness of cross-departmental teams while providing a supportive experience for the employee during the transition. This journey combines activities and content for lifecycle events, tasks, communication, and knowledge template content, to better enable hiring managers through this process.
    -   \*\* Support is available on the ServiceNow community linked under Supporting Links and Docs on the right side of this page.
