# Employee experience taxonomy release notes

Version history for the Employee experience taxonomy application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 28.2.5 - December 2025**

    Updated fix script for a smoother upgrade experience for all employee taxonomies, customized or OOTB.

-   **Version 28.2.1 - August 2025**

    Updated to support the latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 28.0.9 - May 2025**

    Uptake of latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 28.0.7 - November 2024**
    -   Topic-Level Access Control:
        -   Added access control based on taxonomy topics to control which employees can see specific topics.
        -   Optimized portal performance by removing the dependency to check access for individual content items. \(Requires Yokohama family release\)
-   **Version 28.0.1 - August 2024**

    Added a new taxonomy structure and Topic Page experience for Kiosk to provide additional support content. Provisioned new taxonomy contributors to EC Pro kiosk taxonomy.

-   **Version 27.3.1 - November 2023**

    Taxonomy changes for updated information architecture \(IA\) for Employee Center and Employee Center Pro.

-   **Version 27.2.1 - August 2023**

    Added a topic to the legal section of the taxonomy in support of the contract management changes.

-   **Version 22.0.3 - September 2021**

    Leverage a pre-built content taxonomy for IT, HR, Workplace Services, Legal, and Procurement. The Employee Experience Taxonomy delivers an employee-centric taxonomy and topics that bring together services and information \(requests, articles, quick links, employee communications\), making it easier for employees to find what they need.
