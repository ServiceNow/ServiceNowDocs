# AI Service Graph Connector for Snowflake release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® AI Service Graph Connector for Snowflake application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.3 - June 2026**
    -   Integration with Snowflake which would allow discovery and inventory of AI agents, related models, prompts, tools and dataset information. Also include tracking usage of AI agents.
    -   The AI Control Tower \(AICT\) imports the discovered artifacts into its AI inventory, where the AI steward and Product Owner can access and review them.
-   **Version 2.0.1 - May 2026**

    This integration connects AI Control Tower’s AI Discovery capabilities with Snowflake, enabling automated discovery and governance of AI assets across enterprise environments
