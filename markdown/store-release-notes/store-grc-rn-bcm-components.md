# GRC: Business Continuity Management - Components release notes

Version history for the GRC: Business Continuity Management - Components on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.2 - March 2021**
    -   New:
        -   Added capability to support RPO impact assessment in Business impact Analysis
        -   Plan Overview captures the status of the exercises and crisis events
        -   Events authoring supports addition of assets and adhoc tasks
-   **Version 1.0.0 - October 2020**

    The Business Continuity Management - Components plugin provides the Seismic UX components required to deliver the business continuity workspace experience.
