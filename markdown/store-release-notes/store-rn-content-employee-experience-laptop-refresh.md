# Employee Experience Pack: Laptop Refresh Initiative release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Laptop Refresh Initiative content on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - November 2022**
    -   The Laptop Refresh Initiative consists of campaign and sample knowledge content, in addition to an HR service for accelerating time-to-value of this use case.
    -   Please note that version 2.1.0 of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the San Diego release only.
    -   Campaign content items:
        -   Actions \(9\)
        -   Notifications \(1\)
        -   Knowledge article \(1\)
-   **Version 2.0.0 - April 2022**
    -   The Laptop Refresh Initiative consists of campaign and sample knowledge content, in addition to an HR service for accelerating time-to-value of this use case. Please note that version 2.x of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the Rome release only.
    -   Campaign content items:
        -   Actions \(9\)
        -   Notifications \(1\)
        -   Knowledge article \(1\)
