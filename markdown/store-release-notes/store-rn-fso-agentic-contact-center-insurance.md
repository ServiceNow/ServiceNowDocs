# Agentic Contact Center for Insurance release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Agentic Contact Center for Insurance application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.0 - June 2026**

    The ServiceNow Agentic Contact Center for Insurance application provides an AI‑led experience for CSRs \(Customer Services Representatives\) that streamlines front‑office insurance operations and reduces the challenges CSRs face such as navigating multiple systems, improving overall efficiency.
