# ITOM - Prime release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® ITOM - Prime application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.6 - June 2026**

    See the Now Assist for Platform and AIOps Experience release notes.

-   **Version 1.0.4 - May 2026**

    New: Introducing the ITOM MCP Server, available through the MCP Server console. The ITOM MCP Server enables seamless integration with your ServiceNow instance, allowing you to connect to and manage alerts efficiently. This capability extends to leveraging any third-party AI tool such as Claude, ChatGPT, and others, to retrieve information, perform analysis, investigate, and access additional skills to streamline your workflow.

-   **Version 1.0.3 - April 2026**
    -   ITOM - Prime is the most comprehensive, AI-native IT Operations Management offering, designed to help enterprises move from reactive operations to autonomous, service-aware, and resilient IT. It combines full ITOM visibility, advanced observability, and end-to-end AIOps with built-in generative AI and agentic workflows to proactively prevent outages, accelerate remediation, and continuously improve service reliability.
    -   ITOM - Prime includes everything in ITOM - Advanced and adds full-spectrum AIOps and Service Observability, enabling organizations to automatically discover and map infrastructure and services, correlate events and metrics at scale, and apply AI-driven insights to reduce alert noise, pinpoint root causes, and assess business impact in real time.
    -   With Now Assist for ITOM, Predictive Intelligence, and multiple AI agents embedded across Discovery, Service Mapping, Event Management, and observability, ITOM - Prime empowers IT teams to operate with speed, precision, and confidence—shifting from manual firefighting to self-healing, outcome-driven operations.
