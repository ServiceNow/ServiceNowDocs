# Recruiter Workspace release notes

Version history for the Recruiter Workspace application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 8.0.0 - June 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Interview Health Tracker: A unified list view for Recruiters to monitor the health of all their interviews across job requisitions centrally enabling them to identify and address issues in a timely manner.
        -   Substitute interviewer: Recruiters can substitute an interviewer for any interview on a job where they're part of the hiring team.
-   **Version 7.1.0 - March 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Additional recruiters can now be added to a job requisition for better coordination &amp; collaboration on the hiring.
        -   Ability to track all feedback and its status related to job requisitions being managed.
-   **Version 7.0.0 - December 2025**
    -   Addressed few minor defects.
    -   Updates to support the interview management agentic workflow.
-   **Version 6.0.0 - August 2025**

    Recruiter workspace empowers the recruitment teams to manage, track &amp; complete all their hiring-related activities centrally. It allows hiring professionals to manage all hiring-related work, including job requisitions, applications, interviews &amp; feedback, talent pools conveniently &amp; efficiently.
