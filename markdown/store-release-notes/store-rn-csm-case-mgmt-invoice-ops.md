# Case Management for Invoice Operations release notes

Version history for the Case Management for Invoice Operations application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.0 - June 2026 \(Australia\)**

    Fixed: Minor security fixes

-   **Version 1.8.2 - June 2026 \(Zurich\)**

    Fixed: Security fixes

-   **Version 1.8.0 - May 2026**

    Fixed: Grants customer service agents read access to invoice cases.

-   **Version 1.7.0 - April 2026**

    This release includes file restructuring to align with platform guidelines.

-   **Version 1.6.0 - March 2026 \(Zurich\)**
    -   New:
        -   Retrieve invoice cases together with their associated line items for a more comprehensive view on the Business Portal.
        -   Created new ACLs that enable B2B customers to view invoice case and invoice case line details on the Business Portal.
        -   Introduced a custom invoice record details page in the CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace. This is a backend implementation and does not affect functionality.
-   **Version 1.5.0 - March 2026 \(Yokohama\)**
    -   New:
        -   Retrieve invoice cases together with their associated line items for a more comprehensive view on the Business Portal.
        -   Created new ACLs that enable B2B customers to view invoice case and invoice case line details on the Business Portal.
        -   Introduced a custom invoice record details page in the CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace. This is a backend implementation and does not affect functionality.
-   **Version 1.4.0 - August 2025 \(Zurich\)**

    Changed: Convert existing product models to customer visible.

-   **Version 1.3.0 - August 2025 \(Yokohama\)**

    Changed: Convert existing product models to customer visible.

-   **Version 1.2.0 - July 2025**

    Fixed: Addressed bug : Unknown field action\_status in table sn\_csm\_invoice\_case.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - May 2025**

    Fixed: Addressed bug : Unknown field action\_status in table sn\_csm\_invoice\_case.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - February 2025**

    The Case Management for invoice Operations application enables B2B customers to operationalize invoice-related services such as invoice disputes and collections. This application includes a dedicated invoice case type along with invoice case lines. This application also includes the common services that customers can request on an invoice, workflow, and agent experiences.
