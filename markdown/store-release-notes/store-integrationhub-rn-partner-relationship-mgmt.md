# Partner Relationship Management release notes

Version history for the Partner Relationship Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

        -   List refresh in partner overview, and so on

    -   Minor enhancements:

    -   Changed:

    -   Changed:

        -   Added Quote related list in partner overview

-   **Version 3.0.0 - December 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Configured Criteria to partner program and partner program relationship tables

-   **Version 2.1.0 - August 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Workspace access for enterprise users.
        -   Partner 360 view for enterprise users.
        -   Updates to the channel partner, program and partnership table fields.
        -   Map and configure the channel parter record entities to the company record to ensure consistency between the two records.
        -   Enterprise users can track sold products and install base.
    -   Fixed: Improved UI/UX for Partner Portal and Workspace.
-   **Version 2.0.0 - May 2025**
    -   Partner Relationship Management \(PRM\) transforms how enterprises engage and collaborate with their partner ecosystem. PRM will deliver a unified workspace for sales, delivery, marketing, and operations teams to manage the entire partner lifecycle.
    -   With PRM, organizations gain real-time visibility into partner performance, can personalize engagement based on partner programs and segment, and foster stronger, more strategic relationships.
    -   Partners will benefit from a simplified portal experience that brings together enablement, insights, and transaction workflows, allowing them to focus on growth and value creation for themselves and the enterprise.
