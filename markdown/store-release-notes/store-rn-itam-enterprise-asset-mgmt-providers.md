# Enterprise Asset Management for Providers release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Enterprise Asset Management for Providers application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - March 2026**

    Fixed: Translation supports multiple languages.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - December 2025**
    -   The Provider RMA capability within Enterprise Asset Management enables Device as a Service \(DaaS\) providers, vendors, and manufacturers to manage Return Merchandise Authorization \(RMA\) workflows and inbound asset orders from a single location. This feature streamlines provider-side asset life-cycle operations, reducing complexity and improving efficiency.
    -   Provider-focused workflowsAdds specialized flows for RMA response orders \(repair or replacement of defective assets\) and inbound asset orders \(fulfillment of customer asset requests\).
    -   New roles
        -   Provider Asset Manager – Oversees provider asset life-cycle and RMA processes.
        -   Provider Asset Technician – Executes operational tasks for RMA response orders and inbound asset orders.
    -   Architecture &amp; integration
        -   Built as a top-level application, similar to EAM for Healthcare, Facilities, and DCNAM.
        -   Compatible with Yokohama and later releases.
        -   Designed for future independent licensing, if required.
    -   Data model &amp; components
        -   New tables for enterprise RMA orders, extending ITAM common RMA structures.
        -   Record producer-based approach for flexible request handling.
    -   Business outcomes
        -   Consolidates provider operations for RMA and asset fulfillment.
        -   Reduces manual effort and accelerates turnaround for defective asset replacement.
        -   Improves visibility and compliance across provider workflows.
