# Opportunity Management for Channel Partners release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Opportunity Management for Channel Partners application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 2.1.0 - June 2026**
    -   Enhancements:
        -   Ability to create an opportunity from channel partner's overview page
        -   Updates opportunity analytics widgets

-   **Version 1.1.0 - June 2026 \(Zurich\)**
    -   Enhancements:
        -   Ability to create an opportunity from channel partner's overview page
        -   Updates opportunity analytics widgets
-   **Version 1.0.0 - March 2026**

    Empower your channel partners with seamless opportunity management capabilities. This app enables tracking of channel partners associated with an opportunity, provide channel partners to capture customer details and requirements, products and services of interest, and internal sales team interactions. While providing enterprises to maintain full visibility and control over their channel partners sales process.
