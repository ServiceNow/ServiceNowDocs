# Legal Hold Notification release notes

Version history for the Legal Hold Notification application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.1 - June 2026**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Security fixes
        -   Fixed an issue to improve the performance while issuing the Legal hold notice
-   **Version 1.1.0 - December 2025**
    -   Changed:
        -   Security enhancements
        -   Performance improvements
    -   Fixed:
        -   Resolved UI and content related issues.
        -   "Issue Legal hold" UI action has been removed for the native experience.
-   **Version 1.0.2 - August 2025**

    The Legal Hold Notification communicates to employees that a legal hold is being placed on data for which they are custodians and manages their acknowledgments. It generates legal hold notices, delivers notifications and reminders to custodians, and tracks acknowledgments within the legal counsel center.
