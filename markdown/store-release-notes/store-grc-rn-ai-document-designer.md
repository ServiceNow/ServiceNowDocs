# AI for Document designer release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® AI for Document designer application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 22.3.4 - June 2026 \(Australia\)**
    -   AI for Doc Designer brings the power of ServiceNow data directly into Microsoft Word through a simple conversational experience. No more manual data exports, copy-pasting, or formatting struggles — just ask, and your report is drafted for you.
    -   Designed for business users who need fast, accurate, and consistently formatted reports, AI for Doc Designer connects your ServiceNow data with the familiarity of Word. Whether it's incident summaries, compliance reports, sustainability reports or operational updates, simply describe what you need and let AI do the heavy lifting.
