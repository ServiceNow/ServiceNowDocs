-   **Version 1.0.0 - August 2020**

    This initial release delivers all applications within the Safe Workplace suite:

    -   Contact Tracing: Identify employees that may have been exposed to an individual classified as positive for a condition
    -   Workplace Core: Configure sanitized and socially distanced workspaces for the return of employees
    -   Workplace PPE Inventory Management: Manage and monitor workplace stock for the personal protective equipment provided to your workforce
    -   Safe Workplace Dashboard: View your workforce and workplace readiness for a safer employee ready working environment
    -   Employee Readiness Surveys: Send surveys to help gauge whether employees are ready to return to the workplace
    -   Employee Health Screening: Enable employers to screen employees and visitors before they enter the workplace
    -   Employee Readiness Core: Provide shared functionality and data structures for Employee Readiness apps such as Employee Readiness Surveys and Employee Health Screening
    -   COVID-19 Global Health Data Set: Leverage COVID-19 Data to inform key business decisions regarding employee health and safety
