# Now Assist in Conversational Spokes release notes

Version history for the Now Assist in Conversational Spokes on the ServiceNow Store.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.0 - November 2024**

    Perform business actions through a conversational interface such as Now Assist. This feature creates a personalized and interactive experience to automate your business workflows by unlocking the conversational capability from Integration Hub Spoke Actions that are conversational.
