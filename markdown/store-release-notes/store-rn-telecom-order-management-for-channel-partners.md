# Order Management for Channel Partners release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Order Management for Channel Partners application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 3.0.0 - March 2026 \(Australia\)**

    Changed: Navigation changes to Orders in partner portal

-   **Version 2.0.0 - March 2026**

    Changed: Navigation changes to Orders in portal

-   **Version 1.0.0 - December 2025**

    Empower your channel partners with seamless order management capabilities. This app enables resellers and distributors to efficiently track and manage orders through a partner portal, while enterprises maintain full visibility and control over the partner sales process.
