# Customer Data Models for B2B2C release notes

Version history for the Customer Service Management Customer Data Models for B2B2C on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - August 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Enhanced the Account Consumer \(sn\_acct\_consumer\_account\_consumer\) table security by adding Deny Unless ACLs for Delete and Report View operations.
        -   Removed relationship\_contributor and relationship\_agent roles from existing ACLs as part of the transition to the declarative responsibility framework.
-   **Version 2.0.0 - February 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Improved the Account Consumer \(sn\_acct\_consumer\_account\_consumer\) table security by adding deny ACLs.
        -   Implemented the declarative responsibility framework for account team member relationships for the relationship agent and contributor personas.
-   **Version 1.0.2 - September 2022**

    Fixes.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - July 2021**
    -   New: Customer Data Models for B2B2C \(business-to-business-to-consumer\) allows organizations across industries to easily configure muti-level customer relationships that enable them to trace case flows from business customers to their end-customers. With the B2B2C model, you can support business customers who, in turn, support the end consumer. This model can be used to support:
        -   Employees of a business customer
        -   End consumers of a business customer
