# GRC Mobile application release notes

Version history for the GRC Mobile application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 18.0.0 - February 2024**

    Fixed: PRB1683603 Design improvements.

-   **Version 17.0.1 - August 2023**
    -   New
        -   Support for next experience theming
-   **Version 16.0.0 - February 2023**

    Fixed:Reduced the disk space size of the app

-   **Version 11.0.0 - October 2020**

    Fixed: Questionable language cleanup for com.sn\_grc\_mobile

-   **Version 8.0.11 - July 2019**
    -   The GRC mobile app has two modules: Policy and Compliance and Risk.
    -   With the Policy and Compliance module on the GRC mobile app, you can perform the following functions:
        -   Approve or reject pending policies and policy exception approvals.
        -   View your team's overdue and unassigned work, such as remediation tasks, attestations, and indicator tasks.
        -   View items that are assigned to you, such as attestations, controls, policies, issues, and remediation tasks.
    -   With the Risk module on the GRC mobile app, you can perform the following functions:
        -   Approve or reject pending risks and risk acceptance approvals.
        -   View your team's overdue and unassigned work, such as issues, remediation tasks, assessments, and indicator tasks.
        -   View items that are assigned to you, such as assessments, risks, issues, and remediation tasks.
