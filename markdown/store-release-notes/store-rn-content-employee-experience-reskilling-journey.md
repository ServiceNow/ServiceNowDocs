# Employee Experience Pack: Reskilling Journey release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Reskilling Journey on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.0 - May 2022**
    -   New: This update set contains an Insights guide and Journey blueprint for reskilling an employee or department.
    -   The update set contains the below items. Included in the insights guide are additional artifact examples and task recommendations.
    -   Lifecycle Event:
        -   5 Activity Sets
        -   38 Activities
        -   6 Checklists
        -   3 Notifications
        -   7 URLs
        -   10 Surveys
            -   Reskilling - Old Manager Transfer Survey
            -   Reskilling - New Manager Transfer Survey
            -   Reskilling - Employee Transfer Survey
            -   30 Day Ramp Up Survey - New Manager
            -   30 Day Ramp Up Survey - Employee
            -   30 Day Ramp Up Survey - Old Manager
            -   60 Day Ramp Up Survey - Employee
            -   60 Day Ramp Up Survey - Manager
            -   90 Day Ramp Up Survey - Employee
            -   90 Day Ramp Up Survey - Manager
        -   2 Learning Tasks
        -   7 Mark when complete
        -   5 Knowledge Articles
        -   New Manager Frequently Asked Question \(FAQ\) for Reskilling \(Transfer Phase\)
        -   Old Manager Frequently Asked Question \(FAQ\) for Reskilling \(Transfer Phase\)
        -   Manager Frequently Asked Question \(FAQ\) for Reskilling \(Transfer Phase\)
        -   HR Frequently Asked Questions \(FAQ\) for Reskilling \(Transfer Phase\)
        -   Employee Frequently Asked Question \(FAQ\) for Reskilling \(Transfer Phase\)
    -   Please note that version 2.x of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the Rome releases only.
-   **Version 1.0.0 - May 2021**
