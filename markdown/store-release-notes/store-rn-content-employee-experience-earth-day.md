# Employee Experience Pack: Earth Day &amp; Sustainability Campaign release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Earth Day &amp; Sustainability Campaign on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - September 2022**
    -   Please note version 2.1.x of this Experience Pack is San Diego release compatible only at this time. Previous versions may be available for prior releases. To use the Pulse Survey content in the campaign, Listening Posts also requires activation and be installed via the ServiceNow Store. These update sets contain a campaign and employee-facing knowledge content modeled after common implementation processes. Included content:
        -   -   1 Campaign
-   2 Campaign Bundles
-   2 Styled Content Banners
-   2 Banners
-   1 Event
-   1 Mobile Banner
-   2 Knowledge Articles
-   1 Pulse Survey \(Listening Post\)
-   **Version 1.0.0 - April 2021**
    -   New: Please note version 1.0 of this Experience Pack is Paris release compatible only at this time. To use the Pulse Survey content in the campaign, Listening Posts also need to be activated and installed via the ServiceNow Store. These update sets contain a campaign and employee-facing knowledge content modeled after common implementation processes. Included content:
        -   1 Campaign
        -   2 Campaign Bundles
        -   2 Styled Content Banners
        -   2 Banners
        -   1 Event
        -   1 Mobile Banner
        -   2 Knowledge Articles
        -   1 Pulse Survey \(Listening Post\)
