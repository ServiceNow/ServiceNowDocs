# Talent Development Core release notes

Version history for the Talent Development Core on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 5.5.0 - June 2026**

    Minor release. Green light code changes but no major functionality

-   **Version 5.4.1 - March 2026**
    -   Changed: Enhanced GlideRecordSecure security.
    -   Fixed: Few bugs.
-   **Version 5.3.0 - December 2025**

    Changed: Granular admin directive changes.

-   **Version 5.2.1 - August 2025**

    Minor licensing changes.

-   **Version 5.1.4 - June 2025**

    Minor bug fixes.

-   **Version 5.1.3 - May 2025**
    -   Minor performance updates
    -   Theming changes
-   **Version 5.0.3 - February 2025**

    New Feature: Growth Plan Generation - This feature allows employees to input their goals and interests in text form and then generate a personalized growth plan for them to get started on their growth journey.

-   **Version 4.3.2 - November 2024**
    -   New:
        -   Growth Plan Activity Insights for Skill Validation
        -   Aspirations Activity Insights for Skill Validation
    -   Updated:
        -   Security improvements for aspirations
        -   Security improvements for growth plans
        -   Demo Data for aspirations and growth plans
    -   Fixed:
        -   UI issues in Skill approvals flyout
        -   UI issues in edit link for aspirations
        -   RCA issues with Career Hub, Aspirations
        -   Tabbing issue in Growth Plans
-   **Version 4.2.2 - August 2024**

    Fixed: Various accessibility and usability defects.

-   **Version 4.1.9 - July 2024**

    Application renamed from EGD Core to Talent Development Core.

-   **Version 4.1.7 - May 2024**
    -   New:
        -   Growth plans
            -   Employee goals can be used to create a growth plan
    -   Fixed:
        -   Aspirations, Career Hub, and Growth plans
            -   Various accessibility and internationalization issues
            -   Various issues to improve user experience
-   **Version 4.0.4 - February 2024**
    -   New Growth Plans: Support for Mentoring Employees can enroll in mentoring and find mentors as a part of their growth plans
    -   Growth Plans: Support for Enhanced Conversations
        -   If Outlook Integration is supported in Conversations, employees can leverage that capability when scheduling within growth plans
    -   Career Hub: Support for Opportunity Marketplace Employees can view top-matching internal job opportunities and their recent job application status
    -   Aspirations: Featured Aspirations by Company Employees can view up to 9 role or skill aspirations promoted by their organization from a new widget
    -   Featured by your company:
        -   Changed Growth Plans: To support enhanced Conversation scheduling, date is no longer a required field when creating a growth plan; it can be added later.
        -   Career Hub: Enabled auto-updating completed steps and retrieving skipped steps in Get started widget
-   **Version 3.0.5 - January 2024**

    Fixed: Misconfiguration of ACLs for EGD Core.

-   **Version 3.0.4 - November 2023**
    -   New:
        -   Growth Plans:
            -   Users can now resequence activities in their growth plans
            -   Admins can configure whether and how to automatically start activities
            -   Various usability and accessibility improvements
    -   Fixed:
        -   Aspirations:
            -   Users can now add links to Aspiration descriptions using the HTML editor button
            -   Only a single confirmation is now displayed after completing an aspiration
            -   Various accessibility fixes
        -   Career Hub:
            -   Skills are now displayed without reloading after uploading a resume and importing skills to your profile
            -   Various accessibility and visual experience fixes
        -   Growth Plans:
            -   Users can now open Growth Plans in a new tab from the Growth Plan home page
            -   Auto-completed learning tasks are displayed as complete in the Growth Plan activity flyout
            -   Demo data fixes
            -   EGD Admins can now access the EGD Core properties page
            -   Activity templates can be deleted
            -   Various responsive, accessibility, and visual experience fixes
    -   Removed:
        -   Growth Plans:
            -   The following activity categories are no longer available for Growth Plan templates
                -   Validation
                -   Development
                -   Other
-   **Version 2.0.0 - September 2023**
    -   The ServiceNow Employee Growth and Development core application supports an end-to-end process for employees and managers to identify, track, and take control of their career growth and development journey. Embedded within Employee Center, employees have a centralized and unified place to design their personalized roadmap for career growth in an intuitive and comprehensive application.
    -   Key highlights:
        -   Helps capture an employee's aspirations and turn them into actionable growth plans.
        -   Aids in creation, update and tracking of career growth and development plans.
        -   A personalized overview for an employee that captures key information of all their activities under career growth and development in the form of Career Hub.
        -   Built on skills intelligence engine to identify areas to help identify gaps and provide recommendations to advance and grow careers.
        -   Provide proactive prompts to keep growth and development at the forefront of employees thoughts.
-   **Version 1.0.6 - August 2023**
    -   New: Initial release of Employee Growth &amp; Development functionality.
    -   Changed: A new navigation link to Your career will be added to the Mega Menu in Employee Center.
