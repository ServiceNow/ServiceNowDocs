# Alert Assist release notes

Version history for the ITOM Alert Assist application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.0 - March 2024**

    Alert Assist is a cutting-edge capability within ServiceNow that harnesses the power of generative AI technology to revolutionize the way alerts are managed. By seamlessly integrating with existing systems, Alert Assist automatically generates concise alert descriptions and supplements them with comprehensive analysis. This analysis includes a detailed technical assessment of the issue, providing invaluable insights to aid in swift and effective resolution. By streamlining the alert management process, Alert Assist empowers teams to respond proactively to alerts, minimizing downtime and optimizing operational efficiency. With its intuitive interface and advanced AI algorithms, Alert Assist sets a new standard for alert management, enabling organizations to stay ahead in today's dynamic and fast-paced digital landscape.
