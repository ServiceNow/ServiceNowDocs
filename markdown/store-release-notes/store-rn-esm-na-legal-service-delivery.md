# Now Assist for Legal Service Delivery \(LSD\) release notes

Version history for the Now Assist for Legal Service Delivery \(LSD\) application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.8.1 - June 2026**
    -   Changed: Support for upgraded versions of third party LLMs has been provided.
    -   Fixed: Legal request and matter summarization now delivers improved accuracy across all supported models
-   **Version 1.7.7 - May 2026**
    -   Changed:
        -   Support for upgraded versions of third party LLMs has been provided.
        -   Enhanced UI for visual clarity in AI-assisted features.
    -   Fixed: The Now Assist summary card header is now displayed consistently across Legal Requests and Legal Matters while switching the records.
-   **Version 1.6.5 - April 2026**

    Refer to Legal Service Delivery-Prime \(sn\_lg\_ai\_prime\) application release notes.

-   **Version 1.6.2 - March 2026**
    -   New: Conversational intake form for submitting a Conflict of Interest request.
    -   Changed: Legal Request and Matter summarization now considers data from extended practice area tables for summarization, ensuring that summaries reflect all relevant information.
-   **Version 1.5.6 - January 2026**

    Fixed: Addressed a technical warning in Now Assist for Legal Service Delivery to improve code stability and development.

-   **Version 1.5.2 - December 2025**
    -   New:
        -   The following capabilities will be supported for the Long Term Stability LLM model-
            -   Legal Request case summarization
            -   Legal Matter case summarization
            -   Triage Legal request Agentic AI capabilities
    -   Changed:
        -   Triage Legal Request agentic workflow has been updated with the user friendly messages in the Now Assist panel for better readability.
        -   Security enhancements
    -   Fixed: Resolved Japanese language related error in Legal request and matter summarization.
-   **Version 1.4.7 - October 2025 \(Zurich\)**

    Fixed: Addressed performance-related issues in Triage legal request agentic workflow.

-   **Version 1.2.5 - October 2025 \(Xanadu\)**

    New: Create ACLs for AI agents and agentic workflows to customize who can discover and trigger AI agents and agentic workflows.

-   **Version 1.4.3 - September 2025**

    New: Create ACLs for AI agents and agentic workflows to customize who can discover and trigger AI agents and agentic workflows.

-   **Version 1.3.0 - August 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Triage legal request agentic workflow adds support for the following third party LLMs: Azure OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini
        -   Legal request summarization and legal matter summarization skills add support for the following third party LLMs: Azure OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini
-   **Version 1.2.2 - June 2025**
    -   New: Agentic workflow to analyze General Legal Requests, predict the appropriate legal category, and initiate a transfer after confirmation from the legal fulfiller or group manager.
    -   Changed: For legal request and matter summarization, you can now configure record producer variables to be considered for summarization.
    -   Fixed: Security issues.
-   **Version 1.2.0 - May 2025**
    -   New: Agentic workflow to analyze General Legal Requests, predict the appropriate legal category, and initiate a transfer after confirmation from the legal fulfiller or group manager.
    -   Changed: For legal request and matter summarization, you can now configure record producer variables to be considered for summarization.
    -   Fixed: Security issues.
-   **Version 1.1.0 - February 2025**

    New: Now Assist Q and A Genius Results that generate actionable answers from Knowledge article results in Employee Center, Legal Counsel Center and global search.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - December 2024**

    Changed: UI improvements.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - November 2024**

    ServiceNow Now Assist for Legal Service Delivery \(LSD\) enables generative AI capabilities to streamline legal work by summarising legal requests and matters to boost legal team's productivity and deliver faster resolution for employees.
