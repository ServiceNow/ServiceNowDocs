# Sales CRM - Prime release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Sales CRM - Prime application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - April 2026**

    Sales CRM - Prime is the highest-tier AI subscription for sales and order management on the ServiceNow AI Platform. Extending Sales CRM - Advanced, this offering unlocks Platform Prime features for enterprises that require the most advanced AI capabilities across their sales and order workflows. Sales CRM - Prime includes everything in Sales CRM - Advanced and adds access to Now Assist Platform Prime, enabling organizations to take full advantage of the latest platform-level AI innovations as they become available.
