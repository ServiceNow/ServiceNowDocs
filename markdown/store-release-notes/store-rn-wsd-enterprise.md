    -   ServiceNow Workplace Service Delivery Suite provides a suite of applications to help optimize the workspace and deliver digital experiences for employees to interact with the services offered by their work place. The solution supports Workplace and Facilities leads with full workplace management and optimization.
    -   The suite includes the following applications:
    -   -   Workplace Case Management
-   Workplace Reservation Management
-   Workplace Indoor Mapping
-   Workplace Space Mapping
-   Workplace Visitor Management
-   Workplace Space Management
-   Workplace Move Management
-   Workplace Maintenance Management
-   Workplace Calendar Synchronization
-   Workplace Reservations for Microsoft Outlook Add-in
-   Workplace Central
-   Workplace Concierge
-   Workplace Connectors
-   Workplace Core
    -   The Workplace Service Delivery Enterprise application allows customers to easily download all licensed Workplace Service Deliver y applications at once, eliminating the need to install them individually.
    -   To automatically install all the Workplace Service Delivery applications at once, download the ServiceNow Workplace Service Delivery Enterprise application.
