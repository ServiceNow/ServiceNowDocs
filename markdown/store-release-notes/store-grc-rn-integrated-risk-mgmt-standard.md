# Integrated Risk Management Standard release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Integrated Risk Management Standard application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 22.1.1 - April 2026**

    IRM Standard is the foundation of your Integrated Risk Management program on ServiceNow. Install this plugin to access the core capabilities your teams need to manage risk, track issues, and stay audit-ready.
