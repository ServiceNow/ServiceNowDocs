# Quote Management for Business Locations release notes

Version history for the Quote Management for Business Locations application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.1 - June 2026**

    Changed: Updated the dependent quote management app's version.

-   **Version 2.2.0 - June 2026**

    Fixed: Minor defect fixes

-   **Version 2.1.0 - April 2026**

    Changed: Renamed Service Model Foundation entities to improve clarity and maintain consistency across the platform.Please refer to ServiceNow Documentation for the label changes.Eg: "Service Organization" is renamed to "Organization Core", "Service Organization Criteria" is renamed to "Organization Criteria".

-   **Version 2.0.0 - March 2026**
    -   Changed: The following dependencies for the Quote Management for Business Locations Store app are upgraded:
        -   sn\_bus\_loc: 5.2.0
        -   sn\_quote\_mgmt: 10.0.0
-   **Version 1.2.0 - December 2025**

    New: New Quote Relationship Manager persona : Quote relationship manager for channel partners having abilities to track and manage channel partner activities for assigned locations.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - August 2025**

    Integrate  Service Model Foundation  with Quote Management for business organizations to streamline operations and optimize the end to end life cycle for quote management.
