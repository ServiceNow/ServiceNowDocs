# Manufacturing Recall Claim Management release notes

Version history for the Manufacturing Recall Claim Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.5.0 - June 2026**

    No release notes.

-   **Version 2.4.1 - June 2026 \(Zurich\)**

    No release notes.

-   **Version 2.3.1 - April 2026**

    No release notes.

-   **Version 2.2.1 - April 2026 \(Zurich\)**

    No release notes.

-   **Version 2.1.0 - March 2026**

    New: Parts availability data model

-   **Version 2.0.0 - March 2026 \(Zurich\)**

    New: Parts availability data model

-   **Version 1.1.0 - August 2025**
    -   New: Dealers will have the ability to create a claim and include corrective actions from the recall campaigns and submit to OEM. OEM can review and approve or reject the claim.
    -   https://docs-preview.corp.service-now.com/bundle/zurich-release-notes/page/release-notes/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations-rn.html
-   **Version 1.0.0 - August 2025**
    -   TheServiceNow Manufacturing Commercial Operations \(MCO\)product empowers you to optimize sales, support, and service operations enhancing operational efficiency, reducing costs, and boosting productivity.
    -   TheManufacturing Recall Claim Management application from ServiceNow helps customers to:
        -   Manage Recall Campaigns:
            -   Streamline collaboration with dealers to efficiently resolve claims related to product recalls. Dealers can submit repair claims for work performed as part of recall campaigns, while OEM assessors benefit from a playbook-guided experience that simplifies and standardizes claim resolution.
