# Employee Experience Pack: Tax Time Campaign release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Tax Time Campaign on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - November 2022**
    -   The Tax Time Campaign consists of three bundles, with four portal banners, two email notifications, and one knowledge article. Four re-usable images are also included.
    -   Please note that version 2.1.0 of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the San Diego release only.
    -   Included content:
        -   1 Tax Time Campaign consisting of three Bundles:
            -   Prepare for Tax Season
            -   W-2s Are Available
            -   Tax Withholdings Reminder
        -   6 Campaign Content Items:
            -   Get Ready for Tax Time Banner\*
            -   Your W-2, Faster Banner\*
            -   Prepare for Tax Season Email
            -   W-2s Are Here Banner\*
            -   W-2s Are Available Email
            -   Taxes of the Future Banner\*
        -   1 Knowledge Article:
            -   How to Update Your Tax Withholdings
-   **Version 1.0.0 - November 2020**
    -   New:
        -   The Tax Time Campaign consists of three bundles, with four portal banners, two email notifications, and one knowledge article. Four re-usable images are also included.
        -   Included content:
            -   1 Tax Time Campaign consisting of three Bundles:
                -   Prepare for Tax Season
                -   W-2s Are Available
                -   Tax Withholdings Reminder
            -   6 Campaign Content Items:
                -   Get Ready for Tax Time Banner\*
                -   Your W-2, Faster Banner\*
                -   Prepare for Tax Season Email
                -   W-2s Are Here Banner\* W-2s Are Available Email Taxes of the Future Banner\*
            -   1 Knowledge Article:
                -   How to Update Your Tax Withholdings
