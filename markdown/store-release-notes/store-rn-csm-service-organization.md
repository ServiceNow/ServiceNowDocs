# Service Organization release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Service Organization application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 2.7.0 - June 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Changes to support Business Org Self Contributor persona
        -   Changes to support Broad Access Responsibility for Contributor persona
    -   Fixed: Minor fixes
-   **Version 2.4.1 - April 2026**

    Changed: Renamed Service Model Foundation entities to improve clarity and maintain consistency across the platform. Please refer to ServiceNow Documentation for the label changes. Eg: "Service Organization" is renamed to "Organization Core", "Service Organization Criteria" is renamed to "Organization Criteria".

-   **Version 2.3.1 - March 2026**
    -   The Service Organization application enables organizations to define and manage service entities — including internal teams, external partners, and business locations — that deliver customer service in a structured way.
    -   It integrates with the Customer Service Management data model to represent business units, service centers, and outsourced partners as service organizations or locations.
    -   With this capability, enterprises can model their service delivery footprint, link service teams to accounts and sold products, assign staff members, and provide appropriate access in CSM workflows. This foundation supports role-based access and location-aware case routing and fulfillment.
