# CRM Outlook Add-in release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® CRM Outlook Add-in application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.1.1 - June 2026**
    -   Changed:
        -   Expanded email association capabilities, improved usability, and accessibility conformance.
        -   Support for associating sent emails with CRM entities, automatic email thread association, and smarter preset filters.
-   **Version 1.0.1 - May 2026**

    Fixed: Emails were not being promoted from the staging email table to the emails table due to a duplicate UID check failure caused by UID truncation. Emails now promote successfully.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - March 2026**

    The CRM Outlook Add-in enables sales agents to capture emails efficiently into ServiceNow CRM without leaving Microsoft Outlook.
