# Contract Management for Sales Customer Relationship Management release notes

Version history for the Contract Management for Sales Customer Relationship Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.2 - June 2026**

    Change: Updated the plugin name to Contract Management for Sales CRM

-   **Version 1.1.1 - April 2026**

    Fixed: Minor fixes to demo data and a preview document loading issue

-   **Version 1.1.0 - December 2025**

    Fixed a translation issue.

-   **Version 1.0.4 - February 2025**

    Fixed the OOB demo data.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - August 2024**

    This Contract Management for Sales and Order Management application will enable customers to create sales legal agreement or other type of documents from ServiceNow Sales and Order Management. This integrated feature with Contract Management Pro streamlines the process of finalizing deals and formalizing agreements between a company and its customers.
