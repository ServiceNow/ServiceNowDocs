# Guided Self-Service in Employee Center release notes

Version history for the Guided Self-Service in Employee Center application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.3.0 - June 2026**

    Updated to support the latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 3.2.2 - December 2025**

    Updated to support the latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 3.1.10 - August 2025 \(Zurich\)**

    Updated to support the latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 3.1.3 - August 2025 \(Yokohama\)**

    Updated to support the latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 3.0.2 - February 2025**

    Employees can now find Guided Self-Service links in AI Search-powered search results.

-   **Version 2.0.2 - November 2024**
    -   Added the following improvements:
        -   Users can review and edit their previous responses, providing a more streamlined experience.
        -   Admins can add rich media, including images and videos, as well as formatted text, providing employees detailed answers or guidance as the Q&amp;A result.
-   **Version 1.0.6 - August 2024**

    Provides employees with a visual, Q&amp;A-based experience for self-serving on highly used and complex topics such as time off balance. It offers a more intuitive and engaging way for employees to quickly find the information they need. Suitable for organizations of all sizes and across various industries.
