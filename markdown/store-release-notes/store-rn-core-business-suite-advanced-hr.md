# Core Business Suite Advanced for Human Resources release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Core Business Suite Advanced for Human Resources application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.0.5 - June 2026**

    Core Business Suite Advanced for Human Resources is now available for direct installation from the Product Hub. This enables one-click installation of all CBS Advanced HR applications.

-   **Version 3.0.1 - April 2026**

    Core Business Suite Advanced for Human Resources extends the capabilities of the Foundation version to support more complex enterprise workflows. It provides advanced AI capabilities for the Human Resources department.
