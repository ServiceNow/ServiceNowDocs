# Technician driven sales with Field Service release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Technician driven sales with Field Service application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 29.1.2 - March 2026**
    -   Technician-Driven Sales lets field service technicians create and track sales opportunities directly from their mobile app while on-site, capturing upsell, cross-sell, and replacement opportunities at the moment they're identified, not hours later in a post-visit report.
    -   Technicians can log opportunities based on what they observe and hear during a service call. Those opportunities are immediately visible to sales teams for follow-up, replacing manual handoffs and the revenue that typically falls through the gap between field and office.
    -   Built into the mobile experience technicians already use, opportunity creation adds no separate workflow or tool. Just a direct path from on-site observation to tracked pipeline.
