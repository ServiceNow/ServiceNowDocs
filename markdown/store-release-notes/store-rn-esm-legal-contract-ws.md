# Contract Workspace release notes

Version history for the Contract Workspace application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.9.0 - June 2026**

    New: Contract requests now support offline signatures, enabling users to manage contracts signed outside the system.

-   **Version 1.8.5 - May 2026**

    Fixed: Improved performance when displaying expired contracts list.

-   **Version 1.7.7 - April 2026 \(Yokohama\)**

    Fixed: The New button on the contract list view now appears correctly in Software Asset Management and Hardware Asset Management workspaces when the Contract Management Pro application is installed.

-   **Version 1.8.1 - March 2026**

    New: Document comparison now supports contract documents stored in external storage.

-   **Version 1.7.2 - December 2025**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Resolved performance issues on the Contract workspace home page.
        -   Resolved conditional visibility of "Related contract requests" related list in a contract request.
-   **Version 1.6.0 - September 2025**
    -   New:
        -   A new Playbook related list is now available in Contract Workspace when the NowAssist in Contract Management plugin is installed. The Playbook allows users to review metadata extracted by AI agents within the Manage Contract Repository agentic workflow. Users can edit the extracted information as needed and submit updates to the contract repository. If the contract end date is available, the Review contract reminders tab appears in the playbook, enabling you to review and update the AI-calculated contract reminder date and specify recipients for contract renewal or terminations.
        -   The Executed contracts view on the Contracts Workspace has been improved to display additional fields and related lists \(e.g. Obligations, Playbook, Executed contracts etc.\).
-   **Version 1.5.0 - August 2025**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Accessibility issues are fixed.
        -   The message to use Now Assist contract analysis is now visible only to appropriate roles like contract fulfiller working on request and not visible to contract users who are on the watchlist.
        -   Widgets, welcome banner image, headers, and labels are optimized to work with the Dark theme in contract workspace.
-   **Version 1.4.0 - May 2025**
    -   New: For self-served contract request in mixed signature workflow, the contract request will be displayed under unassigned contract request list when a requestor submits a change request for requests in Awaiting signature state.
    -   Fixed: UI issues
-   **Version 1.3.0 - February 2025**
    -   Changed: In Contract workspace and Legal Counsel Center, the contract documents in the Contract documents tab are grouped based on contract types, by default.
    -   Fixed: Performance improvements.
-   **Version 1.2.1 - November 2024**

    Fixed: The Send Document button does not appear in Contracts Workspace if Legal Counsel Center app is not installed. This has been fixed.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - August 2024**

    Fixed: Related lists displayed for a legal request in Legal Counsel Center and Contract Workspace are inconsistent.

-   **Version 1.0.3 - May 2024**

    ServiceNow Contract Workspace is included with Contract Management Pro. Contract Workspace helps fulfillers and stakeholders to stay on top of all contract request management and processing related activities within their organization. It enables them to prioritize and efficiently address contract requests from a single interface.
