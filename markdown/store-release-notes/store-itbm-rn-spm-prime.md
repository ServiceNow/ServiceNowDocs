# Strategic Portfolio Management - Prime release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Strategic Portfolio Management - Prime application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.4 - April 2026**
    -   Achieve true strategy-to-execution alignment with SPM Prime. Go beyond project tracking, connecting strategic investments to business outcomes, optimizing funding decisions in real time, and deploying AI agents that work across your enterprise ecosystem. With the ability to build custom agents and skills, your organization gains the intelligence and agility to continuously adapt plans to changing business priorities.
    -   SPM Prime is the most comprehensive AI-native strategic portfolio management. Built on top of SPM Advanced, it adds Strategic Planning and Investment Funding capabilities, along with advanced Platform AI features including new agent/skill creation. SPM Prime is Ideal for enterprises that need end-to-end strategy-to-execution alignment.
