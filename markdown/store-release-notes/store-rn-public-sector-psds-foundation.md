# PSDS - Foundation release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® PSDS - Foundation application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - April 2026**
    -   PSDS Foundation enables baseline AI capabilities to be available within Public Sector Digital Services in alignment with ServiceNow’s product tier packaging approach.
    -   This application supports entitlement‑based access to standard, out‑of‑the‑box AI features provided by PSDS and the ServiceNow platform. Availability of specific AI capabilities depends on the customer’s licensed tier and applicable entitlements.
    -   For information about licensing tiers and availability, refer to the PSDS Australia Release Notes.
