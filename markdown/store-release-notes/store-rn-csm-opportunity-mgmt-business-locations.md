# Opportunity Management for Business Locations release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Opportunity Management for Business Locations application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 2.2.0 - June 2026**

    No release notes.

-   **Version 2.1.0 - April 2026**

    Changed: Renamed Service Model Foundation entities to improve clarity and maintain consistency across the platform.Please refer to ServiceNow Documentation for the label changes.Eg: "Service Organization" is renamed to "Organization Core", "Service Organization Criteria" is renamed to "Organization Criteria".

-   **Version 2.0.0 - March 2026**

    Changed: Replaced the admin role with the opportunity administrator role \(sn\_opty\_mgmt\_core.opportunity\_admin\) in applicable scripts.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - December 2025**

    Opportunity management for business location enables your channel partners to create and track opportunities across the lifecycle of the customer journey. On an opportunity channel partners can capture customer related details, customer requirements, products and services the customer is interested in, and interactions within the sales team.
