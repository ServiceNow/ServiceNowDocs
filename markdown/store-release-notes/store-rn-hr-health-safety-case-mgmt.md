# Health and Safety Case Management release notes

Version history for the Health and Safety Case Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 6.2.0 - June 2026**

    New: Added Portal \(Employee Hub\) notifications for Health and Safety, enabling case-related notifications to be delivered through the Employee Hub portal

-   **Version 6.1.0 - May 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Automated L1 case approval agent
        -   Configurations for enhanced "My Request" experience for Health and Safety
-   **Version 6.0.1 - March 2026**
    -   Changed: Migrated Case Management files to the updated APP-CBS-BU infrastructure, improving module structure and maintainability.
    -   Fixed: Fixed issue with the case creation portal notification sent to the requestor not being translated, ensuring localized notifications are now correctly delivered.
-   **Version 5.0.0 - December 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Enabled access and management of universal requests for Health and Safety cases.
        -   Enhanced notification content for Health and Safety updates.
        -   Enabled notifications in both the portal and agent workspaces.
        -   Enabled email notifications for Health and Safety cases.
        -   Added bulk management options for case records.
    -   Fixed:
        -   Improved support for encoded queries in Health and Safety Case Management.
        -   Fixed cross-scope privilege issues in Health and Safety Case Management.
        -   Fixed errors related to the Document tab queries in case records.
        -   Updated email templates for Health and Safety communications.
        -   Improved translations for Health and Safety email notifications.
-   **Version 4.0.0 - August 2025**

    Changed: Minor ACL updates.

-   **Version 3.0.0 - May 2025**

    Fixed: Issue where case types were visible on Now Mobile when not installed.

-   **Version 2.0.1 - February 2025**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Case tab is not shown under H&amp;S user profile when only Health and Safety Incident Management and Health and Safety Case Management are installed
        -   Issues with case assignment allowing any user
        -   Issues with hints not appearing in some forms in certain scenarios
-   **Version 2.0.0 - November 2024**
    -   New:
        -   Worker compensation claims case type added
        -   Claims case form includes the following:
            -   Employee claim details
            -   Closure details
            -   Actions
            -   Documents list where documents from the Standards and Documents list can be added
-   **Version 1.0.0 - August 2024**

    ServiceNow Health and Safety Case Management provides your employees with an easy way to submit health and safety questions to your safety teams. This makes it easy for safety teams to track any questions and requests separately from incidents and observations. Employees can submit their questions through the Quick Links in the Employee Center. With the Return to Work case type, RTW coordinators can manage Return-to-work cases to organize employees' return to the workplace after an injury or illness.
