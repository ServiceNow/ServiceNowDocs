# Employee Experience Pack: Offboarding - Involuntary Separation release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Offboarding - Involuntary Separation on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - November 2022**
    -   This update set contains an Offboarding Lifecycle Event for Involuntary Separations.
    -   Please note that version 2.1.0 of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the San Diego releases only.
    -   Lifecycle Event Consisting of 4 Activity Sets:
        -   Initiate Separation
        -   Coordinate Separation Activities
        -   Finalize Separation Activities
        -   Complete Separation
    -   23 Activities:
        -   2 Catalog Items
        -   4 Checklists
        -   5 HR Services
        -   4 Mark When Complete
        -   1 Approval
        -   2 URL Tasks
        -   1 Documents Signature Task
        -   4 Notification Tasks
    -   4 New Knowledge Article Templates:
        -   Involuntary Separation FAQs - Employees
        -   Involuntary Separation FAQs - Managers
        -   Involuntary Separation FAQs - HR
        -   Involuntary Separation Termination Policy Template
    -   4 Checklists:
        -   Manager Separation Checklist
        -   HR Separation Checklist
        -   IT Separation Checklist
        -   Facilities Separation Checklist
-   **Version 1.0.0 - January 2021**
    -   This update set contains an Offboarding Lifecycle Event for Involuntary Separations.
    -   Please note that version 1.x of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the Orlando and Paris releases only.
    -   Lifecycle Event Consisting of 4 Activity Sets
        -   Initiate Separation
        -   Coordinate Separation Activities
        -   Finalize Separation Activities
        -   Complete Separation
    -   23 Activities
        -   2 Catalog Items
        -   4 Checklists
        -   5 HR Services
        -   4 Mark When Complete
        -   1 Approval
        -   2 URL Tasks
        -   1 Documents Signature Task
        -   4 Notification Tasks
    -   4 New Knowledge Article Templates
        -   Involuntary Separation FAQs - Employees
        -   Involuntary Separation FAQs - Managers
        -   Involuntary Separation FAQs - HR
        -   Involuntary Separation Termination Policy Template
    -   4 Checklists
        -   Manager Separation Checklist
        -   HR Separation Checklist
        -   IT Separation Checklist
        -   Facilities Separation Checklist
