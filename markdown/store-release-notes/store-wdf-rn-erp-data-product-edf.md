# ERP Data Product for Enterprise Data Foundation release notes

Version history for the ERP Data Product for Enterprise Data Foundation application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 4.0.1 - May 2026**

    Additional models, including Customer, Vendor, Vendor Bank Details, Vendor Location Details, and Vendor Contact Details, have been added to the content pack for use when interacting with an SAP system.

-   **Version 3.0.1 - December 2025**

    Additional Enterprise Data Foundation models, including Business Partner, Cost Center, and Vendor, have been added to the data product for use when interacting with an SAP system.

-   **Version 2.0.2 - August 2025**
    -   The ERP Data Product for Enterprise Data Foundation includes the following models for reading organizational data:
        -   Sales Areas
        -   Plants
        -   Valuation Areas
        -   Plant Locations
        -   Company Codes
        -   Languages
        -   Payment Terms
        -   Currencies
        -   Controlling Areas
        -   Countries
        -   Purchasing Organizations
        -   Purchasing Groups
        -   Business Areas
        -   Business Partner
        -   Cost Center
        -   Delivery / Billing Blocks
        -   General Ledger Accounts
        -   Profit Center
        -   Bank Data
