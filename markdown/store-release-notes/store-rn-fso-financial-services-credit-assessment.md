# Financial Services Credit Operations release notes

Version history for the Financial Services Operations Financial Services Credit Operations on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.1 - March 2021**

    Changed: Moved plugin dependencies to Financial Services Operations Core

-   **Version 1.1.0 - December 2020**
    -   Changed:
        -   Renamed the application name as Financial Services Credit Operations to include additional functionalities related to loan operations
        -   Introduced Credit Cases for handling cases related to loan operations
-   **Version 1.0.4 - September 2020**

    Provides capabilities to manage middle and back office tasks that are assigned to the Credit Risk Assessment team across any Financial Services workflow.
