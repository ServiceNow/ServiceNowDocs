# Business Continuity Management Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Business Continuity Management Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.3 - June 2026 \(Australia\)**
    1.  AI Agents and skills
    2.  Business Continuity management features for impact analysis, business continuity plans, crisis management, and crisis map.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - April 2026**

    Business Continuity Management Advanced helps organizations meet their business continuity and resilience objectives by identifying critical processes, assessing the impact of disruptions, and building, testing, and executing recovery plans to remain compliant and operative. This Advanced AI-enabled offering, delivers core business continuity management with AI-powered platform capabilities inclusive of skills and AI Agents. For organizations scaling their resilience program, the BCM Advanced offering adds emergency mass notification, crisis mapping, operational resilience, and performance analytics to manage complexity across the full continuity lifecycle.
