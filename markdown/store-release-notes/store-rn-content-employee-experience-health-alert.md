# Employee Experience Pack: Health Alert release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Health Alert in Content on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 4.1.0 - September 2022**
    -   Please note that version 4.1.x of this Experience Pack is compatible with the San Diego release only.
    -   The Health Alert Employee Experience Pack consists of a campaign, sample Knowledge content, and an HR Service for rapidly accelerating your implementation of this use case.
    -   Included content:
        -   1 Health Alert Campaign consisting of 1 bundle
        -   6 Campaign Content Items
            -   Travel Guidance Video To-do
            -   COVID-19 Updates Banner
            -   Employee E-mail Notification-COVID-19
            -   View COVID-19 Helpful Resources To-do
            -   Review the COVID-19 Facts To-do
            -   COVID-19 Video
        -   3 Knowledge Articles
            -   Leading High Performing Remote Teams
            -   COVID-19 FAQs
            -   COVID-19/Coronavirus Company Updates
        -   4 Knowledge Blocks
            -   Communicating and Supporting Your Team During A Health Crisis
            -   Employees In Significant Outbreak Areas
            -   Helpful Resources - US Employees
            -   Helpful Resources - Non-US Employees
            -   Employee Experience Packs Disclaimer for Implementation
        -   1 HR case preconfigured with the following content:
            -   HR Service: Question Regarding COVID-19
            -   HR Template: Question Regarding COVID-19
            -   Record Producer: Question Regarding COVID-19
