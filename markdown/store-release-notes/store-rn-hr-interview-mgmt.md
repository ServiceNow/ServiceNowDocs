# Interview management release notes

Version history for the Interview management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 4.0.0 - June 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Interview Health Tracker: Enable Recruiters to track Interview health centrally across their job requisitions.
        -   Interviewer Self-substitute workflow: Enable Interviewers to reassign their interviews to a substitute to reduce the overhead of manual coordination and the risk of rescheduling.
-   **Version 3.1.0 - March 2026**

    Minor UX enhancements

-   **Version 3.0.0 - December 2025**
    -   Addressed few minor defects.
    -   Updates to support the interview management agentic workflow.
-   **Version 2.0.0 - August 2025**

    Interview management enables Hiring Teams to streamline and organize their hiring process with respect to different types of interviews in the hiring context.It acts as a central hub for recruiters, hiring managers, and candidates, facilitating efficient scheduling, communication, evaluation, and feedback.
