# Strategic Portfolio Management - Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Strategic Portfolio Management - Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - April 2026**
    -   Transform how your teams plan and deliver work with SPM Advanced. Get AI-powered visibility across your entire portfolio: from demand intake to resource allocation, financial planning, and project execution. SPM Advanced allows leaders to make faster, more confident decisions.
    -   SPM Advanced delivers AI-native portfolio and work management at scale. It includes Now Assist for SPM and CWM, Moveworks for SPM, and full coverage across Portfolio Planning, Financial Planning, Project Portfolio Management, Demand, Resource, Cost, Digital Portfolio, Collaborative Work, Innovation, and Time Card Management.
