# Enterprise Asset Management for DCNAM release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Enterprise Asset Management for DCNAM application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - March 2026**

    Fixed: Translation supports multiple languages.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - December 2025**
    -   EAM for DCNAM extends ServiceNow’s Enterprise Asset Management capabilities to data center and network asset management environments. The application enables you to manage facility and linear assets within data centers and network domains, ensuring operational efficiency and compliance.
        -   Maximize asset availability and utilization.
        -   Improve capacity planning through performance trend analysis.
        -   Reduce operational costs via proactive maintenance and energy optimization.
        -   Enhance compliance and risk mitigation.
    -   EAM for DCNAM provides in-depth visibility into the entire asset life cycle for data center and network environments, helping organizations move from reactive to proactive maintenance strategies, which directly benefits operational efficiency and cost savings.
