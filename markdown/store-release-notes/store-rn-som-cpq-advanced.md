# CPQ - Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® CPQ - Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - April 2026**
    -   CPQ - Advanced extends the capabilities of CPQ - Foundation with agentic workflows, AI voice agents, and AI summarization for quote management. Designed for enterprises that require deeper automation across the order-to-revenue lifecycle, this solution reduces escalations, accelerates dispute resolution, and empowers sales agents with instant visibility into complex quotes.
    -   Built on the ServiceNow AI Platform, CPQ - Advanced includes prebuilt agentic workflows for RMA and invoice dispute handling, voice agents for order exceptions and invoice disputes, and AI summarization for quote management, delivering faster resolution and greater operational efficiency.
