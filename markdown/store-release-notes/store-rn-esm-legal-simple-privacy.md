# Legal Simple Privacy release notes

Version history for the Legal Simple Privacy application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.5 - December 2025**

    Changed: Privacy request record producer has been enhanced.

-   **Version 1.2.1 - June 2025**

    Fixed: The Privacy assessment topic was not visible in the ESC portal. This issue has been fixed and the topic is now visible.

-   **Version 1.2.0 - May 2025**
    -   Changed: Improved Legal Simple Privacy icon for improved space utilization in the intake form.
    -   Fixed: Privacy Assessment intake form is not available in Employee Center catalog. This issue has been fixed.
-   **Version 1.1.2 - February 2025**

    Fixed: The catalog items under Privacy Request were not displayed. This has been fixed.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - November 2024**

    Fixed localization fixes.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - June 2023**

    Fixed: New Record Producers are not automatically displayed in the ESC portal when new customers install Legal Simple Privacy.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - May 2023**

    ServiceNow  Legal  Simple  Privacy is a Legal Practice app  included with Legal Service Delivery that provides an automated workflow process to raise and track privacy assessment requests with in-house legals teams. The app helps quickly resolve privacy-related concerns and includes full audit tracking and reporting capabilities.
