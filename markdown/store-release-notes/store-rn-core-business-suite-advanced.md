# Core Business Suite Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Core Business Suite Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.0.5 - June 2026**
    -   Employee Slate is now the default experience - Core Business Suite now uses Employee Slate as the requester experience out-of -the-box, giving employees a single, consistent place to get help.
    -   CBS Analytics Dashboard - A new dashboard gives a consolidated, cross-business-unit view of service performance, including open and unassigned cases, mean time to resolve, and self-service resolution.
    -   Core Business Suite Advanced is now available for direct installation from the Product Hub. This enables one-click installation of all CBS Advanced applications.
-   **Version 3.0.0 - April 2026**

    Core Business Suite Advanced provides a unified case and knowledge management experience that consolidates advanced operational capabilities across Finance, HR, Procurement, Legal, Health &amp; Safety, and Facilities. It enables organizations to standardize, manage, and streamline critical employee and business workflows on a single, unified platform.
