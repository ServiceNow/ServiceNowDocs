# Employee Experience Foundation release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Foundation application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 30.0.3 - December 2025**
    -   Fixed the centre alignment issue of Outlook email notifications.
    -   Enhanced all images in the OOTB email layouts to include descriptive Alt attributes, ensuring better accessibility and alignment with WCAG standards.
-   **Version 30.0.1 - August 2025**

    Updated to support the latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 29.1.1 - May 2025**

    Set background colors for a selected canvas element in Rich Content Editor, as an admin and a content manager.

-   **Version 29.0.1 - November 2024**

    Fixed issues that impacted the user interface.

-   **Version 28.1.4 - August 2024**
    -   Improved functionality to enable admins to personalize email templates and layouts, while maintaining company branding facilitating the sending of knowledge articles and scheduled information to oneself for future viewing.
    -   Enabled 'week view' as the default view for schedule information
-   **Version 28.0.2 - May 2024**

    No new feature was delivered in May '24.

-   **Version 27.0.3 - February 2024**

    Minor fixes.

-   **Version 24.0.3 - May 2022**

    Support for changes in the Employee Center and the Content Experiences.

-   **Version 23.0.3 - November 2021**

    Employee experience foundation app delivers a notification layout and template for employee facing communications to create consistent messaging across the enterprise and improve the employee experience.
