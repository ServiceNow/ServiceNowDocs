# Third-party Risk Management Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Third-party Risk Management Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 22.3.2 - June 2026 \(Australia\)**
    -   New: Added skill categorization metadata for improved organization.
    -   Fixed: Resolved duplicate issue recommendations when multiple issues generated for a single question \(PRB2015422\).
-   **Version 22.0.1 - April 2026**

    Third‑party Risk Management Advanced is an AI‑enabled offering that delivers generative AI capabilities for assessment review and issue management. It helps third‑party risk managers and assessors review third‑party risk information more efficiently by reducing manual analysis and improving review consistency. With Third‑party Risk Management Advanced, users can leverage generative AI to summarize third‑party risk issues and identify potential issues during an assessment review. These capabilities support informed and consistent risk decisions while working with third‑party risk records.
