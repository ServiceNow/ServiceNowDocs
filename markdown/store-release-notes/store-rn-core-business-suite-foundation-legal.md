# Core Business Suite Foundation for Legal release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Core Business Suite Foundation for Legal application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.0.5 - June 2026**

    Shipped out-of-the-box assignment rules for the Core Business Suite for Legal. The goal is to give legal admins immediate visibility into existing rule configurations without manual setup, letting them review and understand what rules are active by default.

-   **Version 3.0.0 - April 2026**

    The Core Business Suite Foundation for Legal provides pre-configured workflows for streamlined case and knowledge management, along with foundational AI capabilities for the Legal department.
