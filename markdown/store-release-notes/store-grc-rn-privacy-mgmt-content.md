# Privacy Management Content release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Privacy Management Content application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 22.3.1 - June 2026 \(Australia\)**

    New: Added authority documents, citations, and risk statements for India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act \(DPDPA\) and for U.S. state privacy laws for Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Nevada. The expanded coverage enables organizations to assess and manage compliance obligations across a broader set of global and domestic privacy frameworks out of the box.

-   **Version 22.0.3 - March 2026**

    Reduce manual effort and accelerate privacy program setup with pre-built frameworks and standards — complete with authority documents, citations, control objectives, and risk statements.
