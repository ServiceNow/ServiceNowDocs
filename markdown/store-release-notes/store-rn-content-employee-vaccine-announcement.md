# Employee Experience Pack: Employee Vaccine Announcement release notes

Version history for the Employee Vaccine Announcement on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.0 - January 2021**
    -   New: Employee Experience Pack:
        -   Employee Vaccine Announcement The Employee Vaccine Announcement Experience Pack consists of a campaign, sample Knowledge content, and an HR Service for rapidly accelerating your implementation of this use case.
        -   Included content:
            -   3 Campaign Content Items
            -   Vaccine Reimbursement Program Announcement Banner
            -   Vaccine Reimbursement Program Announcement Styled Content
            -   Vaccine Reimbursement Mobile Content
        -   2 Knowledge Articles
            -   Company Vaccine Policy FAQs
            -   Vaccine Reimbursement Program KB
        -   2 HR Services preconfigured with the following content:
            -   HR Service: General Vaccine Policy Inquiry
            -   HR Service: Vaccine Reimbursement Request
