# Legal Counsel Center Classic release notes

Version history for the Legal Counsel Center Classic application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 6.5.0 - November 2024**

    Fixed localization issues.

-   **Version 6.4.1 - May 2024**
    -   Fixed:
        -   The Legal Contracts related list was not displayed even though the contract was signed. This is now fixed.
        -   When you Close Complete a legal request, some information on the pop-up window wasn't translated.
-   **Version 6.3.8 - September 2023**

    Fixed: Approval list is loading when you go to My Work &gt; Items Pending Approval &gt;Delegated to Me &gt; Approvals.

-   **Version 6.3.1 - August 2023**
    -   Changed: Renamed the application from "Legal Counsel Center" to "Legal Counsel Center Classic".
    -   Fixed: You can now create a Content Review request from the All Requests page.
-   **Version 6.0.0 - February 2023**
    -   New: Implemented fulfiller experience for Third Party Contracts workflow
    -   Fixes
-   **Version 5.2.1 - November 2022**
    -   New: Added new widgets for the delegated legal requests and legal matters.
    -   Changed:
        -   Updated titles of existing widgets that had open legal requests or open legal matters.
        -   Reordered the Add and Remove buttons in the Legal Matter Tasks tab.
-   **Version 5.1.0 - August 2022**

    Fixed: Minor fixes and changes.

-   **Version 5.0.4 - May 2022**

    New: Added a new Documents related list in the legal request form to list the documents uploaded in external storage.

-   **Version 4.2.1 - February 2022**
    -   New:
        -   Added a new list item Set Delegate for setting a delegate
        -   Added a new list item 'Delegated to me' to view approvals, legal requests, legal matters, legal mater tasks that have been delegated to a user.
    -   Changed: Legal: Counsel Center has been renamed Legal Counsel Center
-   **Version 4.1.2 - December 2021**
    -   Changed: Refactoring of components and minor fixes
    -   Fixed: An issue where the form title of a legal matter was showing short description instead of the name of the legal matter
-   **Version 4.0.3 - September 2021**
    -   New:
        -   Added email composer to enable sending and receiving of emails from the legal request, legal matter, and legal matter task record forms.
        -   View your draft emails from the Email list item.
    -   Changed: Contracts Repository list item under Simple Contracts has been renamed Legal Contracts.
-   **Version 3.6.1 - June 2021**
    -   New: Added options to view similar legal requests and similar legal matters in the Counsel Assist section
    -   Changed: Updated widgets to include adhoc approvals
-   **Version 3.5.2 - April 2021**
    -   New:
        -   The Legal Counsel Center application includes the following features:
        -   Multi-tab interface to address multiple requests and matters
        -   Real-time activity queue with open and unassigned KPI's
        -   Legal request approvals
        -   Department and user-level metrics
        -   Intuitive search and filter capabilities enabling users to quickly find relevant content
-   **Version 3.4.0 - March 2021**
    -   New:
        -   The Legal Counsel Center application includes the following features:
        -   Multi-tab interface to address multiple requests and matters
        -   Real-time activity queue with open and unassigned KPI's
        -   Legal request approvals
        -   Department and user-level metrics
        -   Intuitive search and filter capabilities enabling users to quickly find relevant content
