# APO - Foundation release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® APO - Foundation application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.0 - June 2026**

    Updated app/plugin dependencies.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - May 2026**

    APO - Foundation is the foundation-tier AI subscription for accounts payable operations on the ServiceNow AI Platform. Extending Now Assist for APO, this offering unlocks Platform Foundation features for enterprises that require the most advanced AI capabilities across their accounts payable workflows. APO - Foundation includes foundational skills and adds access to Now Assist Platform Foundation, enabling organizations to take advantage of the latest platform-level AI innovations as they become available.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - April 2026**

    The ServiceNow Accounts Payable Operations Foundation application equips AP help desk agents with assistive Now Assist skills embedded directly in their invoice and case management workflows. From summarizing invoice cases to drafting email responses, these AI-powered skills reduce manual effort and help agents resolve issues faster — while keeping humans in control at every step.
