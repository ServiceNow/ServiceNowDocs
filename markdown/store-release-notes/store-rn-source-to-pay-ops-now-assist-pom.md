# Now Assist for Purchase Order Management \(POM\) release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Now Assist for Purchase Order Management \(POM\) application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.2.0 - June 2026**

    New: Enhancement to the automatic conversion of supplier emails into PO Exception cases, now supporting additional languages and ERP purchase order IDs for improved PO identification.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - April 2026**

    Now Assist for Purchase Order Management includes prebuilt AI agents that automate purchase order creation from emails, and analyze delivery gaps to identify and implement mitigation strategies, helping reduce resolution times and enhance customer satisfaction.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - March 2026**

    Agentic capabilities to support efficient resolution of purchase order exceptions.
