# Adoption Blueprint for Technology Excellence release notes

Version history for the Admin Center Adoption Blueprint for Technology Excellence application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.5.0 - August 2024**
    -   New:
        -   Change Management in Adoption Blueprints
            -   Select the Modernize your change management process adoption blueprint to get a walk-through on how you can adopt the required capabilities to allow your change process to dynamically scale without compromising stability and governance.
-   **Version 1.4.0 - November 2023**

    New: Added Task Intelligence for ITSM.

-   **Version 1.3.0 - August 2023**

    Fixed:

    -   Resolved localization issues.
    -   Fixed broken product and video links.
-   **Version 1.2.2 - May 2023**

    Fixed: Fixed broken links and updated documentation to link to latest release version.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - August 2022**

    These adoption blueprints can be used to address your organizational needs for achieving business growth. Each blueprint focuses on a strategic priority. It suggests applications and plugins best suited for your business goal.
