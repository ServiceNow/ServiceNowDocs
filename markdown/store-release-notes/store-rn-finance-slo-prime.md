# SLO - Prime release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® SLO - Prime application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.0 - June 2026**

    Updated app/plugin dependencies

-   **Version 1.1.0 - May 2026**

    SLO - Prime is the highest-tier Al subscription for supplier lifecycle operations on the ServiceNow Al Platform. Extending SLO - Foundation, this offering unlocks Platform Prime features for enterprises that require the most advanced Al capabilities across their supplier workflows. SLO - Prime includes everything in SLO - Foundation and adds access to Now Assist Platform Prime, enabling organizations to take full advantage of the latest platform-level Al innovations as they become available.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - April 2026**

    The ServiceNow Supplier Lifecycle Operations Prime application delivers a comprehensive suite of AI-powered skills and agents that transform how supplier management specialists handle onboarding, qualification, and inquiries. From intelligent document extraction and automated risk assessment to agentic workflows that resolve exceptions and route ownership — Prime accelerates end-to-end supplier lifecycle operations while keeping humans in control at every step.
