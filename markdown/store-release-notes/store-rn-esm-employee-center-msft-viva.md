# Employee Center for Microsoft Viva Connections release notes

Version history for the on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.8 - August 2025**

    Fixed minor bugs.

-   **Version 2.0.4 - February 2024**

    Minor fixes.

-   **Version 2.0.3 - August 2023**

    For customers using Microsoft Viva Connections, employees are informed of tasks that need their attention and can quickly access Employee Center, where they can get help, stay informed, and manage their tasks and requests.
