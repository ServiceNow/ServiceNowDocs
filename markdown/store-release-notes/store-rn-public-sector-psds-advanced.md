# PSDS - Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® PSDS - Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - April 2026**
    -   PSDS product tier Advanced enables additional AI capabilities to be available within Public Sector Digital Services in alignment with ServiceNow’s product tier packaging approach.
    -   This application supports entitlement‑based access to AI features provided by PSDS and the ServiceNow platform beyond the baseline level. Availability of specific AI capabilities depends on the customer’s licensed tier and applicable entitlements.
    -   For information about licensing tiers and availability, refer to the PSDS Australia Release Notes.
