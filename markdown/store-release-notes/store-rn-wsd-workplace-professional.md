    ServiceNow Workplace Service Delivery Professional provides a suite of applications to help deliver digital experiences for employees to interact with the services offered by their workplace. The solution also supports workplace/facilities leads with full workplace management and optimization.

    The suite includes the following applications:

    -   Workplace Case Management
    -   Workplace Reservation Management
    -   Workplace Calendar Synchronization
    -   Workplace Indoor Mapping
    -   Workplace Visitor Management
    -   Workplace Central
    -   Workplace Reservations for Microsoft Outlook Add-in
    -   Workplace Service Delivery with Mappedin Integration
    -   Workplace Core
    The Workplace Service Delivery Professional application lets customers easily download all licensed Workplace Service Delivery applications at once, eliminating the need to install them individually.

    To automatically install all the Workplace Service Delivery Professional applications at once, download the ServiceNow Workplace Service Delivery Professional suite.
