# Employee Center integration with Zoom release notes

Version history for the HR Employee Center integration with Zoom application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.16 - December 2025**

    Fix provided with this release: Resolved a stored Cross-Site Scripting \(XSS\) security vulnerability to eliminate the risk of client-side attacks on the browser of a user.

-   **Version 2.0.10 - May 2024**

    No new feature was delivered in May '24.

-   **Version 2.0.3 - November 2023**

    Minor fixes.

-   **Version 2.0.1 - May 2023**
    -   Changed:
        -   Eliminate the need for admins to manually build a private Employee Center Zoom app by publishing a public Zoom app \(ServiceNow Employee Center\) in the Zoom App Marketplace.
        -   Connect your ServiceNow instance with your Zoom account to complete the configuration for the public Zoom app.
        -   Embed a custom portal rather than Employee Center in Zoom.
-   **Version 1.0.5 - November 2022**

    Provides the admin configurations needed for setting up Employee Center in Zoom.
