# Employee Experience Pack: Employee Transfers release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Employee Transfers on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - November 2022**
    -   Please note that version 2.1.0 of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the San Diego release only.
    -   These update sets contain a Transfer Lifecycle Event modeled after implementation best practices.
    -   Included content:
        -   Lifecycle Event consisting of 7 Activity Sets
        -   Change Requested
        -   HR Review and Validation
        -   Approvals
        -   Formal Communication to Employee
        -   Planning for Transition
        -   New Job Starts
        -   Integration Into New Team
    -   38 Activities:
        -   6 Catalog Items
        -   5 approvals
        -   1 Checklist
        -   1 E-Signature
        -   1 HR Service
        -   17 Mark When Complete
        -   2 Notification
        -   1 Sign Document tasks
        -   4 Take Survey task
        -   4 Surveys
        -   1 Document Template
        -   1 Guided Setup and 1 Workflow
        -   2 Flows
            1.  Transfer - Create Job Record
            2.  Transfer - Update Primary Job
    -   14 Knowledge Articles:
        -   Internal Mobility Resources and Info
        -   Internal Mobility Resources and Info for Managers
        -   Talking to Employees About Career Growth
        -   Transfer and Promotion Process Overview
        -   Communicating to Your Employees About Their Job Change
        -   Communicating to Employees About Organizational Change
        -   Compensation Guidelines template
        -   Setting Performance Goals and SMART Goal Planning Template
        -   Transitioning Out of Your Current Role
        -   Assimilating Into a New Team and Job Role
        -   Internal Mobility Policy
        -   Employee Integration Planning Template
        -   Manager Checklist
        -   Internal Mobility FAQ
