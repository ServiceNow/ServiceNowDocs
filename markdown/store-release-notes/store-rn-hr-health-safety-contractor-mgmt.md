# Health and Safety Contractor Management release notes

Version history for the Health and Safety Contractor Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 5.2.0 - June 2026**
    -   New: Added WCAG 2.2AA 400% zoom/reflow accessibility support for the Contractor Service Center
    -   Changed: Streamlined Contractor onboarding into a single-flow experience accessible from both Workspace and Classic UI
-   **Version 5.1.0 - May 2026**

    Fixed: Fixed missing contractor action notification when a Health and Safety action is assigned to a contractor user.

-   **Version 5.0.0 - December 2025**

    Changed: Added button under Contractor to primary style

-   **Version 4.0.0 - August 2025**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Minor ACL updates
        -   Improved process for adding contractor workers
-   **Version 3.0.0 - May 2025**
    -   New: Action scheduler for contractors to schedule and auto-assign actions using the condition builder.
    -   Fixed: Spelling typo for the company writer role
-   **Version 2.0.0 - February 2025**
    -   Fixed:
        -   "Reply to" section is not populated when an Action is assigned to a contractor
        -   Empty value for "Primary contract" under Contractor record in Health and Safety
        -   Contractor inbound responses to actions not working
-   **Version 1.0.1 - December 2024**

    Changed: The "Primary contact" field on the contractor company record in the Employee Center and Health and Safety Workspace is no longer mandatory.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - November 2024**

    ServiceNow Health and Safety Contractor Management provides your teams with contractor onboarding capabilities enabling contractor coordinators to onboard contractor companies and their workers and keep track of them in the Health and Safety application. With the included email notifications, each contractor can be assigned prerequisite tasks and documents before they are allowed on site.
