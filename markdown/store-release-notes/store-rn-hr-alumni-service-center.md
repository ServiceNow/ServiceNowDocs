# Alumni Center release notes

Version history for the HR Service Delivery Alumni Center on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 4.2.1 - June 2026**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Work on implementing Non-Glide Cobalt Raven ACLs
        -   Enforcing cross-scope access checks for the getRefRecord\(\) API
-   **Version 4.1.2 - March 2026**
    -   Fixed: Defects in navigation, label changes, and UI corrections.
    -   New: Added automation cases.
-   **Version 4.0.2 - December 2025**
    -   New:
        -   New Alumni Portal \(Alumni Portal Revamp.mp4\)
        -   Revamped Alumni Center with Employee Center Pro experiences such as My Active Items, Proactive Prompts, and Content Widgets.
        -   Improved alumni services are available out of the box.
        -   Access your documents directly and securely from your profile.
        -   Maintain and update your personal details directly within your profile.
        -   Enter your legacy employment details and latest employment details to maintain your employment history.
        -   Enable or disable notifications for recommended job opportunities and news/event updates in your profile.
        -   Admin can showcase the recommended job opportunities.​​​
-   **Version 3.0.3 - August 2025**

    Fixed: Data dictionary changes for Employee Number.

-   **Version 2.4.0 - February 2025**
    -   Improved Security: The new improvements ensure that your data and resources are better safeguarded against unauthorized access.
    -   No Action Required: These updates are implemented seamlessly on the backend, requiring no action or configuration changes from your side.
-   **Version 2.2.0 - November 2024**

    Fixed: Security issues.

-   **Version 2.1.2 - August 2023**
    -   New: Enabled dark theme for Alumni Service Center pages.
    -   Fixed: HR Services that are part of the Alumni Service Center scope are not showing up on the case creation page.
-   **Version 2.1.1 - December 2022**

    Fixes an issue where avatars may not be displayed properly in the Alumni Service Center portal header

-   **Version 2.1.0 - July 2021**
    -   Changed: Now depends on ESC Pro
    -   Fixed: Missing RCA file added
-   **Version 2.0.2 - January 2021**
    -   New:
        -   ASC users \(alumni\) can create the following types of cases:
            -   General inquiry
            -   Payroll discrepancy
            -   Employment verification
-   **Version 1.1.0 - September 2020**

    Provide a centralized hub for your former and furloughed employees to stay connected and engaged with your organization.
