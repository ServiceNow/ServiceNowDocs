# Deal Registration release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Deal Registration application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 3.2.0 - June 2026**
    -   Minor enhancements:
        -   Enhanced field validations on deal registration table
        -   Fixes  - related to account field , deal registration field on deal line items , b2b &amp; b2c usecases
-   **Version 2.2.0 - June 2026 \(Zurich\)**
    -   Minor enhancements:
        -   Enhanced field validations on deal registration table
        -   Fixes  - related to account field , deal registration field on deal line items , b2b &amp; b2c usecases
-   **Version 3.1.2 - May 2026**

    Changed: security upgrades

-   **Version 2.1.1 - May 2026 \(Zurich\)**

    Changed: security upgrades

-   **Version 3.0.0 - March 2026 \(Australia\)**
    -   New: Review page is added as the final step in the deal creation workflow.
    -   Changed:
        -   Deal initiators will now be able to delete deals in the draft state.
        -   List view access to approvers in workspace
-   **Version 2.0.0 - March 2026 \(Zurich\)**
    -   New:
        -   Deal initiators will now be able to delete deals in thedraft state.
        -   Approvers in workspace now have access to the list view of deal registrations.
-   **Version 1.1.0 - January 2026**
    -   Changed:
        -   Enhanced the Deal registration review page in the playbook
        -   Usability Enhancements on the deal registration form \(swapping the order of Deal registration line items &amp; Approvals in the related list etc.\)
-   **Version 1.0.0 - December 2025**

    Streamline your channel sales with a comprehensive deal registration solution. This app enables channel partners to register deals through structured workflows, receive real-time status updates, and prevent duplicate registrations.
