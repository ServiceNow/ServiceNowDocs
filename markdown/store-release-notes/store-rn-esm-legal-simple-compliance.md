# Legal Simple Compliance release notes

Version history for the Legal Simple Compliance application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.5 - June 2026**

    Fixed: Version compatibility for the dependent apps.

-   **Version 1.2.0 - March 2026**

    New: Legal Conflict of Interest application supports conversational intake to submit disclosures using natural language from the Now Assist in Virtual Agent. It also has risk assessment utility to evaluate the request against predefined risk factors and calculate risk score. Based on the score, requests are routed to the appropriate approval workflow for faster resolution.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - December 2025**

    Changed: Security enhancements.

-   **Version 1.0.3 - May 2025**
    -   The Legal Compliance suite installs the following applications:
        -   Legal Conflict of Interest
        -   Gifts and Entertainment Compliance
        -   Legal Stock Preclearance
    -   For more details, refer to their respective release notes.
-   **Version 1.0.1 - March 2025**
    -   The ServiceNow Legal Simple Compliance suite provides pre-built legal workflows to submit and manage legal compliance requests. It includes the following applications:
        -   Legal Conflict of Interest - Manage the disclosure, approval, and registry of conflict of interest that might arise from employees having competing interests or loyalties.
        -   Gifts and Entertainment Compliance - Manage the disclosure that might arise from employees giving or receiving gifts or entertainment to internal or external parties. This also enables you to comply with company policies.
        -   Legal Stock Preclearance - Manage stock preclearance requests complying with the company's stock preclearance policy.
