# Enterprise Asset Management for Facilities release notes

Version history for the Enterprise Asset Management for Facilities on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.0 - August 2024**

    Enterprise Asset Management for Facilities is an application that provides facility asset managers and facility asset technicians with access to Enterprise Asset Management workflows and features to manage the full lifecycle of facility models and assets.
