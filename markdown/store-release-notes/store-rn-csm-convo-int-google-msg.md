# Conversational Integration with Google Business Messages release notes

Version history for the Conversational Integration with Google Business Messages application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.1 - February 2024**

    Minor fixes.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - August 2023**

    Minor fixes.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - February 2023**
    -   ServiceNow Conversational Integration with Google Business Messages enables brands to engage with their customers using Google Business Messages.
    -   New:
        -   Customer-initiated conversations
        -   Agent-initiated conversations
        -   System-initiated conversations
