# Employee Experience Pack: HR Knowledge Starter Content release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: HR Knowledge Starter Content on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.0 - November 2022**
    -   Employee Experience Pack: HR Knowledge Starter Content \(v1.2.0\)
    -   These update sets contain a set of generic sample Knowledge articles and blocks that provide general layout examples and guidance for structuring effective knowledge content. These articles and blocks are meant to be used as a guideline for creating and configuring your own information in knowledge articles and blocks.
-   **Version 1.0.0 - November 2020**
    -   New:

These articles and blocks are meant to be used as a guideline for creating and configuring your own information in knowledge articles and blocks.
