# Core Business Suite Prime for Workplace Services release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Core Business Suite Prime for Workplace Services application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.0.5 - June 2026**

    Core Business Suite Prime for Workplace Services is now available for direct installation from the Product Hub. This enables one-click installation of all CBS Prime of Workplace Services.

-   **Version 3.0.2 - April 2026**

    Core Business Suite Prime includes the highest tier of AI-native capabilities for the Workplace Service Delivery department.
