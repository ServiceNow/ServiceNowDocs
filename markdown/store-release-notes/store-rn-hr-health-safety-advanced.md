# Health and Safety - Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Health and Safety - Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.4 - May 2026**

    Fixed: Updated version to align with latest hard-dependency versions.

-   **Version 1.0.3 - April 2026**
    -   Health and Safety - Advanced extends the ServiceNow Health and Safety Foundation tier with deeper EHS capabilities for organizations managing chemical compliance, contractor safety, and AI-assisted incident management. Advanced includes Now Assist for Health and Safety and AI Agents for Health and Safety, bringing AI-powered summarization, pattern analysis, and agentic workflows to your EHS program.
    -   Designed for mid-to-large organizations that need more than core incident management, Advanced provides the tools to manage chemical inventories, assess risks systematically, and keep contractor workforces safe, all on the ServiceNow platform.
