# Field Service Advanced Capacity and Reservations Management release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Field Service Advanced Capacity and Reservations Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 30.2.5 - June 2026**

    New: Supports territory-specific, recurrence-based demand channel association with technicians. It enables planners to assign technicians to demand channels with complex recurrence patterns \(specific weekdays, weeks, or month-days\) and define start or end dates.

-   **Version 30.0.3 - March 2026**
    -   Field Service Advanced Capacity and Reservations Management gives organizations with complex service demand granular control over how capacity is defined, reserved, and allocated across work types, demand channels, time periods, and both internal teams and external contractors.
    -   It provides access to Capacity Console, a dedicated workspace view that gives planners real-time visibility into allocated, used, and unused capacity alongside reservations, assigned tasks, and booked work. Planners can monitor consumption, identify gaps, and adjust before demand outpaces availability.
    -   Integrated with Dynamic Scheduling, it automates work assignment based on configured capacity rules and real-time availability so the schedule reflects actual capacity.
