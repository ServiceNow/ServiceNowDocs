# Employee Slate Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Employee Slate Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.2 - June 2026 \(Australia\)**
    -   Enhanced canvas with a feed widget to give employees a robust news experience.
    -   Enhanced the authoring and usability experience for content managers.
-   **Version 1.1.0 - June 2026 \(Zurich\)**
    -   Enhanced canvas with a feed widget to give employees a robust news feed experience.
    -   Enhanced the authoring and usability experience for content managers.
-   **Version 1.0.2 - May 2026**

    Deliver an AI-powered employee experience where employees stay informed, find what they need, and boost everyday productivity. Targeted communications, AI-assisted content authoring, and AI-native service delivery—all in one experience that scales across your entire organization. Note: This is an add-on application and requires either Employee Slate for Moveworks or Employee Slate for Now Assist to be separately downloaded.
