# Vulnerability Response mobile experience release notes

Version history for the Vulnerability Management mobile experience on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 11.1.1 - May 2023**

    Version 11.1.1 certified for Utah, Tokyo, and San Diego.

-   **Version 11.0.0 - October 2020**

    New: Version 11.0.0 certified for Orlando and Paris.

-   **Version 10.0.0 - March 2020**
    -   New:
        -   Added Vulnerability Entry field on the Vulnerability Group form
        -   Minor bug fixes
        -   Recertified for Orlando
    -   Changes: Fixes
-   **Version 8.0.7 - July 2019**

    As a vulnerability response remediation owner, you can use the Vulnerability Management Mobile app to access a ServiceNow AI Platform® instance from your Android or iOS mobile device. Receive notifications when vulnerability groups or vulnerability items are assigned to you or to your assignment group. Begin remediation on your most critical vulnerabilities without being tied to the desktop.
