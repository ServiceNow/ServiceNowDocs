# Source-to-Pay Operations with Contract Management Pro release notes

Version history for the Source-to-Pay Operations with Contract Management Pro application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.0.1 - May 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Reviewing contracts is often a time-consuming task for sourcing managers, especially when identifying key metadata. Using Now Assist for Contract Management in Sourcing and Procurement Operations simplifies this process by intelligently scanning contracts and extracting pre-configured metadata, all presented in a clear, easy-to-navigate view within the Source-to-Pay workspace.
        -   Checking whether all required contractual clauses are included often involves extensive back-and-forth with legal teams. Using Now Assist for Contract Management in Sourcing and Procurement Operations addresses this challenge by detecting missing or non-standard clauses and presenting them in a user-friendly format. This enables smoother collaboration between sourcing and legal teams, making contract redlining more efficient and accurate.
-   **Version 1.0.7 - February 2025**

    Minor bug fixes.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - May 2024**

    Brings together the procurement capabilities of Source-to-Pay Operations and the contracting capabilities of Contract Management Pro to create a fully embedded experience for sourcing and procurement professionals to work closer with their legal professionals.
