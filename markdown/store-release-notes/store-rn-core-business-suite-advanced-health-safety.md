# Core Business Suite Advanced for Health and Safety release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Core Business Suite Advanced for Health and Safety application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.0.5 - June 2026**

    Updated to support the latest version of the dependent apps.

-   **Version 3.0.1 - April 2026**

    Core Business Suite Advanced for Health and Safety extends the capabilities of the Foundation version to support more complex enterprise workflows. It provides advanced AI capabilities for the Health and Safety department.
