# Sales Agreement Management release notes

Version history for the CSM Sales Agreement Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 8.0.0 - December 2025 \(Yokohama, Zurich\)**

    New: Introduced granular admin roles for enhanced security

-   **Version 7.0.0 - May 2025 \(Yokohama\)**

    Bug fixes.

-   **Version 6.0.0 - May 2025 \(Washington DC, Xanadu\)**

    Bug fixes.

-   **Version 5.0.0 - February 2025**

    Changed: Use asynchronous process for creation of sales agreements from quote.

-   **Version 2.0.0 - November 2024**
    -   New:
        -   Sales agreements can be created for configurable and bundled product offerings.
        -   Capture different prices applicable for different periods for an item.
-   **Version 1.0.1 - August 2024**

    The Sales Agreement Management application delivers the capability to generate sales agreements from quotes. It integrates with quote management, pricing management and order management and allows creation of agreement-based pricing on orders.
