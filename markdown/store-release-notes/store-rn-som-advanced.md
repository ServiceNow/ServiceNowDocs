# Sales CRM - Advanced release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Sales CRM - Advanced application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - April 2026**
    -   Sales CRM - Advanced delivers autonomous AI agents and agentic workflows purpose-built for sales and order management. Designed for sales development teams managing high-volume pipelines, this solution accelerates lead engagement, automates routine outreach, and streamlines appointment scheduling, freeing sellers to focus on higher-value activity.
    -   Built on the ServiceNow AI Platform and extending CPQ - Advanced, Sales CRM - Advanced includes prebuilt SDR AI agents for lead nurturing, inbound email handling, appointment management, lead outreach, and lead disinterest detection, driving faster pipeline progression and more consistent sales execution.
