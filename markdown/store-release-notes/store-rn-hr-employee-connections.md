# Employee Connections release notes

Version history for the Employee Connections on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.9 - August 2024**

    New: Enable Advanced Portal Navigation for Employee Connections.

-   **Version 1.0.6 - May 2024**
    -   Fixed various internationalization bugs
    -   Fixed issue with job title search when searching for mentors
-   **Version 1.0.4 - February 2024**

    Employee Connections offers improved networking opportunities and enables individuals to forge meaningful connections. It equips employees with tools such as the Mentoring, facilitating the search for guidance and suitable mentors to expedite their learning and amplify their potential for career advancement.
