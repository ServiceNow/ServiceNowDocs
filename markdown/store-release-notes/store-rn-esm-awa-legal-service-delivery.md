# Advanced Work Assignment for Legal Service Delivery release notes

Version history for the Advanced Work Assignment for Legal Service Delivery application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.1 - June 2025**

    Fixed: General Legal Request was not available in Advanced Work Assignment queue. This has been fixed and it is not available in the queue.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - November 2024**

    Fixed localization issues.

-   **Version 1.0.4 - August 2024**

    Advanced Work Assignment for Legal Service Delivery provides configurations to support automatic routing, queuing and assignment of Legal Requests and Matters.
