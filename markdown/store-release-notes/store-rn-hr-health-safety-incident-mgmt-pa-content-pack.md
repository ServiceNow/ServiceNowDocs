# Health and Safety Incident Management PA Content Pack release notes

Version history for the Health and Safety Incident Management PA Content Pack on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 10.1.0 - June 2026**

    Supported for updates in Health and Safety applications.

-   **Version 10.0.1 - May 2026**

    Changed: Localization file updates for supported languages.

-   **Version 10.0.0 - August 2025**

    Changed: Updated indicators to support the new Health and Safety dashboard for Health and Safety Incident Management and Health and Safety Risk Management applications.

-   **Version 9.0.0 - May 2025**

    Changed: Updated localization translations.

-   **Version 8.0.0 - November 2024**

    New: Pack updated to work with V8.0 of the Health and Safety Incident Management application.

-   **Version 7.0.0 - August 2024**

    Changed: Updated library dependencies.

-   **Version 6.0.0 - May 2024**

    Changed: TRIR and DART metrics adjusted to pull total work hours per establishment.

-   **Version 5.0.0 - February 2024**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Includes fixes from previous v4 patch releases:
            -   Fixes for OSHA compliance updates.
-   **Version 4.0.2 - September 2023**

    Fixed: Fixes for OSHA compliance updates.

-   **Version 4.0.0 - August 2023**
    -   New: New indicators included for the new role-specific landing pages.
    -   Changed: Updated some of the existing indicators to support the new role-specific landing pages.
-   **Version 3.0.0 - May 2023**
    -   New: Added support for internationalization
    -   Fixed:
        -   ACL issues
        -   Issues related to incident occurrence date and time
        -   Workbench loading issues
        -   SQL related exceptions
        -   Localization issues
        -   RCA UI issues
        -   Affected employee issues
-   **Version 2.0.2 - March 2023**

    Fixed: Fixes for health and safety incident management

-   **Version 2.0.0 - February 2023**

    The application generates and shows safety frequency rate metrics for the Health and Safety application.
