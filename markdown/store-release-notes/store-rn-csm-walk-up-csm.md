# Walk-Up for CSM release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Walk-Up for CSM application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

-   **Version 2.1.0 - June 2026**
    -   New: Access walk-up check-in from the Business Portal menu
    -   Changed: WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility compliance in the Walk-up Portal.
    -   Fixed: Defect fixes
-   **Version 2.0.1 - March 2026**

    Walk-up for Customer Service Management \(CSM\) helps organizations manage in-person customer visits at physical service locations. Customers can check in online or on-site at a designated walk-up service center. Staff can then manage queues, track visits, and provide real-time support at service desks and help counters. Walk-up for CSM works as part of the broader CSM workflow to deliver fast, face-to-face assistance where customers need it most.
