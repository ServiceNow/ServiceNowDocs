# Legal Counsel Center Components release notes

Version history for the Legal Counsel Center Components on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 5.5.1 - May 2024**

    Fixed: When initiating an approval request, user was able to enter any email id and proceed with initiating the request. This is now fixed and user can only select from the valid list of users.

-   **Version 5.4.1 - June 2023**

    Fixed: For Third-party contracts, internal signatory information is not pre-populated in the Legal Counsel Center.

-   **Version 5.3.0 - February 2023**

    Changed: Made changes for Third party contracts workflow

-   **Version 5.1.0 - August 2022**

    Changed: The 'Document' option is added to the "Approval for" field in the Adhoc Approval pop-up window in legal matters.

-   **Version 5.0.2 - May 2022**

    Changed: The 'Document' option is added to the "Approval for" field in the Adhoc Approval pop-up window.

-   **Version 4.1.2 - February 2022**

    Changed: Legal: Counsel Center Components has been renamed Legal Counsel Center Components

-   **Version 4.1.0 - December 2021**

    Fixed: Minor fixes and refactoring of components

-   **Version 4.0.1 - September 2021**

    Fixed: Minor fixes

-   **Version 3.6.0 - June 2021**

    New: Seismic component to choose approver or user groups and attachment or artifact for adhoc approvals

-   **Version 3.4.0 - March 2021**
    -   New:
        -   The Legal Counsel Center components has Seismic components used by the Legal Counsel Center app, compatible with Orlando family:
            -   Customized Multi-record associator
            -   Customized Checklist
            -   Customized welcome header
            -   Customized bar chart
