# Opportunity Management ML release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Opportunity Management ML application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.2 - June 2026**

    Opportunity Management ML uses machine learning and AI to score opportunities, surface risk signals, and deliver qualitative insights that help sellers and leaders make faster, more confident decisions. Designed to support AEs, managers, and RevOps alike, this plugin integrates directly into the opportunity workspace to bring data-driven visibility into every deal.
