# Service Bridge for Public Sector Digital Services \(PSDS\) release notes

Version history for the Service Exchange for Public Sector Digital Services \(PSDS\) application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.2 - May 2025**

    Service Bridge for Public Sector Digital Services \(PSDS\) enables government agencies to securely connect with other agencies and providers through their ServiceNow instance. Service Bridge provides seamless connections across the provider's ecosystem to enable flow-through service and support requests, without having to set up costly custom integrations between agencies.
