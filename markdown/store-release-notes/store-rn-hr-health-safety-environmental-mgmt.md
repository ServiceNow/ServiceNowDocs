# Health and Safety Environmental Management release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Health and Safety Environmental Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.2.0 - June 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Addition of ingredients by searching for a CAS number to auto-populate data.
        -   Secret flag on ingredient record form.
        -   Automatic deletion of ingredient records when their parent chemical is deleted.
        -   Progress indicator display during automatic chemical creation.
        -   Regulatory list module in Health and Safety workspace.
-   **Version 2.1.0 - May 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Re-sync Regulatory Lists from 3E API for substance \(Regulatory Intel integration\).
        -   Regulatory tab on Chemical page.
        -   List filters by 3E Regulatory Topics on the Regulation list.
        -   Regulatory data sync with 3E validation on Health and Safety workspace.
        -   Regulatory list and record validation on Health and Safety workspace.
    -   Fixed:
        -   Localization issue in Chemical flows.
        -   Draft-save issues with the location field in chemical requests in the Employee Center.
-   **Version 2.0.1 - March 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Added a new data model for Properties and First-aid information on chemical records, enabling richer chemical data capture and management.
        -   Added Workspace and Employee Service Center support for syncing Properties and First Aid data with 3E, keeping chemical records aligned with the 3E source of truth.
-   **Version 1.0.2 - December 2025**
    -   ServiceNow Environmental Management helps organizations manage chemical safety and environmental obligations with ease. Employees can request chemicals and access Safety Data Sheets \(SDS\) directly through Employee Center and Now Mobile, ensuring quick and accurate information at their fingertips. Chemical managers can manage chemical requests and chemical and substance lists and their related documents, item lists, ingredients and hazard identification.
        -   Featuring a Chemical Library and a Health and Safety Workspace, safety and compliance teams can:
            -   Track and manage chemical inventories and hazard classifications aligned with Globally Harmonized System \(GHS\) standards.
            -   Conduct risk assessments on chemicals and manage related documents.
            -   Use integrated tools for employee chemical requests and configurable approval workflows for chemical managers.
            -   Leverage integration connectors for automated SDS and chemical metadata population, reducing manual effort and improving regulatory accuracy.
