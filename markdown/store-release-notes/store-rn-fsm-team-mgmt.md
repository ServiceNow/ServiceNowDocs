# Team Management for Field Service release notes

Version history for the Team Management for Field Service on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 7.1.0 - June 2026**

    Fixed Performance defect

-   **Version 7.0.2 - March 2026**

    Changed: Modified Granular Admin Roles for Team management

-   **Version 6.0.3 - August 2025**
    -   Key Features:
        -   Coral Theme uptake for Teams module in WFO
        -   Latest uptake of the Shift planning changes, that includes location support for personal events.
-   **Version 5.1.1 - May 2025**

    Coral theme support for the Team Management Module in FSM WFO.

-   **Version 4.0.1 - August 2024**

    Accessibility improvements to adhere to WCAG 2.1AA conformant.

-   **Version 3.0.1 - February 2024**

    No new features were added in this release; it is an uptake of the latest platform WFO features.

-   **Version 2.0.5 - August 2023**

    Use Workforce Optimization for Field Service to manage agent schedules and breaks.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - February 2023**

    Team Management enables Managers to monitor and measure the performance goals, schedules, tasks and other activities for the groups they manage. Field Service managers can drive engagement and efficiency, ensuring their respective groups are operating at peak performance and delivering positive customer outcomes.
