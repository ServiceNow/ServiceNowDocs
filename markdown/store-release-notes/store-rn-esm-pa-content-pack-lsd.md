# Performance Analytics Content Pack for Legal Service Delivery release notes

Version history for the Performance Analytics Content Pack for Legal Service Delivery application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.7.0 - December 2025**

    Changed: Language pack related enhancements.

-   **Version 2.6.0 - February 2025**

    Fixed: In Legal Operations dashboard, the widget footer text is truncated in the Open Requests, Unassigned Requests, Open Matters, and Unassigned Matters widgets. This has been fixed.

-   **Version 2.5.0 - November 2024**

    Fixed: In the Legal Executive dashboard, the Open requests by age report displayed an incorrect count as it included closed requests. This is now fixed and closed requests are excluded.

-   **Version 2.4.0 - May 2024**
    -   Fixed:
        -   In Legal Executive Dashboard, the Open contract requests by contract values was incorrectly displaying information for NDA contracts even when it doesn't capture contract value.
        -   In Legal Executive Dashboard, % requests responded within SLA \(YTD\) widget available in Privacy and Compliance section wasn't showing any information.
-   **Version 2.2.0 - November 2023**
    -   Changed:
        -   The Legal Operations and Legal Executive Dashboard moved to the next experience from the classic experience.
        -   Legal Executive Dashboard
            -   Contracts tab has been updated and it will be available when the ServiceNow Legal contracts plugin has been installed.
            -   The Contracts\(Legacy\) tab will be available when the Legal Simple Contracts plugin has been installed.
            -   The contracts tab will show the insights for both - ServiceNow Legal Contracts and Legal Simple Contracts\(Total contract requests YTD, Contract requests by contract type and Contracts completion time\) when both the plugins are installed.
-   **Version 2.1.0 - August 2023**

    New: New reports added for the ServiceNow Legal Contracts application.

-   **Version 2.0.0 - February 2023**
    -   New: Added a new Performance Analytics Indicator called Third Party Contract Review for legal requests for third-party contract reviews. The report widgets in the Legal Executive dashboard are updated to include this new PA Indicator.
    -   Changed: Existing practice area reports were updated.
-   **Version 1.1.0 - November 2022**
    -   New: Added new Performance Analytics Indicators for legal requests and legal matters data related to ethics complaints.
    -   Changed: Updated report widgets in the Legal Executive dashboard to include the new PA Indicators for legal requests and legal matters data related to ethics complaints.
-   **Version 1.0.5 - March 2022**

    Changed: Renamed Contract requests by category \(YTD\) report to Contract requests by subcategory \(YTD\)

-   **Version 1.0.3 - February 2022**

    Gain insight, analyze trends and drill down into the details of legal operations from the Legal Executive dashboard. The application uses ServiceNow Performance Analytics and is included with a full Legal Service Delivery license.
