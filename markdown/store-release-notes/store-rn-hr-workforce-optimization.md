# Workforce Optimization for HR release notes

Version history for the Workforce Optimization for HR application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.2 - August 2025**

    No updates.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - August 2024**

    Fixed: Minor bugs and accessibility issues.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - March 2024**

    Fixed: The Wfo manager should have team performance manager role.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - August 2023**
    -   Fixed:
        -   WFO Landing Page Approvals Widget Localisation Issue
        -   The approval cards on the landing page of HR Manager Workspace not displaying date in the Safari browser
        -   Proper Alignments and Sizing for few widgets \(P1, SLA breached, Unassigned and SLA at risk\)
        -   Changed the filter used for landing page widgets \(to 'My WFO Group' from 'Groups that I manage' \)
        -   Dark theme cosmetic issues in HR Manager WS homepage
-   **Version 1.0.0 - February 2023**
    -   Workforce Optimization for HR is a product that simplifies the daily routine of HR agent managers to efficiently manage their global shared services team's productivity, schedules and respond to adhoc changes in real time, all from a single place.
    -   Its benefits include:
        -   Boost team productivity and gain real-time visibility into work items, escalations, and KPIs by each channel so you can manage for optimal performance.
        -   Break cross-team silos and manage normal work shifts and on-call shifts from one, centralized experience. Track shift coverage, swaps, and time off requests.
        -   Forecast gent demand with precision by modeling demand scenarios to analyze impact to agent schedules, and update forecast to properly staff for demand surges.
        -   Empower your agents with the skills they need to succeed using internal and external learning management platforms.
