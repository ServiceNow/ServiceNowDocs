# Contracts and Entitlement Workflows release notes

Version history for the Contracts and Entitlement Workflows application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 14.4.2 - June 2026**

    Enhancement: Enables users to process early renewals and early terminations on contract lines that have ramps.

-   **Version 14.2.0 - May 2026**

    Defect Fixes related to contract consolidation, amendments and renewals

-   **Version 14.1.0 - April 2026**

    Change: Ability to run renewal workflow in asynchronous manner when the number of lines being renewed crosses a threshold number.

-   **Version 14.0.0 - March 2026**
    -   New:
        -   Consolidation of upsells and downsells into a renewal opportunity and quote.
        -   Ability to consolidate two or more contracts during their renewal cycle.
        -   View ramp details directly from contract line list view for subscriptions with associated quantity or price ramps.
        -   Contract lines reflect dynamically calculated prices and contributing line items for derived price products.
-   **Version 13.0.0 - December 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Support for auto generating renewal opportunity and quote at different times
        -   Auto syncing of new lines on contract to existing renewal opportunity and quote
        -   Early renewal workflow to automate termination of existing customer contract and renewing before the standard renewal date
        -   Modify workflow enhancements to support calculation of upsell/down-sell amounts by capturing effective date for quantity modifications, and updating dates on subscriptions accordingly
        -   Enhancements to support renewal of subscriptions with price and quantity ramps
-   **Version 11.0.0 - August 2025 \(Yokohama, Zurich\)**
    -   New:
        -   Upsell and Downsell existing subscriptions on a contract
        -   Contract expansion by adding new subscriptions to a contract
        -   Modify entire contract or multiple contract lines in a single transaction
        -   Renew product inventory-based contracts
-   **Version 10.0.0 - August 2025 \(Xanadu\)**
    -   New:
        -   Upsell and downgrade existing subscriptions on a contract.
        -   Contract expansion by adding new subscriptions to a contract.
        -   Modify entire contract or multiple contract lines in a single transaction.
        -   Renew product inventory-based contracts.
-   **Version 9.0.0 - May 2025 \(Yokohama\)**
    -   New:
        -   Create renewal opportunity from contracts and entitlements
        -   Generate renewal transactions automatically by configuring auto-renewal
        -   Capture factors to calculate renewal markup or markdown
        -   Expand \(add new line items\) contracts as part of amendments
-   **Version 8.0.0 - May 2025 \(Washington DC, Xanadu\)**
    -   New:
        -   Create renewal opportunity from contracts and entitlements
        -   Generate renewal transactions automatically by configuring auto-renewal
        -   Capture factors to calculate renewal markup or markdown
        -   Expand \(add new line items\) contracts as part of amendments
-   **Version 7.0.0 - February 2025**
    -   New:
        -   Create contracts and entitlements for product inventory based flows.
        -   Manage lifecycle of product inventory based contracts and entitlements using Modify, Cancel, Suspend and Resume workflows.
-   **Version 4.0.1 - November 2024**
    -   New:
        -   Create single contract from an order with multiple lines.
        -   Add new lines to an existing contract.
        -   Create quotes when modifying or renewing a service contract.
-   **Version 3.0.1 - August 2024**

    New: Includes workflow updates to manage covered products on contracts.

-   **Version 2.0.2 - May 2024**
    -   New:
        -   New workflows to cancel or suspend/resume service contracts
        -   OOB renewal workflow to capture renewal orders
        -   Create contracts for subscription products
    -   Changed: Contract line item structure updated to reflect product offer structure
-   **Version 1.0.1 - February 2024**

    This application extends the catalog management and order management capabilities to support customer service teams in managing service contracts and entitlements.
