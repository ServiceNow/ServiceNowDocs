# Employee Experience Pack: Getting Married release notes

Version history for the Employee Experience Pack: Getting Married on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 2.1.0 - September 2022**
    -   Please note that version 2.1.x of this Experience Pack is officially compatible with the San Diego release only.
    -   These update sets contain knowledge templates and a Lifecycle Event modeled after common implementation practices.
    -   Included content:
        -   Lifecycle Event consists of three \(3\) Activity Sets:
            -   15 Activities
                -   1 Catalog Item
                -   2 Checklist
                -   1 HR Service
                -   5 Mark When Complete
                -   2 Notification
                -   1 Take Survey task
                -   2 Knowledge Articles
-   **Version 1.0.0 - March 2021**
    -   New:
        -   Included content:
            -   Lifecycle Event consisting of three \(3\) Activity Sets
            -   15 Activities
                -   1 Catalog Item
                -   2 Checklist
                -   1 HR Service
                -   5 Mark When Complete
                -   2 Notification
                -   1 Take Survey task
                -   2 Knowledge Articles
