# Business Continuity Management Foundation release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® Business Continuity Management Foundation application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.1.3 - June 2026 \(Australia\)**
    1.  AI Agents and skills
    2.  Business Continuity management features for impact analysis, business continuity plans, and crisis management.

-   **Version 1.0.3 - April 2026**

    Business Continuity Management Foundation helps organizations meet their business continuity and resilience objectives by identifying critical processes, assessing the impact of disruptions, and building, testing, and executing recovery plans to remain compliant and operative. This AI-enabled offering, delivers core business continuity management with AI-powered platform capabilities natively built in. It is ideal for organizations establishing their BCM program and looking for quick time-to-value.
