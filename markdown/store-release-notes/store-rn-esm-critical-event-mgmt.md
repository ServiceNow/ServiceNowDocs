# Critical Event Management release notes

Version history for the Critical Event Management application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.3 - June 2026**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Fixed an issue where the Health and Safety workspace failed to load impacted people when adding new impacted people to a critical event
        -   Fixed non-standard search popups in forms that had accessibility issues
        -   Fixed missing dialog titles for the Manage Recipients and New Communications popup dialogs
        -   Improved accessibility for the Notification Center component
        -   Fixed an issue where the critical event record number was being skipped due to duplicate form controllers on the Critical Event record
-   **Version 1.2.0 - December 2025**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Issues with loading impacted people
        -   Issue with read only fields
-   **Version 1.1.0 - October 2025**
    -   New: System property to disable the CEM playbook.
    -   Fixed: Various performance improvements.
-   **Version 1.0.3 - August 2025**

    Fixed: ACL mismatch between application and documentation

-   **Version 1.0.1 - May 2025**
    -   Fixed:
        -   CEM Knowledge base update related to Workflow migration to Zurich
        -   Two playbook steps refactored to correct minor issue
        -   Accessibility issue fixed related to single score widget
        -   UI adjustment for dark theme
-   **Version 1.0.0 - November 2024**
    -   ServiceNow Critical Event Management will be integrated into the Health and Safety Workspace, enabling teams to capture, triage, and manage emergencies that could affect employee well-being at the workplace. With Critical Event Management, companies are equipped with the essential tools to communicate effectively with employees during emergencies and provide timely assistance to those in need.
    -   Employees can quickly report potential emergencies to safety teams using Employee Center and Now Mobile, offering intuitive submission tools. Additionally, employees have access to a rich library of self-service capabilities designed to enhance workplace safety and provide peace of mind.
    -   On-ground responders are empowered with a streamlined digital experience to help them manage emergencies efficiently. They can conduct roll calls at muster points to ensure the safety and accountability of employees impacted by an emergency.
