# Legal Digital Forensics release notes

Version history for the Legal Digital Forensics on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 3.12.1 - June 2026**

    Changed: Security enhancements.

-   **Version 3.11.0 - December 2025**

    Changed: Security enhancements.

-   **Version 3.10.1 - May 2025**
    -   Changed:
        -   Improved Legal Digital Forensics icon for improved space utilization in the intake form.
        -   The "Create new" button has been changed to "Create new action"
    -   Fixed: The approvers are not displayed in Approvers tab when the Digital Forensics request is assigned. This has been fixed.
-   **Version 3.9.0 - February 2025**

    Fixed: Security issues.

-   **Version 3.7.0 - November 2024**

    Fixed: In the Legal Counsel Center, for a Digital Forensics related request, the Remove button incorrectly appeared twice under the Legal Matters related list. This is now fixed.

-   **Version 3.6.2 - August 2024**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Security fixes
        -   Fixed RCA issues observed in Legal Counsel Center, that now allows fulfiller to add information for Associated Allegations, Non Custodial Data and Data Source Details on digital forensic request.
-   **Version 3.5.4 - May 2024**
    -   Fixed:
        -   Requesters of the Digital Forensics request were displayed approval records belonging to other reqeuests.
        -   When cancelling a Digital Forensics related request, the application cancels any open approval requests as well. However, approval requests belonging to other requests were also incorrectly cancelled. This is now fixed.
        -   Security fixes.
-   **Version 3.5.0 - November 2023**

    Fixed: The root cause analysis issues for ethics and Digital Forensics while creating an allegation and saving the involved party, saving the custodian, or the search source.

-   **Version 3.4.2 - August 2023**

    Fixed: Couple of issues on bulk upload of custodians.

-   **Version 3.3.1 - February 2023**
    -   Changed: Added Legal Practice Apps core dependency.
    -   Fixed: Fixed an issue of not displaying a pop-up message in the platform while closing the matter but a prompt is displayed while closing the matter from workspace in DFT.
-   **Version 3.2.1 - November 2022**

    New: Added Task Configurations based on the new Approval Hub in the Employee Center to include approvals for custodial and non-custodial data associated with legal digital forensics requests.

-   **Version 3.1.0 - August 2022**

    Fixed: Performance improvements with minor fixes.

-   **Version 2.7.3 - May 2022**
    -   New: New list "Existing matter" added to the Digital Forensics Request intake form that lists existing legal matters with which the digital forensics request can be associated.
    -   Changed:
        -   Digital Forensics Request intake form label changes:
            -   "Matter" renamed to "Add this request to" and options changed from "New" to "New matter" and "Existing" to "Existing matter"
            -   "New matter" field renamed to "Name of new matter"
-   **Version 2.6.3 - December 2021**
    -   New:
        -   Added new email notifications for various Digital Forensics requests actions
        -   Added the Short description column in the Details tab list of Data source.
    -   Removed: New and Edit buttons have been removed from the Approvers list in a Digital Forensics matter.
    -   Fixed: Fixed the error message that showed up when the legal fulfiller tried to open a DFT matter.
-   **Version 2.5.3 - October 2021**

    Fixes.

-   **Version 2.5.2 - September 2021**

    New: Request legal services related to digital forensics from Employee Center portal.

-   **Version 2.4.2 - June 2021**
    -   New:
        -   Reduce spreadsheet tracking for eDiscovery related requests with predefined digital forensic tasks templates
        -   Streamline process for tracking additional queries against custodians​
        -   Simplify process to mark enterprise systems as forensics search sources​
