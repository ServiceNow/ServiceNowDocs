# APO - Prime release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® APO - Prime application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.0 - June 2026**

    Updated app/plugin dependencies.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - May 2026**

    APO - Prime is the highest-tier AI subscription for accounts payable operations on the ServiceNow AI Platform. Extending APO - Foundation, this offering unlocks Platform Prime features for enterprises that require the most advanced AI capabilities across their accounts payable workflows. APO - Prime includes everything in APO - Foundation and adds access to Now Assist Platform Prime, enabling organizations to take full advantage of the latest platform-level AI innovations as they become available.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - April 2026**

    The ServiceNow Accounts Payable Operations Prime application delivers a comprehensive suite of AI-powered skills and agents that transform how AP users handle invoices, exceptions, and inquiries. From intelligent data extraction and automated PO matching to agentic workflows that resolve exceptions and route ownership — Prime accelerates end-to-end AP operations while keeping humans in control at every step.
