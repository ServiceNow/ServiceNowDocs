# SPO - Foundation release notes

Version history for the ServiceNow® SPO - Foundation application on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.2.0 - June 2026**

    Changed: Updated application and plugin dependencies to ensure correct installation sequencing and compatibility.

-   **Version 1.1.0 - May 2026**

    SPO - Foundation is the foundation-tier AI subscription for supplier lifecycle operations on the ServiceNow AI Platform. Extending Now Assist for SPO, this offering unlocks Platform Foundation features for enterprises that require the most advanced AI capabilities across their supplier workflows. SPO - Foundation includes foundational skills and adds access to Now Assist Platform Foundation, enabling organizations to take advantage of the latest platform-level AI innovations as they become available.

-   **Version 1.0.0 - April 2026**

    The ServiceNow Sourcing and Procurement Operations Foundation application equips procurement specialists with assistive Now Assist skills embedded directly in their requisition and case management workflows. From summarizing procurement cases to drafting email responses, these AI-powered skills reduce manual effort and help specialists resolve issues faster — while keeping humans in control at every step.
