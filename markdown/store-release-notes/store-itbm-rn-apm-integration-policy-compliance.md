# Application Portfolio Management integration with Policy and Compliance release notes

Version history for the IT Business Management Application Portfolio Management integration with Policy and Compliance on the ServiceNow Store.

**Important:** For details on system requirements and family compatibility, view the application listing on the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) website.

## Version history

-   **Version 1.0.3 - May 2023**

    Patch release without any functional change.

-   **Version 1.0.2 - December 2020**

    Fixed: Bug fixes.

-   **Version 1.0.1 - October 2020**
    -   This app integrates APM with the GRC: Policy and Compliance Management app published by the GRC \(Risk\) product of ServiceNow. With this integration, the application owner will be able to view the following on the Business Application record:
        -   Controls
        -   Compliance status of the controls
        -   Issues \(resulting from non-compliant controls\)
        -   Tasks \(that need to be completed to close the issues and achieve compliance with controls\)
