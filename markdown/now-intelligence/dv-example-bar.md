# Bar visualization examples

Bar visualizations enable you to show the comparative size or frequency of different categories or groups, for example, sales in different regions or over different periods of time.

## Horizontal bar use case

Use the horizontal bar to show data by named values, such as departments, agencies, or regions.

## Vertical bar use case

Use vertical bar to show numbered values, such as age or monetary ranges, or time periods.
