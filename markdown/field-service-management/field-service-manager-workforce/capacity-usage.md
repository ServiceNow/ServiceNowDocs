# View Capacity Usages information

View Capacity Usages information to track the capacity used by groups and agents.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_admin, wm\_manager, and wm\_internal\_contractor\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Field Service** &gt; **Capacity Management** &gt; **Capacity Usages**.

2.  The Capacity Usages page provides information about capacity usage.
