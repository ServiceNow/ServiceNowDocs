# Smart Assessment Questionnaires

Smart assessment questionnaires enable field service technicians to capture accurate and task-critical information during work execution.

The questionnaires are designed to automate, standardize, and enforce structured data collection and validation for work orders and work order tasks.

-   Smart assessment questionnaires are used to collect task-specific information such as safety checks, inspection details, compliance confirmations, and work completion checklists from field service agents. Additionally, these questionnaires can be configured as mandatory, ensuring a work order or work order task is not closed until all its relevant details are captured by the agent.
-   The questionnaires help in verifying if the field service agent has completed all the required steps and complied with organizational, safety, and regulatory requirements of the work order.
-   Agents can complete assessments online or offline while executing tasks in fields, ensuring data is captured at the point of work.
-   Smart assessment questionnaires use reusable templates that standardize the process of collecting information across different work order tasks, task types, and operational domains.
