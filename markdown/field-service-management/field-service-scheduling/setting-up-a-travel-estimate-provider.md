# Setting up a travel estimate provider

While configuring the scheduling attributes, set up a travel estimate provider for each of your groups to streamline and enhance the travel planning process.

Efficient allocation of work order tasks to agents depends on accurate estimation of travel time, taking into account both the agent's location and the task's location. Schedule Optimization supports two options to calculate distances between agents and task, Straight-line and Beans.AI. An administrator is required to select a travel estimate provider for each group. See, [Create a scheduling attribute for Schedule Optimization](configure-scheduling-attributes.md) for more information on where the options are located.
