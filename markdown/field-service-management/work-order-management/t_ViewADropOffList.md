# View a drop off list

Agents can view a consolidated list, grouped by stockroom, of all items that have been added to drop off lists by all agents.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_agent

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Field Service** &gt; **Pick Up/Drop Off** &gt; **All Drop Off Lists**.

2.  Expand a group by clicking the arrow beside the group name.
