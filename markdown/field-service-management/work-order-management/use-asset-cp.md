# Record the assets used during a work task on the Contractor Portal

Record any assets that you use during the execution of a task.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_ext\_agent and wm\_ext\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Field Service Contractor Portal** &gt; **My Lists** &gt; **My Tasks**.

2.  Open a work order task.

3.  Click **New** in the Asset Usages related list.

4.  Record the used asset.

    1.  In the Asset Usage form, select **Used** from the **Status** list.

    2.  Select the asset from the Asset list.

5.  Click **Save**.


## Result

The asset you used is successfully recorded in the agent personal stockroom.
