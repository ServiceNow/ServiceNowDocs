# SLA map symbols

Icons in the SLA map and what they represent.

|Icon|Description|
|----|-----------|
|![Green truck icon.](../image/GreenTruck.png)|Current location of an agent selected in the filter. Agents identified with this icon are on time or ahead of schedule.|
|![Blue truck icon.](../image/Agent.png)|Current location of an agent who is less than 30 minutes behind schedule.|
|![Yellow truck icon.](../image/YellowTruck.png)|Current location of an agent who is between 30 and 60 minutes behind schedule.|
|![Red truck icon.](../image/AgentBehind.png)|Current location of an agent who is at least one hour behind schedule.|
|![Gray truck icon.](../image/AgentOffSchedule.png)|Last known location of an agent who is not currently on the schedule or working on any tasks.|
|![Green marker.](../image/IconG.png)|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 0 to less than 25.|
|![Yellow marker.](../image/Icony.png)|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 25 to less than 50.|
|![Orange marker.](../image/Icono.png)|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 50 to less than 75.|
|![Red marker.](../image/IconR.png)|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 75 to 100.|
|![Red indicator with the number of tasks.](../image/MultiTask.png)|Multiple active tasks with SLAs, clustered by proximity.|
|![Blue indicator with the number of agents.](../image/MultiAgent.png)|Multiple agents, clustered by proximity.|
|![Purple indicator with the sum of agents and tasks.](../image/AgentTaskCombo.png)|Sum of agents and tasks with SLAs, clustered by proximity.|
