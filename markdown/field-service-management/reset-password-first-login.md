# Log in to the Field Service Contractor Portal for the first time

Click the link in the email you are sent after you are onboarded to change your password so you can log in for the first time.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to your email inbox.

2.  In the onboarding email message, click the **Reset a password** related link.

    The **Password Reset** dialog box displays.

3.  Enter your new password in the **New Password** field.

4.  Re-enter your new password in the **Retype Password** field.

5.  Click **Reset Password**.

    The **Field Service Contractor Portal** login page displays.

6.  Enter your user ID in the **User name** field.

7.  Enter your new password in the **Password** field.

8.  Click **Login**.
