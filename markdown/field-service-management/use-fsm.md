# Using Field Service Management

Use Field Service Management to manage work tasks performed on location. Match tasks to agents based on skills, geographic territory assignments, and available inventory. Collaborate between operations, field resources, and customers through mobile channels.

-   **[Managing work orders and work order tasks](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/managing-work-orders.md)**  
Create work orders, and work order tasks using templates that integrate the right skills and scheduling.
-   **[Managing inventory in Field Service Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/sourcing-parts.md)**  
Get the parts you need to complete the tasks.
-   **[Managing work order tasks from the Customer or Consumer Service Portal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/use-customer-experience.md)**  
Use Field Service Management Customer Experience to send notifications to your customers through email and SMS, such as status of their work order tasks, agent's current location, and provide agent feedback.
-   **[Managing appointments in Field Service Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/managing-appointments-fsm.md)**  
Book, reschedule, and cancel appointments for available services.
-   **[Scheduling and dispatching work order tasks to agents](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/scheduling-and-dispatching-agents.md)**  
Send agents to work on the tasks.
-   **[Assigning equipment to a work order task](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/assign-equipment-to-wot.md)**  
Assigning equipment to a work order task ensures that crews or assigned agents are properly equipped for the task.
-   **[Use Emergency Exposure Management for Field Service agent monitoring and assignments](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/use-exposure-management-fsm.md)**  
Monitor the spread of infectious diseases by using Emergency Exposure Management to identify affected agents. You can also find agents and customers who might have been potentially exposed. You can view the compliance reports of agents and tasks.
-   **[Field Service Contractor Portal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/field-service-contractor-portal.md)**  
Use Field Service Contractor Portal to manage work order tasks.
-   **[View Capacity Usages information](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/capacity-usage.md)**  
View Capacity Usages information to track the capacity used by groups and agents.
-   **[Field Service with Service Locations](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/field-service-with-service-locations.md)**  
The Field Service with Service Locations feature enables you to add and verify service locations on demand.
-   **[Managing work orders for the planned work](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/creating-work-orders-from-planned-work.md)**  
Planned work records enable the scheduler to create future, everyday \(on regular basis\), or on-demand work orders based on the associated work schedule.
-   **[Managing work order tasks using the Field Service Contractor for mobile application](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/wot-fsc.md)**  
Managers and agents of contractor companies can use the Field Service Contractor for mobile application to view and manage the work order tasks assigned to them.
-   **[Managing Workforce Optimization for Field Service](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/using-wfo-fsm.md)**  
Enhance the quality and efficiency of your teams using smart scheduling, team structure, and real-time refreshing single scores. Assess the quality of tasks completed, recommend skills for agents, and train them to address skill gaps.
-   **[Managing linear assets in Field Service Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/managing-linear-assets-fsm.md)**  
In Field Service Management, you can create and view work orders and work order tasks specifically for the inspection of linear assets. This enables you to systematically assess the condition of the assets and take the necessary measures to maintain the reliability and performance of the linear assets.
-   **[Use the Now Mobile Agent application to perform tasks](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/field-service-management/Use-mobile-app-fsm.md)**  
Field service agents and managers use the mobile application to manage their field service tasks from anywhere.
