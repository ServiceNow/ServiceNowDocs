# Configuring Guided Tours

Guided tours configurations let you enable tours, configure tours to automatically launch, and customize the tour experience.

## Configuration overview

Guided tours offers multiple configurations for a customized experience of your tours. You can configure tours to automatically launch on the instance start-up, and configure triggers to display the next steps during your tour.
