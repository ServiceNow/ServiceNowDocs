# Using Manufacturing Commercial Operations

Learn how Manufacturing Commercial Operations enables you to manage the end-to-end lifecycle of your products, subscriptions, and services.

Using Service Exchange with Manufacturing Commercial Operations, Manufacturers can connect within the company across multiple divisions, with B2B customers, with suppliers, with channel partners, or with third party logistics partners \(3PLs\).
