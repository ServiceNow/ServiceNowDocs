# Manufacturing with Sales Customer Relationship Management

Optimize the lead-to-cash cycle with Sales Customer Relationship Management for Manufacturing Commercial Operations.

With Sales Customer Relationship Management for Manufacturing Commercial Operations, manufacturers can:

-   Launch complex products and services with configurable catalogs.
-   Manage leads &amp; opportunities from start to finish and map customer needs to best offers.
-   Configure and price quotes and convert to orders to speed up revenue.
-   Automate order fulfillment across front, middle, and back office teams.
-   Manage post-sale changes, upgrades, and renewals.
