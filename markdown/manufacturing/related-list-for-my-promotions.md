# Related list for My promotions

In the workspace, you can use the My Promotions related list to track information that is related to the applicable product, visibility criteria, and sales promotion.

|Related list|Description|
|------------|-----------|
|[Create an applicable product](mco-create-applicable-prod.md)|Create an applicable product.|
|[Create a visibility criteria](mco-create-visibility-criteria.md)|Create a visibility criteria.|
|[Create a sales promotion claim case](../task/mco-create-sls-promotion-cc.md)|Create a sales promotion claim case|

-   **[Create an applicable product](mco-create-applicable-prod.md)**  
Create an applicable product. An applicable product specifies the products to which the sales promotion is applied.
-   **[Create a visibility criteria](mco-create-visibility-criteria.md)**  
Create a visibility criteria so that a manufacturer can filter the dealer name for which the target sales promotion is applicable.
-   **[Create a sales promotion claim case](../task/mco-create-sls-promotion-cc.md)**  
Create a sales promotion claim case to claim the reimbursement or discount.
