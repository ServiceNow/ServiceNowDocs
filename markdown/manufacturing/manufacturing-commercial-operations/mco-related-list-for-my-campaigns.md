# Related lists for my campaigns

In the Manufacturing Commercial Operations workspace, you can use my campaign related list to track information that is related to the campaign tasks, corrective action, and recall phases.

|Related list|Description|
|------------|-----------|
|[Campaign tasks](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/yokohama/markdown/yokohama/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-campaign-tasks.md)|Plan, identify, and execute a recall promotion campaign.|
|[Corrective actions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/yokohama/markdown/yokohama/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-corrective-actions.md)|Action taken to rectify the issue raised for a sold product.|
|[Identify an impacted asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/yokohama/markdown/yokohama/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-impacted-asset.md)|Identify the asset impacted for a recall or repair action.|
|[Recall a campaign phase](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/yokohama/markdown/yokohama/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-recall-campaign-phases.md)|Recall campaigns created for a specific geography or for a dealership.|
