# Related list for a warranty contract

In the Manufacturing Commercial Operations application, you can use the Warranty contracts related list to track information that is related to the warranty contract hierarchy, cases, entitlements, and other entities.

|Related list|Description|
|------------|-----------|
|[Create an entitlement](../task/mco-create-entitlements.md)|List of entities, such as accounts, customers, or products.|
|[Create an asset](../task/mco-create-assets-covered.md)|List of assets associated with the warranty contract.|
|[Create terms and conditions for a contact](../task/mco-create-terms-and-conditions.md)|List of terms and conditions associated with the customer or product.|
|[Create a contact](../task/mco-create-contract-contact.md)|List of contact details.|
|[Create a case](../task/create-new-case.md)|List of cases raised for an account, customer, or product.|
