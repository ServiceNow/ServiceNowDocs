# My campaign phases

Recall campaign phase displays the claims assigned to the person who has logged in to the workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace** &gt; **Lists** &gt; **Recall Management** &gt; **My Campaign phases**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the Recall campaign phases form, fill in the fields.

    For a description of the field values, see [Recall campaign phases form](../reference/mco-recall-campaign-phases-form.md).

4.  Select **Save**.
