# Create terms and conditions for a contact

Create a term and conditions record to add to a contract.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace** &gt; **Lists** &gt; **MCO Setup** &gt; **Warranty Contracts** &gt; **Terms and Conditions**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields

<table id="table_hwk_fsz_rgb"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Contract

</td><td>

Link to the contract.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Order

</td><td>

Order id.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Terms and Conditions

</td><td>

One or more terms and conditions associated with the contact. Options include:-   Standard NDA
-   Government Law


</td></tr></tbody>
</table>4.  Select **Submit**.
