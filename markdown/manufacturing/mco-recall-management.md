# Recall management

Recall management enables the OEMs to identify and remove or replace the faulty products sold to the customers.

|Tasks|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|[My campaigns](mco-rc-my-campaigns.md)|Displays all the recall claims that are assigned to the person logged in to the workspace.|
|[All campaigns](mco-rc-all-campaigns.md)|Displays all the recall claims that are assigned to the OEM.|
|[My campaign phases](mco-rc-my-campaign-phases.md)|Displays all the recall campaign phases that are assigned to the person logged in to the workspace.|
|[All campaign phases](mco-rc-all-campaign-phases.md)|Displays all the recall campaign phases that are assigned to the OEM.|
