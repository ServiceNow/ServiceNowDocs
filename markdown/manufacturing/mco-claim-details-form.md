# Claim details form

The Claim details form enables the dealer to enter the claim details for repair work performed.

|Fields|Description|
|------|-----------|
|Asset|Asset or product on which repair action is performed.|
|Requested by|User who is raising the repair claim request.|
|Consumer|Customer information that is auto-populated based on the install base.|
|Description|Short description on the repair claim.|
|Dealer|Dealer who is raising the repair claim request.|
|Reported date|Date on which the claim is being raised.|
|Work order|String value.|
