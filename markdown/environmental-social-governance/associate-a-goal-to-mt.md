# Map a goal to a material topic

Map a goal to a material topic so that you can track the actions being taken for the chosen topic. Based on this mapping exercise, you can act on the goals with the most significant material impact.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_esg.program\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Environmental, Social, and Governance** &gt; **ESG Workspace** &gt; **Lists** &gt; **Program Setup** &gt; **Material topics**.

2.  Open the material topic to which you want to map a goal.

3.  To add a goal, do the following:

    1.  Click the Goals related list.

    2.  Click **Add**.

    3.  Select the goals to add.

    4.  Click **Add**.

    5.  Click **Save**.


## Result

The goal is mapped to the material topic. This goal can now track the progress toward your chosen theme or topic.
