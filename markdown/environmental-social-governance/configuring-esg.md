# Configuring ESG Management

As an administrator, use this section to set up the ESG Management application. You must first create your material topics, your goals and targets, emission activities, and emission factors. You can then create entities and entity types for which you want to report the metrics and measure the ESG performance of your organization.

-   **[ESG Management implementation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/esg-implementation.md)**  
Use the steps in the ServiceNow® ESG Management application checklist to download the ESG Management from the ServiceNow Store, and get it ready for operation. Mandatory and optional setup steps, as well as an implementation checklist are provided to simplify the setup.
-   **[Create a material topic](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/create-material-topics.md)**  
Create material topics for your organization so that you can identify the areas where you want to make an ESG impact.
-   **[Create an ESG goal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/create-esg-goal.md)**  
Establish environmental, social, and governance \(ESG\) goals to track your progress toward your chosen material topics.
-   **[Create an ESG target](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/create-esg-target.md)**  
Create an environmental, social, and social \(ESG\) target to help track the progress towards your ESG goal.
-   **[Set up the emission factor library](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/set-up-the-emission-factor-library.md)**  
To calculate your emissions accurately, you must set up your emission factor library. The emission factor library consists of emission factors, emission activities, activity sources and factor locations.
-   **[Create a new schedule](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/create-a-new-schedule.md)**  
Create a schedule or modify an existing schedule to calculate the due date for metric data tasks.
-   **[Create related list groupings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/create-related-list-groupings.md)**  
Simplify the groupings of related lists on a record page and customize them to your specific needs, assigning meaningful names in the process. This configuration enhances readability and user experience when interacting with the forms.
-   **[Configure Sustainable IT](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/configure-sustainable-it.md)**  
Follow the order of the steps to configure the Sustainable IT plugin.
-   **[Activate or update a framework and install citations using the ESG content accelerator](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/install-citations-frameworks-from-esg-content-accelerator.md)**  
Activate or update your chosen framework and select the citations for the framework you've selected. This will install the framework and the selected citations and metric definitions on your instance.
-   **[Filter citations documents for ESG](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/filter-citations-and-authority-documents-for-esg.md)**  
Set the functional domain to **Environmental, Social, Governance** to view only those citations that are specific to the ESG domain. This ability to select the domain enables you to see only those citations that are relevant to you. The same procedure applies to authority documents as well.
-   **[Configuring the Scope 3 dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/configuring-the-scope-3-dashboard.md)**  
To view data on the Scope 3 dashboard, you must configure the emission category types to define the categories for which you want to report scope 3 data.
-   **[Manage entities in ESG Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/entities-in-esg.md)**  
You can learn about how ESG, which stands for Environmental, Social, and Governance Management \(ESG\) is used by the investors and reporting agencies to evaluate different entities that they want to invest in, such as different business units in an organization.
-   **[Monitoring assessment data using ESG Management dashboards](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/environmental-social-governance/monitor-data-using-esg-dashboards.md)**  
You can monitor and analyze assessment data at various levels in the Environmental, Social, and Governance Management application using the Analytics dashboard. The custom dashboards you create provide tailored insights and deliver relevant information at a glance, improving the decision-making process.
