# Integration with Sales and Order Management

Providers can use Service Exchange to publish their product offers to a consumer using a Service Exchange Remote Record Producer.

Consumers can order a connected provider’s Sales and Order Management product offering from service catalogs in their ServiceNow instances, enabling faster order fulfillment, improved accuracy, and improved customer satisfaction. For more details, see [Order Management for providers with Service Exchange](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=service-bridge-om-for-providers&version=australia&pubname=australia-order-management&ft:locale=en-US).
