# AI-driven regulatory alert summarization skill

The AI-driven summarization feature is designed to provide concise, AI-driven overviews of regulatory alerts directly within the regulatory alert record in the Compliance Workspace. It offers contextual information, including important dates, regulatory authority details, and key changes, helping users to better respond to new alerts.

## Overview of a summary

You can quickly learn the details of a regulatory alert from the record summarization generated by Now Assist summarization skill. By reading the regulatory alert summarization, you can get an overview of the alerts that are often high in volume. By default, regulatory alerts in the New state are eligible for summarization. You can, however, modify this behavior by defining custom states or conditions under which summarization should occur. You can also specify the different prompts and the fields from which you want to collect data to create the summary.

**Note:** Only users with the sn\_grc\_comp\_genai.reg\_change\_ai\_user role are able to see the option to create a summary for a regulatory alert. This role must be manually assigned to an RCM user.

To understand how you can summarize a regulatory alert, refer to [Activate the Regulatory alert summarization skill](../task/summarize-a-regulatory-alert.md) and [Generate a regulatory alert summary](../task/create-a-summary-of-a-reg-alert.md).

## Benefits of summarizing a regulatory alert

-   Distills regulatory information into business-relevant summaries that highlight the most important changes and deadlines.
-   Reduces time spent interpreting complex updates, ensuring no critical information is missed.
-   Improves compliance efficiency, reduces risk, and enhances operational agility.

## Components of a regulatory alert summary

After generating a summary for a regulatory alert in the New state, a summary displays the following sections:

-   Important dates
-   Regulatory authority
-   Summary of key changes
-   Penalties or fines
