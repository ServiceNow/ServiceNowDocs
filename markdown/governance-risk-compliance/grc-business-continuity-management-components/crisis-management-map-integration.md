# Crisis Management map

You can integrate the crisis management application with the Crisis Management map to receive alerts as the alert feeds on possible threats in continuing the business operations of your organization.

A threat feed is the data intelligence information of a potential or current threat that can pose a danger to an organization and stop its business routine or disable its business processes. Threat feeds can include but not limited to:

-   Severe weather or tornado
-   Hurricane
-   Earthquake
-   Wildfire or red flag warnings
-   Flood
-   Civil unrest or riots
-   War or military conflict

As a BCM administrator, you can enable or disable threat feed integrations provided by the base system. You can also configure additional threat feeds in to the Crisis Management application. The integration and configurations that you set up alert the crisis manager of the threats and aid to take appropriate action on time.

Before you integrate the crisis management map with the geographical locations of your assets, there are certain configurations that you must do to display the active alerts in the crisis map.
-   **[View and manage alerts in the crisis map interface](../task/crisis-map-interface-bcm.md)**  
Business Continuity Management integrates with the Crisis Map to provide you the capability of identifying a threat and its geolocation from your asset locations.
-   **[Set controls to customize alerts on the map dynamically](../task/control-crisis-map-bcm.md)**  
Use a set of controls on the crisis map to display the alerts that are critical to your asset locations.
-   **[View resource layer clustering](../task/view-reource-layer-clustering.md)**  
View your assets or resources on the map.
-   **[View assets at risk within the impacted area](../task/assets-risk-crisis-map.md)**  
View the list of resources that are at stake because of an alert threat that is near your business locations. Take actions to protect your resources and prevent major loss.
-   **[Notify stakeholders and initiate response workflows](../task/notify-stakeholders-initiate-crisis-response-flows.md)**  
Notify the stakeholders and initiate multiple workflows for the recovery teams across your organization and help them respond to the threat and take necessary actions.
