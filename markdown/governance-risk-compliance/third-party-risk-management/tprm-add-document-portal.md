# Upload and manage documents in the third-party portal

Use the third-party portal to upload and manage documents for assessments, engagements, issues, and tasks in Third-Party Risk Management \(TPRM\).

## Before you begin

Role required: Primary contacts can complete all actions while secondary contacts must be assigned read and write access.

## About this task

Third parties and engagement owners can upload and manage documents directly in the portal. Uploaded documents can include contracts, policies, or compliance evidence and can be linked to assessments, engagements, and related tasks.

## Procedure

1.  Log in to the Third-party portal at `https://<myCompany>.service-now.com/svdp`.

2.  Select the **Documents** tab, and then select **Create document** to upload a document.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    For descriptions of all these fields, see [Create new document form](../reference/tprm-create-new-document-form.md).

4.  Attach the document file.

5.  Select **Submit** to save the document record.

    If the file upload fails, check the file size and format restrictions and retry. If permissions prevent upload, request access from the engagement owner.

6.  When managing documents, you have the following actions available.
