# TPRM Risk activity page

The Risk activity page enables you to quickly identify assessments, issues, and tasks that need attention.

## Accessing the page

To view the Risk activity page, select **Workspaces** &gt; **Vendor Management Workspace** and on the **Risk** tab, select the risk activity icon ![](../image/icon-tprm-ws-risk-activity.png).

![Risk activity page.](../image/tprm-ws-risk-activity-page.png)

## Working on the page

-   **Work queue**

    Select any number in a box to open the associated list of assessments, issues, or tasks.

-   **Activity overview**

    Select any bar on a chart to open the associated list of assessments, issues, or tasks.

-   **Expiring third-party risk assessments.**

    The section lists risk assessment due diligence requests that will expire soon.

    For each external risk assessment, the system auto-assigns a unique ID number that starts with the text VRA. Select a VRA number to work on the risk assessment on the **External assessments** tab.
