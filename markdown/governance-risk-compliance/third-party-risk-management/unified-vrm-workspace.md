# Viewing third-party risk reports

Use the **Risk** tab on the Vendor Management Workspace to access and prioritize activities such as managing your portfolio of third parties, assessing third-party risk, and completing the remediation life cycle. The workspace enables automation that reduces the manual burden and costs of risk assessment.

## Navigating the Vendor Management Workspace

Select **Workspaces** &gt; **Vendor Management Workspace** and then select the **Risk** tab to open the workspace to the home page. Each icon in the navigation panel opens a specialized page.

![Accessing the Vendor Management Workspace.](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/tprm-ws-risk-tab-simplified.png)

-   **![](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/icon-tprm-ws-home.png) Home page**

    The home page displays reports of important risk information and provides quick access to actions for TPR managers and TPR assessors. See [TPRM Home page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-home-page.md).

-   **![](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/icon-tprm-ws-dd-mgt.png) Due diligence management page**

    TPR managers and assessors use the due diligence management reports to track, prioritize, and manage their responsibilities. See [TPRM Due diligence management reports](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-dd-management-reports.md).

-   **![](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/icon-tprm-ws-risk-activity.png) Risk activity page**

    The Risk activity page enables you to quickly identify assessments, issues, and tasks that need attention. See [TPRM Risk activity page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-risk-activity-page.md).

-   **![](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/icon-tprm-ws-dashboard.png) Dashboards page**

    The Dashboards page shows the Third-party insights dashboard and TPRM custom analytics dashboard. You can use these dashboards to provide tailored insights, improve decision-making, and create dashboards to meet any additional requirements. For more information, see [TPRM Dashboards page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-dd-dashboards.md) and [Monitoring assessment data using TPRM dashboards](tprm-monitor-dashboards.md).

-   **![](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/icon-tprm-ws-risk-map.png) Risk concentration map page**

    The Risk concentration map page pinpoints the geographical locations of active third parties and engagements. You can configure filters to view particular risk ratings and engagement types. See [TPRM Risk concentration map](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-risk-concentration-map.md).

-   **![](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/icon-tprm-ws-tasks.png) Task page**

    The task page gives you access to tasks that are assigned to you and to members of your group. You can further filter the lists of tasks by due diligence requests, type of third-party action, and by risk or tiering assessment. See [TPRM Task page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-task-page.md).

-   **![](../../grc-workspace-vrm/image/icon-tprm-ws-list.png) List page**

    The List page is a general-purpose page that enables access to all items that you can view or act on in TPRM. See [TPRM List page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-list-page.md).


## Who can use the Vendor Management Workspace

The Vendor Management Workspace is designed for users with the following roles:

-   TPR manager \(Third-party risk manager\) \[sn\_vdr\_risk\_asmt.vendor\_risk\_manager\]
-   TPR assessor \(Third-party risk assessor\) \[sn\_vdr\_risk\_asmt.vendor\_assessor\]
-   Third-party assessment reviewer \[sn\_vdr\_risk\_asmt.vendor\_assessment\_reviewer\]

-   **[TPRM Home page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-home-page.md)**  
The home page displays reports of important risk information and provides quick access to actions for TPR managers and TPR assessors.
-   **[TPRM Due diligence management reports](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-dd-management-reports.md)**  
TPR managers and assessors use the due diligence management reports to track, prioritize, and manage their responsibilities.
-   **[TPRM Risk activity page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-risk-activity-page.md)**  
The Risk activity page enables you to quickly identify assessments, issues, and tasks that need attention.
-   **[TPRM Dashboards page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-dd-dashboards.md)**  
The dashboards page displays the Third-party insights dashboard and TPRM custom analytics dashboard as well as any other dashboards you and your team create.
-   **[TPRM Risk concentration map](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-risk-concentration-map.md)**  
The Risk concentration map page pinpoints the geographical locations of active third parties and engagements. You can configure filters to view particular risk ratings and engagement types.
-   **[TPRM Task page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-task-page.md)**  
The task page gives you access to tasks that are assigned to you and to members of your group. You can further filter the lists of tasks by due diligence requests, type of third-party action, and by risk or tiering assessment.
-   **[TPRM List page](../../grc-workspace-vrm/concept/tprm-ws-list-page.md)**  
The List page is a general-purpose page that enables access to all items that you can view or act on in TPRM.
