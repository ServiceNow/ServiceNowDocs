# Verify AI Risk and Compliance Content

Verify if the AI Risk and Compliance content is installed correctly.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Plugins**.

2.  In the Application Manager, search for AI Risk and Compliance.

3.  In the Installed related list, make sure that you see AI Risk and Compliance Content and AI Risk and Compliance Management.

4.  Go to **Workspaces** &gt; **Assessment Workspace**.

5.  In the Assessment templates list, make sure that **Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment for High-Risk AI Systems \(FRIA\)** and **EU AI Act Conformity Assessment** templates appear with the published state as false.


## Result

The AI Risk and Compliance Content is installed and verified.
