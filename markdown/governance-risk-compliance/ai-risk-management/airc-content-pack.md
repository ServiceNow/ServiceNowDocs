# AI Risk and Compliance Content Pack

The ServiceNow AI Risk and Compliance Content Pack provides basic content that enables the risk and compliance managers to ensure that the organizations comply with the regulations and policies with respect to their AI systems.

## Risk Assessment Methodologies \(RAMs\)

The content pack offers the following RAMs, by default:

-   Risk classification for AI system.
-   Risk assessment for AI inventory.
-   Risk classification for AI Model or Dataset.

## Assessment templates

The following assessment templates are available by default when you download and install the content pack:

-   **AI systems**

    -   AI impact assessment.
    -   Lab AI Impact assessment.
    -   High-risk AI assessment questionnaire.
    -   AI impact assessment for EU AI Act conformity assessment.
    -   EU AI Act Conformity Assessment- available in Draft state starting with the Zurich release.
    -   Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment for High-Risk AI Systems \(FRIA\)- available in Draft state starting with the Zurich release.
    **Note:** You must publish the assessments in the Draft state before you can use them.

-   **AI models**

    AI Impact Assessment on AI asset inventory.

-   **AI cases**

    AI case assessment questionnaire.
