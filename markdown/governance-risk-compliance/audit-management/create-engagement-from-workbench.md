# Create an engagement from Audit Workbench

The Engagement Workbench provides a timeline view from which you can select an audit engagement to view details or create a new engagement. Audit managers create engagements directly from the Workbench to manage audit information and collect entities, controls, and control tests that are relevant to the audit.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_audit.admin or sn\_audit.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Audit** &gt; **Engagements** &gt; **Workbench**.

2.  Click **Create Engagement**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Name|Name of the engagement.|
    |Assigned to|User assigned to the engagement.|
    |Description|General description of the engagement.|
    |Objectives|Objectives of the engagement.|
    |Planned start date|Intended date the activity should begin.|
    |Planned duration|Expected duration of this activity. As with actual duration, the planned duration shows total activity time and takes the activity schedule into consideration.|
    |Audit period start|Date that work on the engagement is expected to begin.|
    |Audit period end|Date that work on the engagement is expected to end.|
    |Auditors|Auditors assigned to the engagement.|
    |Approvers|Approvers assigned to the engagement.|

4.  Click **Create**.
