# Respond to attestations on the Risk Portal

Respond to your attestations by logging in to the Risk Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_grc.business\_user, sn\_grc.business\_user\_lite

## About this task

As an attestation respondent of a control you can log in to the Risk Portal and respond to the attestations.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Policy and Compliance** &gt; **Risk Portal**.

2.  Select the new task in My pending tasks section of the Risk Portal.

    You can view the Attestations on the left panel.

3.  Select the assessment instance link on the right panel of the My pending tasks related list.

4.  Enter your response to the questions in the category.

5.  To attach an evidence, select the Add File link, select the document in the folder, and click **Open**.

6.  Select the **Upload** button.

7.  Select the **Next** button.

8.  Select the **Provide acknowledgement** option in the Submit page.

9.  Select the **Submit assessment** button.

10. Select **Close** in the Submission Successful pop-up.

    All the questions are read only now that you have submitted the response.
