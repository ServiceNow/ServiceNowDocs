# Acknowledge a policy using the Compliance Workspace

After a policy has been published, you can create an acknowledgement campaign in the Compliance Workspace to define a group of your employees who must provide an acknowledgement that a particular policy is in compliance. When the campaign has been defined, you can submit the request to the audience.
