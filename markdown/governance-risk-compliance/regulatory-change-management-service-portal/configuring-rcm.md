# Configuring your compliance needs and integration with regulatory content providers

Configure the Regulatory Change Management application to manage your compliance needs and integration with regulatory intelligence content providers.

A typical configuration of the Regulatory Change Management application consists of the following steps:

1.  Download the Regulatory Change Management application from ServiceNow Store. For more information, see [Download Regulatory Change Management application](https://servicenow.com/docs/bundle/xanadu-governance-risk-compliance/page/product/grc-rcm/task/activate-grc-rcm.html).
2.  Do the setup tasks for the Administration module. For more information, see [Manage Administration portal](https://servicenow.com/docs/bundle/xanadu-governance-risk-compliance/page/product/grc-rcm/concept/manage-admin-portal.html).
