# Add a risk event consequence to the consequence library

A consequences library is a centralized library of the possible consequences of a risk event.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_risk.manager and sn\_risk.admin

## About this task

Add a risk event consequence to the consequence library to help with risk analysis, risk prediction, and risk prevention.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk Events** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Consequences**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Name|Consequence name. For example, `Asset loss`.|
    |Parent|Parent of the consequence. The parent refers to the group under which the consequence is categorized. For example, for Asset loss, the parent consequence can be Damage to Physical Assets.|
    |Description|Brief description of the consequence.|

4.  Click **Submit**.


## Result

The risk event consequence is added to the library.
