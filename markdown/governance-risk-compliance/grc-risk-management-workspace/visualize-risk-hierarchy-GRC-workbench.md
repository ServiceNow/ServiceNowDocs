# Visualize risk hierarchies using the GRC: Workbench

Risk users can see the hierarchy of risk statements using the GRC: Workbench.

## Before you begin

Activate the GRC: Workbench \(app-grc-workbench\) plugin.

Role required: sn\_risk.reader

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk** &gt; **GRC Workbench**.

2.  Click **Risk Dependencies**.

3.  Click **Risk Statement Hierarchy**.

4.  On the left pane, click the risk statement for which you want to visualize the risk statement hierarchy.

    For example, if you want to see the hierarchy of the Information Technology risk statement, click Information Technology Risk and you can see the parent risk statement and the child risk statements such as information security, IT infrastructure, IT operations, and IT performance.

    The risk statement hierarchy is displayed.
