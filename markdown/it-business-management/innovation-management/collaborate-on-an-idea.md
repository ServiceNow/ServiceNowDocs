# Collaborate on ideas

Use the comment option to discuss ideas and exchange information about ideas. Up-vote or down-vote an idea to register your level of interest and support for an idea. Subscribe to an idea to track its progress.

## Before you begin

Role required: None

## About this task

You cannot comment and vote for ideas that are in the **Completed**, **Unlikely to implement**, **Duplicate**, or **Already exists** state. The Open \( \[Omitted image "open-idea.png"\] Alt text: Open for comments and votes icon\) or Closed \(\[Omitted image "closed-idea.png"\] Alt text: Closed for comments and votes icon\) icon indicates whether the idea is open or closed for comments and votes.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Ideas** &gt; **Idea Portal**.

2.  Select the title of an idea to view its details.

3.  Review the idea details and then choose which action to perform on the idea.

    For more information on actions and steps, see [Collaborate on idea](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/yokohama/markdown/yokohama/it-business-management/innovation-management/collaborate-on-idea-actions.md).
