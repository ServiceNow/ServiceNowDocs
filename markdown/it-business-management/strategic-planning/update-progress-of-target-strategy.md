# Update the progress of a manual target

Update the progress of a target when its status is changed.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_apw\_advanced.spw\_goal\_user

## About this task

**Note:** Only the owner or contributors of the goal can update a goal target.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Strategic Planning Workspace** &gt; **Strategy and Goals**.

2.  On the Strategy and Goals page, select the strategy-based board or goal-based board for which your target belongs to.

    The Strategy page opens if selected a strategy-based or combined strategy and goals-based board. The Goals page opens if selected a goals-based board.

3.  Update the target for your goal using one of the following methods depending on your board type.
