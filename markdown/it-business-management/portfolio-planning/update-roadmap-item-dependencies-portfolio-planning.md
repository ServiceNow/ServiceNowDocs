# Update roadmap item dependencies in Portfolio Planning

Update or delete the dependencies for the planning items on your roadmap so that they reflect any change of plans.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_align\_core.apw\_user

## About this task

Use the roadmap side panel to change the details of a dependency or delete it.

Dependencies for free-form roadmaps are available only if the roadmap's source table is Planning Item \[sn\_align\_core\_planning\_item\] or one of its extensions.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Portfolio Planning Workspace** &gt; **Portfolio Planning** and open your roadmap.
