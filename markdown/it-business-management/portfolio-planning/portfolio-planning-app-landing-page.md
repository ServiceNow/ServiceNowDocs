# Portfolio Planning

Enhance your traditional product and portfolio management by prioritizing work and visualizing plans with ServiceNow® Portfolio Planning \(formerly known as Alignment Planner Workspace\). Effectively plan, track, and manage work for projects and demands using a centralized workspace.

Portfolio Planning is available for users with a Strategic Portfolio Management \(SPM\) Standard license.
