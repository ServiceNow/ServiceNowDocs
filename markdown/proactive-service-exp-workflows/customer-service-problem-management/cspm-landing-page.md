# Customer Service Problem Management

Efficiently identify and resolve service problems experienced by the customers with the ServiceNow® Customer Service Problem Management \(CSPM\) application. Telecommunications, Media, and Technology \(TMT\) service providers can streamline their service management processes and ensure a seamless experience for their customers by leveraging this application.
