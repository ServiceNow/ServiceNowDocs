# Remove a member from a feed

The feed administrator can remove any member from the feed.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  Click either **Group Feeds** or **Record Feeds**, and then select the feed name.

3.  Click the **Members** tab.

4.  Locate the member you want to remove and click **Remove**.
