# Copy a survey

Create a copy of a survey with at least one category to reduce the effort of creating another survey with similar data.

## Before you begin

Role required: survey\_admin or admin, survey\_creator

**Note:** Users with the survey\_creator role can copy a survey only if they own the survey. If they do not own the survey, they cannot copy it.

## About this task

All associated questions \(type\), configurations, categories, metrics, domain separation rules, and role-based categories are copied. Assigned users, category users, instances, and trigger conditions are not copied.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Survey** &gt; **View Surveys**.

2.  Open a survey definition.

3.  Perform any of the following steps.
