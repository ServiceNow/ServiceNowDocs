# Check in a document

Check in the document after you revise it so other users can check out the document.

## Before you begin

Role required: document\_management\_admin

or

Role required: document\_management\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the document record.

2.  Select the **Upload/Check In Revision** related link.

3.  Click **Browse** and navigate to the revised file.

4.  Click **Open**.

5.  Check the Name and Revision Number.

6.  Click **OK**.
