# Activate Article Optimization skill

Activate the Knowledge Center Article Optimization skill to enable use of article optimization features for generating knowledge articles in the Knowledge Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Now Assist Admin** &gt; **Now Assist Skills**.

2.  Select the list view and locate your new skill in the location specified during the setup \(Platform &gt; Knowledge &gt; Skill Name\).

3.  Select **Activate Skill** on the skill card.


## Result

The Article Optimization skill is activated.
