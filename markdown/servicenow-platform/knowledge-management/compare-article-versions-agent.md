# Compare knowledge article versions in Agent Workspace

Select and compare two versions of a knowledge article in Agent Workspace to view the differences between them.

## Before you begin

You must have contribute access to the knowledge base that stores the knowledge article.

Role required: agent\_workspace\_user

**Note:** For comparison of the knowledge article versions in the Next Experience UI, see [Compare two versions of an article](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/servicenow-platform/knowledge-management/compare-two-article-versions.md).

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Agent Workspace** &gt; **Agent Workspace Home**.

2.  Click **Lists** &gt; **Knowledge** &gt; **All Articles**.

3.  Click the link to a knowledge article for which you want to compare two versions.

4.  Click **Edit** to open the Knowledge article form.

5.  In the Article Versions related list, select the two versions of the article to compare.

6.  Click **Compare**.


## Result

The **Compare versions** tab opens within Agent Workspace and lists the fields for the selected versions in a side-by-side format. Any differences between the two versions are highlighted as shown in the following example image.
