# Retail Core

The ServiceNow® application empowers managers and associates to manage daily retail operations by automating tasks and resolving issues quickly with self-service, freeing up time to serve customers. It enables seamless two-way communication and visibility between stores and HQ, delivering a great customer experience while enhancing staff productivity and reducing costs.
