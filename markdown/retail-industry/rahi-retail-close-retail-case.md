# Close a retail case

Once a retail case has been resolved within your retail organization, close the case.

## Before you begin

Role required: n\_retail.support\_agent or sn\_retail.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Retail Service Management** &gt; **Retail Cases** &gt; **All**.

2.  Select the case that you want to close.

3.  Fill in **Resolution Information** as needed.

4.  Click **Close Case**.
