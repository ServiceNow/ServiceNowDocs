# Configure Business Location Service Portal for Retail Core

Retail Core users can utilize the Business Location Service Portal to create and close retail case support requests.

Retail Core enables users to leverage the Business Location Service Portal \(BLSP\) available with the Customer Service Management application to manage their retail case support requests.

\[Omitted image "blsp\_2.png"\] Alt text: UI showing Business Location Service Portal for retail.

For more information on the Business Location Service Portal, see [Using the Business Location Service Portal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/customer-service-management/using-blsp-portal.md).
