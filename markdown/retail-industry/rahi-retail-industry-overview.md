# Retail

The Retail industry products provide Retail Operations and Retail Service Management users with a suite of applications to help optimize their retail processes.

[Retail Core](rahi-retail-operations-overview.md)

Retail Core empowers managers and associates to manage daily retail operations by automating tasks and resolving issues quickly with self-service, freeing up time to serve customers.
