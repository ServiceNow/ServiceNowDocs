# Track a customer complaint case in Workspace

Track the status of a retail customer complaint case in Workspace.

## Before you begin

Roles required: sn\_rtl\_cs\_cmplnt.agent, sn\_rtl\_cs\_cmplnt.agent\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Lists** &gt; **Retail Customer Complaints** &gt; **All**.

2.  Select the case using case number to track the case.
