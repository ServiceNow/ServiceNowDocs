# Configure an email subject line prefix

Users with the system administrator role can configure a prefix to include in an email subject line that creates a case.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Customer Service** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Properties**.

2.  In the **Email subject prefix format for new case** field, enter the prefix.

    The default prefix is **Case:**.

3.  Click **Save**.
