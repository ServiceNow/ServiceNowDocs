# Portal Banner widget

You can use the Portal Banner widget to display announcements, new product or feature highlights, and important information on the portal page.

For information about how to configure instance options for the Portal Banner widget, see [Add and configure the Portal Banner widget](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/customer-service-management/config-portal-banner-widget-inst-opt.md).
