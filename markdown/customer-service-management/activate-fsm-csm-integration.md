# Integrate with Field Service Management

Customer Service Management provides an integration with the Field Service Management application. This integration enables you to view work order and work order task information from a case.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System Definition** &gt; **Plugins**.

2.  Search for the Customer Service with Field Service Management plugin \(com.snc.csm\_fsm\_integration\).

3.  Click **Activate**.
