# Walk-up Check-in on Business Portal

Schedule an in-person walk-up appointment at a service center from the Business Portal.

## Before you begin

Install Walk-up for CSM \(com.snc.walkup\_for\_csm\) app from [ServiceNow store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home).

Role required: sn\_customerservice.customer

## About this task

Walk-up check-in enables business users to schedule appointments for in-person support at service centers. The system automatically retrieves the nearest service center and provides appointment scheduling options.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the Business Portal.

2.  Navigate to **Requests** &gt; **Submit a request** &gt; **Walk-up check in** in the portal header.

    The application identifies the nearest service center.

3.  Schedule an appointment.

    For details, see [Schedule appointments or join a queue from the Self Service portals](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/customer-service-management/csm-walkup-checkin-online.md).
