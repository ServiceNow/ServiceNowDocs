# Computer Telephone demo integration with tasks

The CTI Softphone component can be integrated with other task entities on the ServiceNow AI Platform.

A workflow can be implemented to operate on the Incident table.

**Note:** The Call Center for Incident Management workflow, a demo workflow, is available with the Customer Service CTI Demo Data plugin.

Incoming or outgoing calls are logged in the Phone Log \[sn\_openframe\_phone\_log\] table. The call logs also appear in the activity history of the task.
