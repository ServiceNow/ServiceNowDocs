# Using Walk-up Experience

Use the Walk-up Experience feature to check-in at a walk-up location, book appointments from the customer service portal, and monitor queues.

Users with roles including sn\_walkup.walkup\_login, sn\_csm\_walkup.walkup\_technician, sn\_csm\_walkup.walkup\_manager and walk-up users can use the Walk-up Experience feature.
