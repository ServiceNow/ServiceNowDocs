# Approve a service contract

Approve a service contract for a customer account.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice\_manager or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **My Approvals**.

2.  Select a contract for review from the Approvals list that has a state of **Requested**.

3.  Review the contract details in the summary at the bottom of the approval form.

4.  Add review comments to the **Comments** field.

5.  Click one of the following options.

<table id="choicetable_i13_3fn_55"><tbody><tr><td id="d200127e106">

**Approve**

</td><td>

The review state changes to **Approved** on the Approvals list.

</td></tr><tr><td id="d200127e118">

**Reject**

</td><td>

The review state changes to **Rejected** on the Approvals list. If you reject a contract, you must enter a rejection reason in the **Comments** field.

</td></tr></tbody>
</table>
