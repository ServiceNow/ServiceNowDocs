# Report knowledge gap from a case

When an OSP agent has a knowledge gap which impacts the ability to resolve a case, the agent can report the knowledge gap.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_csm\_ocs.ext\_agent

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Outsourced Customer Service** &gt; **Cases** &gt; **My Cases**.

2.  Open a case.

3.  Is knowledge available for the case in the **Knowledge Gaps** related list.

4.  Click **Report Knowledge Gap** related link.

5.  Enter the description.

6.  Click **Submit**.

    A knowledge gap is created and you can view it under the **Knowledge Gaps** related list.
