# Setup the Customer and Consumer Service Portals

Customize the Customer and Consumer Service Portals to meet your needs.

## Before you begin

Role required: sp\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Portal** &gt; **Service Portal Configuration**.

    Some of the components that you can customize include:

    -   Branding: [Configure the portal branding](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=c_BrandingEditor&version=australia&pubname=australia-platform-user-interface&ft:locale=en-US) to give your Customer Service Portal its own look and feel. The default theme for Customer and Consumer Service portals is the Coral theme.
    -   Menus: [Configure the portal header menu](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=configure-header-menu&version=australia&pubname=australia-platform-user-interface&ft:locale=en-US) to set up the main menu, one of the main navigation controls for your users.
    -   Homepage: [Assign a homepage to the portal](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=assign-portal-homepage&version=australia&pubname=australia-platform-user-interface&ft:locale=en-US).
    -   Widgets: Use [Service Portal widgets](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=service-portal-widgets&version=australia&pubname=australia-platform-user-interface&ft:locale=en-US) to define the content in your portal.
    -   Roles: [Configure page security by role](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=configure-page-security&version=australia&pubname=australia-platform-user-interface&ft:locale=en-US) to either set up pages to be public \(no login required\) or to filter them by user role.
