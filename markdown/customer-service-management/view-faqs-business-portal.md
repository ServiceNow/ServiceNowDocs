# View common questions \(FAQs\) on Business Portal

View a list of frequently asked questions on the Business Portal to find answers for some common questions.

You can access the frequently asked questions page by navigating to &gt; **Resources** &gt; **FAQs**. The system displays a list of 10 frequently asked questions as a default view.
