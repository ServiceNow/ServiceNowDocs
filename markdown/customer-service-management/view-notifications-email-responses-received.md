# View notifications on email response

View notifications on email responses that are received from the customer in the ongoing email conversation.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice\_agent, or sn\_customerservice\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace**.

2.  Select the ongoing tab icon \(\[Omitted image "Ongoing\_tab.jpg"\] Alt text: Ongoing tab icon\).

    A list of ongoing email interactions is displayed.

3.  Select the relevant email interaction.


## Result

The interaction record displays the latest email responses.
