# Field Service Management for Telecommunications

The ServiceNow Field Service Management for Telecommunication \(FSMT\) application enables you to manage the field service workflow for the Telecom customers. Use this application to streamline the appointment booking, service delivery, and assurance processes.

The ServiceNow Field Service Management for Telecommunication \(FSMT\) application integrates with the Field Service Management \(FSM\) to manage and streamline field service workflows for the telecommunications industry. The FSMT supports the TM Forum-aligned Open APIs and their functionalities that enable an open digital ecosystem. These APIs promote faster integration with Field Service Management to manage appointment booking, service delivery, and assurance processes.
