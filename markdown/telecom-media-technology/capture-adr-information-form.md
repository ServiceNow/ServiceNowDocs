# Capture ADR information form

The Capture initiator details form enables you to collect the escalated complaint details for an Alternative Dispute Resolution \(ADR\) case.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Product/service location|Location of the product or service where the issue is on.|
|Affected product/service inventory|The product or service, which the issue is facing.|
|Service status|Status of the service.|
|Category|Category for the complaint. This list contains the industry-standard categories that are based on the selected complaint type.|
|Subcategory|Subcategory for the complaint. This list contains the industry-standard subcategories that are based on the selected complaint category.|
|Priority| |
|Date issue occurred|Date on which your issue is occurred.|
|ADR received date|Date on which the complaint is received.|
|Short description|Brief description of the complaint.|
|Description|Detailed summary of the complaint.|
