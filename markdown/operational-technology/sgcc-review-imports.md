# Review imports

This section reviews all your importation settings in SGC Central.

## About this task

After you have completed the steps of the SGC Central OT Discovery playbook, you can review your imports by following this process.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the **All** menu and type in "discovery" in the search field.

2.  From the drop-down list, choose a review task.

    After completing the configurations and scheduling imports in the SGC Central, you can now go back and review the settings. Select:

    -   Review Imported OT Devices
    -   Review Imported Network Connections
    -   Review Imported Images
