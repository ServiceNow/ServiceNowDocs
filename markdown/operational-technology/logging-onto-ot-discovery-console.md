# Logging onto the Discovery Console for OT

This section provides additional information for getting started with the Discovery Console for OT.

## Initial log in

After installing the Discovery Console for OT you are initially prompted to register and log into the Console. This initial login gives you administrative rights throughout the entire system.

You are prompted to fill in your username, email, and password. The window indicates the strength of your password. You can select **Generate Random Password** instead of creating a password.

Copy or note the password you entered or generated. And then select the check box **I acknowledge I saved this password**.

![Initial log in window](../images/console-log-in-B.png)

You are now logged into the Discovery Console for OT.

**Note:** The process of saving your password ensures future access and helps prevent you from being locked out of the system.
