# Impact Delivery Instance \(formerly Impact Digital Experience\)

The Impact Delivery Instance \(IDI\) is a centralized, interactive portal where you can access your Impact tools and benefits.

## IDI Overview

See the Impact overview video to get started with Impact.ServiceNow Impact overview 

**Note:** Your Impact Squad sends you login information to access your IDI portal.

## Homepage

\[Omitted image "impact-landing-page.png"\] Alt text: Impact IDI homepage

**Note:** Portions of the IDI may not be available to customers in the FedRAMP, NSC DOD IL5, or Australia IRAP-Protected data centers, to self-hosted customers, or in other restricted environments, or to managed service providers \(except for their internal use\)
