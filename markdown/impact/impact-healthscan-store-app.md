# Impact Platform Health

The Impact Health Store Application enhances platform health by enabling proactive management of instance quality, compliance, and performance. Integrated with the Impact Store App, the Impact Health Store Application is designed to support platform health and adherence to leading practices.

-   **[Impact Proactive Code Check](proactive-code-check.md)**  
Developers scan update sets for leading practice violations in non-production instances before promoting to production and Platform Owners gain insight into technical debt and stability of both non-production and production instances, resulting in improved code quality, reduced errors, and compliance verification.
