# Activity Center Conversations

Scan all conversations you are included in. Start a conversation any time to ask a question or get advice from your Impact squad.

Conversations is customizable by either the Kanban view or a List View. Several filter options are also available:

-   Subject or reference number
-   Stakeholder group
-   Your conversations or all conversations
-   State of conversation, either open or closed

![Shows conversation lanes and the action menu to reposition a conversation category.](../image/conversations-sort.png)

-   Rearrange the order of the conversation subject the action menu for that lane.
-   Search by Subject or Reference Number.
-   Filter by All conversations or by My conversations.
-   View the state by Open or Closed.
