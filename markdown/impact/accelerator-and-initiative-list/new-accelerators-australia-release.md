# New Accelerators-Australia Release

Provides a list of latest accelerators available in the release Australia Q1-2026.

## New Technical Accelerators

Use the following latest Impact technical accelerators:

-   [Jumpstart Your Impact Platform Health](jumpstart-your-impact-platform-health.md): Enables teams to proactively monitor, maintain, and improve platform health in alignment with leading practices.
-   [Jumpstart Your Data Privacy](jumpstart-your-data-privacy.md): Introduces ServiceNow Vault’s Data Privacy capabilities and helps discover, classify, and make sensitive data anonymous,to support compliance, security, and trust.
-   [Jumpstart Your Walk-up Experience](jumpstart-your-walkup-experience.md): Demonstrates set up and operations of Walk-Up centers, enabling users to seek guidance on IT issues from experts.
-   [Jumpstart Your Digital Product Release](jumpstart-your-digital-product-release.md): Enables product and release teams to consistently plan and deliver new versions by providing visibility across the release life cycle and automating release-readiness validation.
-   [Jumpstart Your Modern Change Management](jumpstart-your-modern-change-management.md): Helps modernize change management implementations with change models and key supporting change features — such as approval policies and data-driven risk.
-   [Jumpstart Your Major Incident Management](jumpstart-your-major-incident-management.md): Envisions how to accelerate response times for major incidents and reduce business impact during critical outages.
-   [Jumpstart Your CSDM for Service Operations](jumpstart-your-csdm-for-service-operations.md): Enables you to build a data foundation that helps achieve implementation success of both IT Service and IT Operation products.
-   [Jumpstart Your Integration Hub](jumpstart-your-integration-hub.md): Helps customers understand the value of building integrations using Integration Hub as part of the Workflow Data Fabric.

## New Strategic Accelerators

Use the following latest Impact strategy accelerators:

-   [OCM: Preparing for Change](ocm-preparing-for-change.md): Builds change readiness and ensures successful user adoption ahead of go-live during ServiceNow transformations.
-   [Adopt AI Governance](adopt-ai-governance.md): Guides customers through the process of establishing and managing an Artificial Intelligence Management System \(AIMS\).

## New Architecture Accelerators

Use the following latest Impact architecture accelerators:

-   [Assess Your CSDM Maturity](csdm-maturity-assessment.md): Provides clear snapshot of current data management maturity related to the organization's capabilities and services.
-   [CSDM Data Modeling](csdm-data-modeling.md): Provides key resources and leading practices to get started with modeling your data to align to the ServiceNow platform successfully.
-   [UX: Optimize Your Portal with Usage Insights](ux-optimize-your-portal-with-usage-insights.md): Guides customers how to measure, monitor and analyze user behavior with UX analytics to identify and prioritize portal improvements.
-   [UX: Virtual Agent Experience Design or Review](ux-virtual-agent-experience-design-or-review.md): Provides guidance on designing or improving the conversational experience of Virtual Agent or Now Assist in Virtual Agent.
