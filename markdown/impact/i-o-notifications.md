# Instance Observer Notifications

Notifications for alerts and other system activity are displayed on the Instance Observer banner.

A notification indicator is available allowing direct access to the in app notifications that are sent to user email or SMS accounts, such as alerts and new releases.

-   Access the record directly to see the details.
-   Use Notification History to view the entire list from the last 72 hours.
-   Clear the alerts from the notification panel.
-   Access notification settings for Intelligent alerts and Monitoring alerts from the panel.
