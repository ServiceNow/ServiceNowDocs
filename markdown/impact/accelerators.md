# Request Accelerators and Initiatives

Depending on the Impact package that you are subscribed to, you can request and consume a designated number of concurrent Impact Accelerators.

## Before you begin

For details on available Accelerators, refer to the [Accelerator catalog](accelerator-catalog.md).

Some Accelerators require additional terms and conditions and are offered pursuant to the applicable [Impact Accelerator Description](https://www.servicenow.com/legal/servicenow-impact.html). For Accelerators that require consent, the terms and conditions must be accepted from the **Manage Permissions** menu.

Role required: impact app admin, or impact platform owner, or impact portfolio owner

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Impact** &gt; **Accelerators &amp; initiatives** &gt; **Overview**.

2.  Select **Request Accelerators &amp; initiatives** from the upper right-hand of the **Your Accelerators and Initiatives Overview** screen.

    The page redirects to the Accelerator and initiative catalog.

3.  Select an Accelerator flash card and select the maximize window icon.

    The Accelerator details page opens.

4.  Select **Request Accelerator**.
