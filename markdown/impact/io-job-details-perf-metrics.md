# Job details performance metrics

The metrics provide the job details performance snapshot within the ServiceNow AI Platform®.

-   **Job Execution**

    This presents a bar graph illustrating the hourly count of jobs, overlaid with transaction counts to correlate with the working hours of the day. The left-side Y-axis indicates the count of scheduled jobs executing, while the right-side Y-axis represents transaction counts.

    **Note:** This chart is limited to displaying data available for the past 28 days.

-   **Top 2K Executing Jobs**

    Tabular representation of the top 2,000 executing jobs ranked by their execution time. This data is available for the past 6 months.

-   **Top 2K Pending Jobs**

    Tabular representation of the top 2,000 jobs based on their average delay in starting the scheduled job, also known as Lateness duration. This data is available for the past 6 months.

-   **Top 50 Recurring Jobs**

    Tabular representation of the top 50 jobs that have been executed multiple times, including their respective counts. This data is available for the past 6 months.
