# Xanadu Public Sector

- [Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/government-industry/public-sector-digital-services/bun-public-sector-landing-page.md) -- Extend beyond the packaged government case types, leveraging the government data model to create your own workflows with low-code tools. Fulfill public service requests end-to-end with pre-packaged playbooks. Deliver a personalized experience to take care of common constituent requests using resources like the portal, service catalog, virtual agent, and online communities. Use the ServiceNow Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS) application to serve constituents, businesses, and agencies on a single platform.
