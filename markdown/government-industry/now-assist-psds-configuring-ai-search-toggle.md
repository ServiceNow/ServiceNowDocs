# Enable AI Search for Now Assist for Public Sector Digital Services \(PSDS\)

Enable the Now Assist in AI Search application to extract actionable Q&amp;A Genius Result answers from knowledge articles found in the Service Portal, Virtual Agent, Employee Center, and global searches.

## About this task

**Note:** By default, Now Assist Search is enabled in Now Assist for Public Sector Digital Services \(PSDS\). Follow these steps only if the application has been previously disabled.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **AI Search** &gt; **AI Search Status**.

2.  If the screen says you do not have AI search on the instance, select **Request AI Search**.

3.  Wait until AI Search is displayed as activated and ready for use.

    ![Modal displaying AIS as activated and ready.](../image/ais-activated-na-psds.png)
