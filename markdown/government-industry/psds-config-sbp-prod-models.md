# Configure a Social Benefits Playbook Product Model

Set up one or more product models to model the various social benefit programs your agency may choose to provide, so that your agency can define and manage all its benefit programs.

Product models are used to model social benefit programs, such as nutrition assistance, temporary financial assistance, unemployment insurance, vocational training, and more. Product Models help in defining the benefit, linking services, creating workflows, and generating reports.

## Configure product model form for the Social Benefits Playbook

Configure the product model form.

### Before you begin

Role required: admin

Application scope from Global to Social Benefits Playbook.

### Procedure

1.  In the Unified Navigation bar, enter `sn_ent_social_benefit_model.do`, and select **Enter**.

2.  Select the Additional actions icon, then select **Configure** &gt; **Form Layout**,

3.  On the modal pop-up, select **Not Now**.

4.  Select **Edit this section in Expanded Model and Asset Classes**.

5.  Add the following fields to the form:

    -   Applicant Type
    -   Benefit Frequency
    -   Benefit Type
    -   Cost
    -   Customer Visible
    -   Reporting Frequency
    -   Requires Interview
6.  Remove the following fields from the form:

    -   Asset Tracking Strategy
    -   Asset Tracking Unit
    -   Life Cycle Stage
    -   Life Cycle Stage Status
    -   Model Categories
7.  Select **Save**.

8.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **** &gt; ****.


## Define the Social Benefits product model in Social Benefits Playbook

Define the attributes of a nutrition assistance benefit program.

### Before you begin

Role required: admin

### Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **** &gt; ****.

2.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    |Product Model Form Field|Description|
    |------------------------|-----------|
    |Name|Name of the benefit program|
    |Status|Status of the benefit program|
    |Short Description|Brief description of the benefit program.|
    |Applicant type|Type of applicant that can submit for the benefit program. Individual,|
    |Benefit Frequency|How often the benefit will be sent out to recipients.|
    |Benefit Type|The amount of the benefit to be paid out at the above frequency.|
    |Cost|The amount of the benefit to be paid out at the above frequency. As an admin, you can create a benefit flow that enables adjustments to be made based on household size, disability payments, and household expenses. For more information, see .|
    |Customer Visible|Whether this benefit program will be visible on the constituent-facing benefits portal.|
    |Reporting frequency|How often the benefit?|
    |Requires Interview|Whether the benefit case requires an interview to verify benefits.|

3.  Select **Submit**.
