# Create offerings

Create offerings from scratch. For more information about using Now Assist to generate offerings for your service, see X.

## Before you begin

Role required: service\_author

An offering is an aspect or variant of your service that meets particular needs or business requirements. While your service represents a high-level view, an offering provides more detailed, tangible components. Your offerings may include features, capabilities, or configurations that are part of your broader service.

The Service Modeler workflow helps you define your offerings by guiding you through the following:

1.  Overview
2.  Define your offering
3.  Create a support model
4.  Add service level commitments
5.  Summary

You can complete the steps in any order you choose. For more information about the guided questions in each step, see [Create offering forms](../reference/sm-create-offering-forms.md).

**Tip:** You can cancel out of creating a service anytime by selecting X and then selecting **Discard changes**. To delete your service at any point in the process, select the more actions icon \(![](../../digital-product-release/image/dpr-icon-more-actions-v.png)\), then select **Delete**. Alternatively, you may select **Save and exit** at any time to save your progress and come back later.
