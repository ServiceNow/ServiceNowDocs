# Create a service in Service Modeler

Create services to improve visibility, automation, and user experience. You can model, map, and document your own business and technology management services and offerings.

## Before you begin

Role required: service\_author

## About this task

The Service Modeler workflow helps you define, configure, and publish business and technology management services by guiding you through the process of adding information in a logical order:

1.  Define the service
2.  Create offerings
3.  Validate the service and offerings: this isn't available yet for Innovation Lab, but will be coming soon
4.  Get approvals
5.  Publish

**Tip:** You can cancel out of creating a service anytime by selecting X and then selecting **Discard changes**. To delete your service at any point in the process, select the more actions icon \(![](../../digital-product-release/image/dpr-icon-more-actions-v.png)\), then select **Delete**. Alternatively, you may select **Save and exit** at any time to save your progress and come back later.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Modeler**.

2.  Select **Create a service**.

    For more information about creating a service using Now Assist, see [Create a service with Now Assist in Service Modeler](sm-create-service-now-assist.md).

    The Overview tab shows the steps: Define the service, Create offerings, Validate the service and offerings, Get approvals, and Publish.

3.  Select **Start** to begin defining your service.

4.  Select **Define the service**.

5.  Answer the following question to help us determine the type of service you're creating: Who uses the service?

    -   Service owners: Technology management service
    -   General employees: Business service
    -   External customers: Business service
    -   Not sure
6.  Describe the purpose of the service.

7.  Give the service a unique name.

8.  Answer the remaining questions.

9.  For more information, see the Define service form.
