# Digital End-user Experience Self-service

Digital End-user Experience Self-service \(DEX Self-service\) enables you to check the real-time performance of your devices through the Device health check.

This functionality is available via a widget in Desktop Assistant and Employee Center, as well as through the Now Assist-enabled Virtual Agent. Using DEX Self-service, you can check device issues, resolve them based on suggested resolutions, and initiate device actions to maintain optimal device performance.
