# Approve or reject a service in Service Modeler

Approve a service in order for it to be published. You can reject a service and provide comments describing your reasoning and suggestions for edits.

## Before you begin

Role required: service\_viewer

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Modeler** &gt; ****.

2.  Review the details of the service and its offerings and do one of the following:

    -   To approve the service and its offerings, select **Approve**.
    -   To reject the service and its offerings, select **Reject**.
3.  Leave a comment:

    -   If you're approving the service, provide positive feedback and select **Approve**.
    -   If you're rejecting the service, provide feedback specifically describing what you're rejecting and why, then select **Reject**.
    If you approve, the status of the request changes from Under review to Approved. The service author can publish the service. If you reject, the status of the request changes to Rejected. The service author can make edits and resubmit for your approval.
