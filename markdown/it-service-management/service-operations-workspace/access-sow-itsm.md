# Access Service Operations Workspace for ITSM

Experience a unified and seamless experience when managing the life cycle of task records such as incidents and requests.

## Before you begin

Role required: itil

## Procedure

1.  Log into your ServiceNow instance.

2.  From the **Workspaces** menu, select **Service Operations Workspace**.
