# Get a free application

Get a free application from your ServiceNow instance, without navigating to the ServiceNow Store.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System Application** &gt; **All Available Applications** &gt; **Available To Obtain From Store**.

2.  Search for the required application and click the application record.

3.  Click **Get**.

4.  In the pop-up window, review information in these tabs and click **Continue**:

    -   **Usage Analytics and Contact**
    -   **Platform Licenses**
    -   **Terms and Conditions**

        **Note:** Select the **I read and accept terms of use above** check box.

        You can download the terms and conditions documents.

    In the **Confirmation** tab, a confirmation message is displayed that the application can be installed.

5.  Click **Go to install**.
