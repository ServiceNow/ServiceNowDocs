# Entitle a product from your ServiceNow instance

Manage entitlement of products from your ServiceNow instance.

## Before you begin

Purchase the product on ServiceNow Store and [opt in](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/platform-administration/optin-optout-prod.md) from your ServiceNow instance.

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **System Applications** &gt; **All Available Applications** &gt; **All**.

2.  From the filter options, select the **Product not entitled** indicator.

    All the purchased unentitled products are displayed.

3.  Select the menu icon \( \[Omitted image "menu-icon.png"\] Alt text: Menu icon \) on the product tile, and select **Entitle this instance**.

    \[Omitted image "entitle-product.png"\] Alt text: Entitle a product.

4.  In the Application entitlement dialog box, select **OK**.


## Result

Product entitlement is provided to your ServiceNow instance.

## What to do next

[Install](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/platform-administration/t_InstallApplications.md) the product if it’s compatible with the version of your ServiceNow instance.
