# Activate email administration

Activate email filter and email service.

-   **[Activate email filters](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/platform-administration/t_ActivateEmailFilters.md)**  
Administrators can activate the Email Filters plugin \(com.glide.email\_filter\).
-   **[Activate Email Service](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/platform-administration/activate-email-service.md)**  
Users with the admin role can activate the Email Service plugin \(com.glide.email.service\) to enable the Email API.
