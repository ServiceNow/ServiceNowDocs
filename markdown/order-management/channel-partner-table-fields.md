# Channel partner table fields

Use the fields on the channel partner \[sn\_prm\_channel\_partner\] table to manage and store information related to channel partners.

<table id="table_fzm_sdx_1fc"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Number

</td><td>

Automatically generated number of the channel partner table.**Note:** By default, channel partner numbers start with the prefix CHPRTNR.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Name

</td><td>

Name of the channel partner.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Business function

</td><td>

Business function associated with the channel partner, either sales or service.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Enterprise partner manager

</td><td>

Manager from the enterprise who is associated with the channel partner.When an enterprise manager is added on the channel partner form, they are also added as a member of the enterprise with a role and responsibility.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Partner manager

</td><td>

Manager from the channel partner that supervises partner member activities.When an enterprise manager is added on the channel partner form, they are also added as a member of the enterprise with a role and responsibility.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Status

</td><td>

Status of the channel partner, whether **Suspended** or **Terminated**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Parent channel partner

</td><td>

Parent entity of the channel partner.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Type

</td><td>

 

</td></tr><tr><td>

</td></tr><tr><td>

Activity

</td><td>

Updates and information related to the channel partner.
