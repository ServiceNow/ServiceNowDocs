# Access the Business Portal

Customers can access the Business Portal from their instance to browse products and create orders.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice.customer

## Procedure

1.  Go to your instance and select the URL.

2.  Edit the instance URL and append **/business\_portal** to the end after the com.

    The Business Portals opens.
