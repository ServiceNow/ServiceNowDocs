# View export job status

Check the status of an export job for product catalog or pricing entities to see if it's in progress or completed.

## Before you begin

Role required: product\_catalog\_admin for catalog entities, sn\_csm\_pricing\_pricelist\_administrator for pricing entities

## Procedure

1.  In the **CSM Configurable Workspace**, select the **List** ![image.Lists] view.

2.  Navigate to **Export** &gt; **Export Catalog**.

    The **Export Catalog** list shows the status of export jobs.

3.  In the **State** column for the export job, view the status.


## What to do next

If the export job is complete, [import the product catalog entities](import-product-catalog-entities.md) or [import the pricing entities](import-pricing-entities.md) to your target instance.
