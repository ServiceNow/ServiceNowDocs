# Apply a sales agreement to a quote in Quote Management

Agents can add a sales agreement to a quote while they are building the quote. The sales agreements conditions are then applied to the quote.

## Before you begin

Role required: sales\_agent and sales\_manager

## About this task

A sales agreement is a contract to order a set of products and services for a set price. Sales agreements let agents move quickly through the quote process because the sales agreements establish the products and pricing for the quote.

-   Sales agreement terms are defaulted for the quote.​
-   Price lists are defaulted based on the pricing terms in the sales agreement​.
-   The quote catalog is filtered for products and options by the terms of the sales agreement​.
-   Agents can configure product bundles for quotes with sales agreements.
-   When a new revision of a quote is created, sales agreement references on quote header and quote line items are carried over to the revised quote.
-   When an order is created from a quote, the sales agreement references on quote header and quote line items is carried over to the order.

**Note:** Consumer quotes do not have sales agreement references present on quote header or quote line items.

See [Using Sales Agreement Management](../concept/sales-agreement-mgmt-using.md) and [Create a sales agreement](create-new-sales-agreement.md) to learn more about creating a sales agreement.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Quote** &gt; **All-Quotes** and select the quote you’re working with.

2.  In the **Quote** form, find the **Sales Agreement** field and select the sales agreement you want.

3.  Select **Save**.

    The sales agreement with its product offerings and price lists is added to the quote.
