# Enable the Business Portal

Admins can enable the Business Portal \(sn\_b2b\_portal\) so customers can browse products and create orders.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## About this task

To set up the Business Portal and Sales Customer Relationship Management self-service enhancements, first install the Business Portal plugins, which are available in the app store.

See [Configuring the Business Portal](../concept/order-management-configure-business-portal.md) for more information.

## Procedure

1.  In the CSM Configurable Workspace, select **All** &gt; **Portals**.

2.  Select **Business Portals**.

3.  Clear the **Inactive** box.

4.  Select **Save**.

    The Business Portal is enabled.
