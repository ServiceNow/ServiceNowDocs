# Create opportunity stages

Create sales cycle stages for an opportunity.

## Before you begin

Role required: sales\_ops\_specialist

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Opportunity Management** &gt; **Opportunity Stages**.

2.  Select **New** to create an opportunity stage or select an existing opportunity stage to make changes.

3.  Fill in the fields in the Opportunity Stage form.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Name|Name of the opportunity stage. For example, Qualify or Develop or Propose or Negotiate.|
    |Order|Number of orders that is in the opportunity stage.|
    |Type|Type of the opportunity stages. For example, Open or Closed.|
    |Active|When the option is selected, the opportunity stage is active.|

4.  Select **Submit**.

    The sales cycle stage has now been created for the opportunity.
