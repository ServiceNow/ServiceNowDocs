# Customer Contracts and Entitlements reference

Reference topics provide additional information on service contract life cycle and forms available within the Customer Contracts and Entitlements application.

## What to explore next

To learn more about configuring and using Customer Contracts and Entitlements, see:

-   [Configure Customer Contracts and Entitlements](configuring-post-sales-support.md)
-   [Using Customer Contracts and Entitlements](using-post-sales-support.md)

-   **[Service contract life cycle](life-cycle-management.md)**  
A service contract goes through the various states in each phase of its life cycle.
-   **[Service contract form](../reference/service-contract-form.md)**  
The Customer Contracts and Entitlements application uses the Service contract form to create service contracts.
-   **[Service contract line form](../reference/service-contract-line-form.md)**  
You can create service contract lines in the Customer Contracts and Entitlements application by using the Service Contract Line form.
-   **[Entitlement form](../reference/entitlement-form.md)**  
The Entitlement form can be used to create entitlements in Customer Contracts and Entitlements.
-   **[Sold Product Covered form](../reference/sold-product-covered-form.md)**  
The Customer Contracts and Entitlements application uses the Sold Product Covered form to add sold products or install base items that are covered to the Customer Contracts and Entitlements entities.
