# How price is displayed on a layout with multiple BOMs

CPQ shows only the price of the products in each bill of materials.

When multiple bills of material \(BOMs\) are displayed in a layout, CPQ automatically filters the total of each BOM to show only the price of the products contained in the BOM, filtered by BOM type. If a combined product list of all BOM types is displayed, it shows the prices of all products in the BOM. See the following example.
