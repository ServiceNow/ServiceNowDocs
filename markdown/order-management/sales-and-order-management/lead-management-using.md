# Using Lead Management

Use the Lead Management to create your lead.

## Lead Management interface

The Lead Management interface contains the following tabs:

|Tab|Description|
|---|-----------|
|[Details tab](../task/lead-management-details-tab.md)|Add basic information for your lead.|
|[Catalog tab](../task/lead-management-add-product.md)|Search for and add product offerings to your lead.|
|[Needs tab](../task/lead-management-use-needs-analysis.md)|Lists the need templates that provide product recommendations for a lead.|
|[Tasks tab](../task/lead-management-use-tasks-tab.md)|Create lead tasks.|
|[Appointments tab](../task/lead-management-create-appointment.md)|Create appointments for your lead.|
|[Line items tab](../task/lead-management-use-line-items-tab.md)|Add different line items to a lead.|
|[Emails tab](../task/lead-management-use-emails-tab.md)|Create emails to communicate with the required stakeholders.|
