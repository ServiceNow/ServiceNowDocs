# Add details to a quote

Use the Details tab to add basic information to a quote.

## Before you begin

Role required: sales\_agent

## Procedure

1.  In the CSM Configurable Workspace, select the **List** ![](../image/Lists.png) view.

2.  Navigate to **Quotes** &gt; **All** and select your quote.

3.  In the Details tab, fill in the basic information.
