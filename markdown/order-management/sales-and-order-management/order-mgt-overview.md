# Sales and Order Management

Learn how you can use the ServiceNow® Sales and Order Management \(SOM\) applications to manage the product sales life cycle in your organization. Your agents can use these applications to generate pre-sales leads and opportunities, provide sales quotes, capture and fulfill orders, work with contracts and entitlements, and manage the customer order workflow for changes.
