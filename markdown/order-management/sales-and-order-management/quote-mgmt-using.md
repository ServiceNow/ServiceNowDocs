# Using Quote Management

Use the Quote Management application to build quotes. The Quote Management application provides the following features to help you create and manage product quotes.

## Quote management features

Quote management lets agents create and manage customer quotes. Once quotes are agreed to by customers, quotes can be converted into product and service orders.

|Quote Management fields|Description|
|-----------------------|-----------|
|[Details field](../task/quote-management-details-tab.md)|Add details to a quote, such as customer address and contact information.|
|[Enable a sales agreement in a quote](../task/quote-create-sales-agreement.md)|Enables a sales agreement quote. A sales agreement quote uses the agreement parameters to filter products and services for agents to choose from.|
|[Line Items fields](../task/quote-managment-line-items-tab.md)|Add quote line items to a quote. Quote line items are products and services for the customer. The prices are displayed and totaled for the customer to review.|
|[Catalog fields](../task/quote-management-catalog-tab.md)|Use product catalogs to add products to your quote. The catalogs contains simple product or bundled products that have option that agents can select. Products can also be filtered by location.|
|[Revisions fields](../task/quote-management-revisions-tab.md)|Create multiple versions of a quote. As information changes, agents can version quotes to keep track of the quote process.|
|[Customer Orders fields](../task/quote-management-revisions-tab.md)|Convert a quote to a product sales order. When agents have quotes that are ready, they can easily convert them to customers orders.|
|[Pricing adjustment fields](../task/quote-management-pricing-adjustments-tab.md)|Add manual pricing adjustments to products and services in a quote. This lets agents add incentive to customers through pricing adjustments.|
|[Add subscription start and end dates to a quote](../task/quote-managment-add-subscription-pricing.md)|Add a subscription start date and end date for the subscription items such as warranties and service agreements. See to learn more about subscription pricing.|
|[Control cascading quantity values in child product offerings](../task/som-activate-cascade-quantity.md)|Cascade quantity specifies that the desired quantity from a parent line item is cascaded to the child line item through a system level parameter.​|
|[Sync a quote with an opportunity](../task/som-sync-quote-with-opportunity.md)|Agents can sync the information in a quote back to an opportunity to keep records update to date and accurate.|

## Quote workflow

The following table shows the basic tasks for creating, building, and managing a quote.

|Quote tasks|Description|
|-----------|-----------|
|[Enable a sales agreement in a quote](../task/quote-create-sales-agreement.md).|See [Create a quote](../task/create-new-quote.md) and [Add details to a quote](../task/quote-management-details-tab.md) for more information.|
|Enable a sales agreement quote.|Sales agreement quotes are built according to the linked sales agreement.|
|Build your quote by adding products from the product catalogs.|See [Add products to a quote](../task/quote-management-catalog-tab.md) for more information.|
|Select options to products using the product configurator.|See [View and update products in a quote](../task/quote-managment-line-items-tab.md) for more information.|
|Add pricing adjustments to quotes.|See [View manual pricing adjustments](../task/quote-management-pricing-adjustments-tab.md) for more information.|
|Create quote revisions.|See [Create quote versions](../task/quote-management-revisions-tab.md) for more information.|
|Convert your quotes to customer orders.|See [Convert quotes into orders](../task/quote-management-customer-orders-tab.md)for more information.|
|Enable a sales agreement quote.|If your organization is using sales agreements, you can create a sales agreement within the quote by selecting the **Generate Sales Agreement** option. To learn more about sales agreements, see [Using Sales Agreement Management](sales-agreement-mgmt-using.md). For details on using a quote to generate a sales agreement, see [Enable a sales agreement in a quote](../task/quote-create-sales-agreement.md)|
