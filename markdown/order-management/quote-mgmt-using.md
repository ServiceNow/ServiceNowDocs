# Using Quote Management

Use the Quote Management application to build your quote. The interface includes the following tabs to help you create and manage product quotes.

|Quote Management tab|Description|
|--------------------|-----------|
|Details tab|Add details of the quote, such as address and contact information.|
|Line Items tab|Add quote line items to a quote.|
|Catalog tab|Use product catalogs to add products to your quote.|
|Revisions tab|Create multiple versions of quotes.|
|Customer Orders tab|Convert quotes to product order sales.|
|Pricing adjustment tab|Add manual pricing adjustments to products in a quote.|

-   **[Create a quote](../task/create-new-quote.md)**  
Create a quote in Quote Management to start the quote building process.
-   **[Add and view details of a quote](../task/quote-management-details-tab.md)**  
Use the Details tab to add and view basic information for a quote.
-   **[Add products to a quote](../task/quote-management-catalog-tab.md)**  
Use the Catalog tab in Quote Management to add products to your quote.
-   **[Using product offering recommendations in quotes](using-prod-recommendations-quote.md)**  
As a sales agent, you can use recommended product offerings to upsell or cross-sell additional products in your quotes.
-   **[Add tasks to a quote](../task/quote-management-tasks.md)**  
Create a single or multiple tasks for a quote and assign those to different agents. Tasks are the various actions that agents perform to create a quote.
-   **[Create quote versions](../task/quote-management-revisions-tab.md)**  
Use the Revisions tab to create and view the versions of your quote.
-   **[Convert quotes into orders](../task/quote-management-customer-orders-tab.md)**  
Use the Customer Order tab to convert a quote into a customer order in Quote Management.
-   **[Add pricing adjustment to a quote line item](../task/quote-management-add-pricing-adjustment.md)**  
Add pricing adjustments to quote line items after the line item is created.
-   **[Add price ramps on a quote line item](../task/add-price-ramps-on-a-quote-line-item.md)**  
Add price ramps to quote line items to define incremental price changes for recurring product offerings.
-   **[Add a header discount to a quote](../task/add-header-discount-to-a-quote.md)**  
Apply a percentage-based discount to the entire quote, or to a selected type of line items within the quote. The quote line items automatically inherit the designated discount percentage as a manual adjustment on the quote line level.
-   **[Approving or rejecting quotes](approving-or-rejecting-quotes.md)**  
Sales agents request approval for quotes, and approvers decide whether quotes can proceed under an advanced approval workflow.
-   **[Create an order from a quote line item](../task/quote-management-create-order-quote-line.md)**  
Create orders from quote line items by using the **Create new order** UI action.
-   **[Add subscription pricing to a quote](../task/quote-management-add-subscription-pricing.md)**  
Add subscription pricing to a quote or quote line items including start and end dates in Quote Management.
-   **[Submit a quote for advanced approval](../task/submit-a-quote-for-approval.md)**  
Submit a quote for advanced approval to route it to required approvers before sharing it with a customer.
-   **[Recall a quote approval request](../task/recall-a-quote-approval-request.md)**  
Cancel a pending quote approval request to change a quote before resubmitting it for advanced approval.
-   **[Approve an approval request for a quote](../task/approve-a-quote-approval-request.md)**  
Review and approve or reject a quote advanced approval request that is routed to you as part of an advanced approval workflow.
-   **[View and update products using the line items tab](../task/quote-management-line-items-tab.md)**  
Use the line items tab to review and update products in your quote and to add additional line items.
-   **[View and update manual pricing adjustments in quotes](../task/quote-management-pricing-adjustments-tab.md)**  
Use the Pricing Adjustments tab in Quote Management to view and update manual pricing adjustments made to order line items in your quote.
-   **[Initiate a contract request](../task/quote-management-create-contract.md)**  
Initiate a contract request from the Quote Management application.
-   **[View an existing quote](../task/quote-management-viewing-a-quote.md)**  
View existing quotes to update the quote status or make other changes.
-   **[Export quotes](../task/quote-management-export-quotes.md)**  
Export a list of all quotes in Quote Management and provide it to a customer.
-   **[Enable a sales agreement in a quote](../task/quote-create-sales-agreement.md)**  
Agents can enable a sales agreement for a quote. Once enabled in Quote Management, sales agreements are generated that show the product and service lines and other relevant details for the quote.
-   **[Sync a quote with an opportunity](../task/som-sync-quote-with-opportunity.md)**  
Agents can sync quote information with an opportunity to ensure that information is consistent between both applications in Quote Management.
-   **[Add covered products to a quote](../task/som-quote-add-covered-products.md)**  
Add a covered product to line items in a quote in Quote Management.
-   **[Filter catalogs by location in a quote](../task/quote-using-product-location.md)**  
Use the location filter on the Catalog tab to filter products by location in Quote Management.
-   **[Copy locations for quote line items](../task/quote-mgt-copy-quote-item-location.md)**  
Use the copy location function to change the location of multiple quote line items in Quote Management.
-   **[Create a quote PDF document](../task/quote-create-pdf-document.md)**  
In Quote Management, sales agents can create a PDF of a quote that can be sent to customers for review and signatures.
-   **[View a quote PDF document](../task/quote-view-a-quote-document.md)**  
Agents can view quote documents in the PDF viewer window. The PDF viewer window has controls that enable agents to manage the PDF.
-   **[Use hierarchical list view for quote line items](../task/quote-management-view-hierarchical-line-items.md)**  
Sales agents can view quote line items as a hierarchical list, which helps to view the parent and child relationship between line items.
-   **[Consolidate quotes](../task/consolidate-quotes.md)**  
Consolidate renewal quotes to merge multiple quotes for the same account into a single unified quote.
