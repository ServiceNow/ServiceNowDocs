# General Inquiry form

Use the general inquiry form on the Partner portal to raise queries and get in touch with the enterprise.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Partner Organization|Partner organization to which the partner belongs.|
|Requested by|User who has raised the request.|
|Priority|Level of importance of the inquiry.|
|Subject|Overview of the inquiry.|
|What can we help you with|Detailed explanation of the inquiry.|
