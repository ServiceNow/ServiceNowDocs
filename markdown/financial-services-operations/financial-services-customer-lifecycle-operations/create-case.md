# Create a CLO case

Begin the Customer Lifecycle Operations application process by creating a CLO case for review.

## Before you begin

Role required:

-   For a business client: sn\_bom\_clo\_b2b.manager or sn\_bom\_clo\_b2b.relationship\_manager
-   For a personal client: sn\_bom\_clo\_b2c.manager or sn\_bom\_clo\_b2c.relationship\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Financial services operations** &gt; **Workspace**.

2.  Click the lists icon \(![Lists icon.](../../fso-card-operations/image/list-icon.png)\).

3.  In the upper right-hand corner, click **New**.

4.  From the Create a new case menu that appears in the middle of the screen, select a service.

5.  Click **Create**.

6.  In the upper right-hand corner of the screen, click **Save**.

7.  If you are the agent that will be handling the case, select **Assign to me**.

    If not, select the agent who will handle the case.

8.  Click the **Playbook** tab.

9.  Fill in any applicable fields, then click **Create Account**.


## Result

A Customer Lifecycle Operations case is created and assigned to an available agent.

## What to do next

Continue to [Add contacts](create-contact.md).
