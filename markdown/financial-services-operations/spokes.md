# Integrating with spokes

By integrating spokes with the Financial Services Operations \(FSO\) applications, flow designers can provide the actions within Workflow Studio for specific applications.

A spoke is a scoped application containing Workflow Studio content dedicated to a particular application or record type. You can integrate a ServiceNow application with a FSO banking application spoke such as Financial Services Operations Integration with Jack Henry jXchange Socure, or FRISS.

|Plugin name|What you can do|Plugin directory|
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
|[Equifax Spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=equifax-spoke&version=australia&pubname=australia-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US)|Access customer credit history, fraud alerts, digital identity verification, and transaction screening to enhance risk checks and onboarding workflows.|\[com.sn\_equifax\_spoke\]|
|[Ethoca spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=ethoca-spoke&version=australia&pubname=australia-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US)|View merchant details, control subscriptions, and manage disputes with access to digital receipts and transaction data.|\[com.sn\_ethoca\_spoke\]|
|[Jack Henry jXchange Spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=jha-spoke&version=australia&pubname=australia-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US)|Automate account management, transaction processing, and fraud detection by adding financial process actions to flows.|\[com.sn.jha.spoke\]|
|[Socure Spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=socure-fso&version=australia&pubname=australia-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US)|Improve onboarding and authentication by validating user identity, performing risk assessments, and detecting fraud.|\[com.sn\_socure\_spoke\]|
|[Mastercard Spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=mastercard-spoke&version=australia&pubname=australia-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US)|Search transactions, create claims, and process chargebacks as part of the Mastercard dispute handling process.|\[com.sn\_mastercard\_spoke\]|
|[Visa Spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=visa-spoke&version=australia&pubname=australia-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US)|Automate Visa card dispute processes by invoking Visa APIs for end-to-end case management.|\[com.sn\_visa\_spoke\]|
|[Verifi Spoke](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=verifi-spoke&version=australia&pubname=australia-integrate-applications&ft:locale=en-US)|Resolve pre-disputes, prevent chargebacks, and manage the dispute resolution process efficiently.|\[com.sn\_verifi\_spoke\]|
