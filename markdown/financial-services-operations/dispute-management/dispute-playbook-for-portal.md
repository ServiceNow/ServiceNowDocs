# Managing dispute intake in portal

With Financial Services Operations \(FSO\) card disputes intake in portal, you get the step-by-step guidance for the case flow process. It includes all the information you need to successfully complete each stage and its related tasks.

## Overview

Using the FSO card disputes playbook, cardholders can directly create a dispute case from the portal. This playbook provides a detailed, step-by-step guide to help them navigate through the dispute process.

Key benefits of using a card disputes playbook for portal:

-   Streamline the cardholder experience with a step-by-step process that helps in selecting disputed transactions, completing questionnaires, uploading supporting documents, and tracking dispute progress seamlessly. The system automatically selects the relevant questionnaire based on the dispute category.
-   Enable cardholders to monitor the latest updates on the status of their disputed cases and to provide updates at the overall case level using the dispute tracker.
-   Reduce case resolution times by helping cardholders to promptly and accurately track and complete requested tasks.

-   **[Creating a card disputes case with the Dispute Intake in Portal](creating-an-onboarding-case-with-dispute-playbook-for-portal.md)**  
Whether you're a contact or a consumer, you can create a card disputes case in either the Customer Service Portal or Consumer Service Portal, respectively.

Create a card disputes case request from the Customer or Consumer Service Portals by navigating the service catalog.
-   **[Viewing the status of a request using the dispute tracker](../task/view-the-status-of-a-request.md)**  
Track the progress of card disputes case requests using the dispute tracker.
