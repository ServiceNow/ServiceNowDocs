# About Dispute Management

ServiceNow Dispute Management enables issuing banks to deflect card disputes, streamline data collection, and simplify payment exception operations.

## What is Dispute Management?

Dispute management is a system that helps issuing banks handle customer complaints about card transactions. It ensures that disputes are resolved efficiently, enhancing the overall experience for cardholders.

## Why is It Important?

Dispute management is important because it:

-   Helps prevent issues before they escalate.
-   Makes it easier for banks to gather and manage information.
-   Improves how customers feel about the service by resolving disputes quickly.

## How Does It Work?

-   When a dispute is reported, all necessary information is collected upfront to understand the situation better.
-   Disputes are categorized to determine how best to address them, whether they involve fraud, billing errors, or other issues.
-   With pre-built integrations to Visa and Mastercard, issuing banks can provide additional transaction details to help deflect potential disputes. If a dispute is initiated, these integrations allow banks to manage the resolution process effectively.
-   The system supports efficient decision-making for both fraud and non-fraud disputes. Banks can process claims and automatically submit chargebacks to the appropriate card networks with minimal effort, ensuring that the process is smooth and quick.

## Benefits

-   Customers receive quicker answers, enhancing their overall satisfaction.
-   Efficient processes help minimize penalties and financial losses associated with disputes.
-   The system can handle disputes for multiple card networks, including Visa and Mastercard, providing banks with scalability and adaptability.
