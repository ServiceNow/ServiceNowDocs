# Investigating a dispute

Complete tasks such as initiate provisional credit, determine a recovery option, write off a disputed transaction amount, provide immediate final credit, notify the merchant, determine reversal of provisional credit, and investigate the transaction.

-   **[Detect friendly fraud](resolve-friendly-fraud.md)**  
Detect friendly fraud and determine the course of actions required depending on the amount being disputed, the customer relationship, and the outcome of the detection logic.
-   **[Customer communication](resolve-fraud-customer-communication.md)**  
Provide a response and feedback to the customer for a decision made on a dispute, such as friendly fraud.
-   **[Review participating merchant alerts](alert-merchant-regarding-dispute.md)**  
Alert the merchant about a card transaction dispute.
-   **[Report fraud](report-fraud.md)**  
Report potential fraud on a transaction to a card network. If the dispute category is **Fraud**, the **Report fraud** activity is displayed for a transaction.
-   **[Issue provisional credit](provide-provisional-credit-to-customer.md)**  
Initiate temporary provisional credit to the card holder during the Investigation stage based on the amount being disputed and the policy for the credit.
-   **[Set recovery option](recovery-option-for-final-credit.md)**  
Determine whether to initiate a chargeback for a transaction, where the cardholder has not yet received the final credit, ensuring that the recovery process aligns with established guidelines.
-   **[Issue credit](provide-immediate-final-credit.md)**  
Issue immediate final credit to the card holder during the Investigation stage based on the amount being disputed and the policy for the credit.
-   **[Investigate transactions](manually-investigate-disputed-transaction.md)**  
If a merchant refuses or declines a transaction dispute, the case may move to the manual investigation activity.
