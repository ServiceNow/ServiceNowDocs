# Reason codes supported in Dispute Rules Content Pack for Visa

This section lists the Visa reason codes that are supported in Financial Services Operations Dispute Rules Content Pack for Visa.

## About Visa Reason Codes

Reason codes are used by Visa to categorize disputes. The reason codes listed below determine what information needs to be collected from a cardholder and ultimately whether a transaction is eligible to be disputed. These reason codes, the associated questions/ questionnaires, and transaction-level eligibility guidance are all pre-configured in Dispute Management.

|------------------|-----------|--------------------------------|

|EMV Liability Shift Non-Counterfeit Fraud|10.2|120|
|Other Fraud – Card-Present Environment|10.3|120|
|Other Fraud – Card-Absent Environment|10.4|120|
|Visa Fraud Monitoring Program|10.5|120|
|Authorization|
|Card Recovery Bulletin|11.1|75|
|Declined Authorization|11.2|75|
|No Authorization|11.3|75|

|Incorrect Account Number|12.4|120|
|Incorrect Amount|12.5|120|
|Duplicate Processing/Paid by Other Means|12.6|120|
|Invalid Data|12.7|75|
