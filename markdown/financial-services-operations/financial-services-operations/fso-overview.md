# Financial Services

The ServiceNow® Financial Services Operations \(FSO\) solution helps financial services institutions to reduce costs through automation, improve customer satisfaction, and embed risk and compliance controls across workflows. FSO is a cloud-based digital workflow platform that is purpose-built to solve the unique challenges that banks face.
