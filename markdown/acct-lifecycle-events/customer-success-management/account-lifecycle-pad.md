# Configure the account onboarding playbook using Playbooks

Use Playbooks in Workflow Studio to configure the Customer Success Management playbook.

In Playbooks, an activity represents one step in your overall business process. You can sequence many activities together in the stages of your process. A stage is made up of many sequenced activities that are grouped in a logical way. You can add or create your own activity definitions, or delete an activity that is not required. For more details, see [Playbooks](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/yokohama/markdown/build-workflows/workflow-studio/exploring-process-automation-designer.md).
