# Product adoption roadmap

Guide customers through product adoption with roadmaps that define phased approaches, sequence capabilities, and track progress.

Use the product adoption roadmap to create structured plans on how customers can adopt and gain value from purchased products over time. The roadmap displays as a visual board that organizes products and capabilities into lanes with clear sequencing, making it easy to visualize the adoption path. Success Managers can apply standardized templates across customer engagements or create custom roadmaps tailored to specific needs.

Each lane represents a stage \(for example, critical, high, or moderate\), and each card displays adoption score, priority, usage plan, objectives, and expected outcomes. You can use the roadmap to track customer value, promote complete product adoption, and identify expansion opportunities.

With the product adoption roadmap, you can:

-   Create roadmaps from standardized templates or build custom ones tailored to specific needs.
-   Organize products into lanes to show the optimal sequence for implementation and maximum value.
-   Track adoption status through Draft, Published, Canceled, or Retired states.
-   Link products to success objectives and business value metrics.
-   Filter by adoption priority or status to manage complex scenarios with many products.
-   Duplicate roadmaps to create copies for similar customer engagements.
