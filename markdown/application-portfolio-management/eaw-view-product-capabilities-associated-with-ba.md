# View product capabilities associated with a business application

You can view the list of product capabilities associated with business applications, in the Enterprise Architecture Workspace \(EA Workspace\).

## Before you begin

The Product capabilities tab is displayed only when the Model ID detail is also available for the business application.

When the **Model ID** field for a business application is initially empty and later populated, all product capabilities associated with that model ID will automatically appear in the **Product capabilities** related list of the corresponding business application.

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Enterprise Architecture Workspace**.

2.  Open the Portfolio List view by selecting the Portfolio icon \(\[Omitted image "portfolio-icon.png"\] Alt text: Portfolio icon\).

3.  Select the expand row icon \(\[Omitted image "ExpandIcon.png"\] Alt text: Expand Row icon\) next to **Application Portfolio**.

4.  Select **Business Applications**.

5.  Select the business application that you want to view the product capabilities for.

6.  Select the **Product capabilities** tab.\[Omitted image "product-capability-related-list-ba.png"\] Alt text: Product capabilities tab available as a related entity for a business application.


## Result

A list of product capabilities associated with the business application is displayed.
