# Approve or reject a TRM product or product lifecycle request

As an Enterprise Architect, approve or reject a TRM product version request submitted by other users.

## Before you begin

The user must be part of the Enterprise Architect Group.

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_analyst

## About this task

When an Enterprise Architecture user requests for TRM product or TRM product lifecycle approval, an email notification is received by the approver. The approver belongs to the Enterprise Architect group.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Desk** &gt; **My Approvals**.

2.  Select the TRM product or product lifecycle request for which you want to provide approval.

3.  Select **Approve** or **Reject**.


## Result

The requester receives an email notification for the approval or rejection.
