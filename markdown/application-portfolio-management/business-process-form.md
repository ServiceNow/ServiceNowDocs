# Business Process Form

A Business process is a method of related structured tasks performed to complete a specific application service.

Assigned to

</td><td>

Person using or responsible for the item.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Category

</td><td>

Category of the business process.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Fault count

</td><td>

</td></tr><tr><td>

Installed

</td><td>

Date and time when the business application is installed.

-   In Maintenance
-   In Stock
-   Installed
-   On Order
-   Pending Install
-   Pending Repair
-   Retired
-   Stolen
-   None
