# Create new related entities form

Enterprise Architecture \(formerly APM\) enables the Enterprise Architects to associate an architectural artifact to existing elements in the ServiceNow AI Platform, such as business capabilities or business applications. These associations creates a relationship between the artifact and related entities.

## Related Entities form fields

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Architectural Artifact|Name of the architectural artifact.|
|Entity|Type of the business entity. The entity type can be a business application or a business capability.|
|Target Record|Name of the particular business application or business capability to which you want to associate the artifact.|
