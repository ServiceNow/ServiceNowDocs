# Exploring the application portfolio

The Application Portfolio section within the Portfolio page of the Enterprise Architecture Workspace helps you to manage business applications, application services, digital integrations, and digital interfaces.

-   **[Exploring business applications](eaw-manage-business-applications.md)**  
A business application is software used by business users to perform a business function. Classify the applications to maintain an inventory and consolidate the business applications. Analyze, assess, and evaluate the applications across various dimensions and determine the action that you can take for each application.
-   **[AI Control Tower integration with Enterprise Architecture](eaw-aict.md)**  
View and manage the AI systems associated with a business application from the Enterprise Architecture Workspace to understand AI risk and governance status as part of your broader application portfolio.
-   **[Exploring application services](eaw-manage-application-services.md)**  
An application service is a set of interconnected applications and hosts which are configured to offer a service to the organization. An application service is a logical grouping of application functions.
-   **[Exploring digital interfaces in Enterprise Architecture Workspace](eaw-digital-interface.md)**  
Manage all your digital interfaces from the Portfolio page of the Enterprise Architecture Workspace.
-   **[Exploring digital integrations in Enterprise Architecture Workspace](eaw-digital-integration.md)**  
Manage all your digital integrations in the Enterprise Architecture Workspace.
