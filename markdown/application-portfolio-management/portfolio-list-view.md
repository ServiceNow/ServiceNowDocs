# Portfolio list view

As an Enterprise Architect, use the Portfolio list view in the Enterprise Architecture Workspace to manage your business architecture, application portfolio, technology portfolio, information portfolio of your organization and also manage your own entities.

**Note:** You can zoom on this page or any of the child pages to 200% or 400% through your browser settings without the loss of content or functionality. Page layouts are transformed into a vertical, stacked view automatically.

The My Lists view shows a list of records that you have been assigned. Select the filter icon \(![Filter icon](../image/icon-filter.png)\) to check the applied fields.

You can save, filter and export, or email the list. You can also create your own list and access it from the My Lists page.

![Portfolio List view](../image/eaw-image/eaw-portfolio-list-view.png "List view")

**Note:** In the Lists view, the Digital Integrations and Digital Interfaces are displayed only when the Digital Integration Management plugin \(com.snc.apm\_di\) is installed. The Diagrams tab under Architectural Artifacts is displayed in the list only when the Lucidchart Integration plugin \(sn\_lcdchart\_int\) is installed.

## Lists

The Lists view enables you to manage your portfolios and all your own entities.

You can see the following portfolio items in the **Portfolio &gt; List** view:

-   Business Architecture
-   Application Portfolio
-   Information Portfolio
-   My Entities

## My Lists

Any list that you create appear in the My Lists section. Lists in this section are only visible to you.
