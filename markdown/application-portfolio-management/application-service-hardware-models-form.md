# Application Service Hardware Models form

Track your equipment assets such as computers and servers using hardware models.

## Application Service Hardware Models form fields

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Application Service|Name of the application service.|
|Ignore Technical Risk|Option to ignore the technical risk of the hardware model.|
|Hardware Product Model|Name of the model category.|
