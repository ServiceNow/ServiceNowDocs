# View all TRM products grouped by product category

You can view all your Technology Reference Model \(TRM\) products, grouped by category, in the Enterprise Architecture Workspace.

## Before you begin

You can add or hide the displayed columns according to your requirement by selecting the personalize icon \(\[Omitted image "icon-bubblechart-settings.png"\] Alt text: Settings icon.\) and then selecting **Edit columns**.

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspace** &gt; **Enterprise Architecture Workspace**.

2.  Open the Technology Portfolio page by selecting the Technology Portfolio icon \[Omitted image "technology-portfolio-icon.png"\] Alt text: Technology portfolio icon.

3.  In the **TRM catalog** tab, select the expand row icon \(\[Omitted image "ExpandIcon.png"\] Alt text: Expand Row icon\) next to the TRM product category that you want to view the TRM products for.

    A list of TRM products is displayed.
