# Delete a digital integration

Delete a digital integration that you no longer require.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Enterprise Architecture** &gt; **Application Portfolio** &gt; **Digital Integrations**.

2.  Select and open the digital integration to be deleted.

3.  Click **Delete**.

    A confirmation message appears.
