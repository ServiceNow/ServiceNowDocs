# Responding to healthcare requests

As a healthcare agent, you can respond to healthcare requests to resolve issues raised by a patient.

Patients can submit a healthcare request via a phone call or a chat. As a healthcare agent, you can respond to healthcare requests by accepting a work item from a patient or accessing the interaction records that store work items. For more information, see [Interaction Management](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=interaction-management&version=xanadu&pubname=xanadu-servicenow-platform&ft:locale=en-US).
