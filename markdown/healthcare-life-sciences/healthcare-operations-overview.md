# Healthcare Operations

Streamline and optimize your care team operational tasks with the ServiceNow® Healthcare Operations applications.

Healthcare Operations includes the following applications:

-   **[Healthcare Operations Core](hcls-cto-app.md)**

    Provides the foundation to enable hospitals to streamline and automate their operations.

-   **[Care Team Operations for Healthcare IT](hcls-cto-it-app.md)**

    Contains the case types to report and fulfill healthcare IT issues.

-   **[Care Team Operations for Biomed](care-team-operations-for-biomed.md)**

    Contains the case types to report and fulfill biomed issues.

-   **[Care Team Operations for Environmental Services](cto-evs-landing.md)**

    Contains the case types to report and fulfill environmental services issues.

-   **[Care Team Operations for Facilities](cto-facilities-landing.md)**

    Contains the case types to report and fulfill facilities issues.
