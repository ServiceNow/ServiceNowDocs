# Performance management: Metric collection

Performance management enables you to capture and analyze operational metrics to identify anomalies. Detected anomalies can generate alerts that surface in Service Operations Workspace, helping you avoid service outages.ServiceNow

ServiceNow uses out-of-the box solutions to collect metrics and monitor your devices.

For Cisco Meraki device collection, see [Cisco Meraki installed integrations](meraki-installed-integrations.md).

For Fortinet device collection, see [Fortinet installed integrations](fortinet-installed-integrations.md).
