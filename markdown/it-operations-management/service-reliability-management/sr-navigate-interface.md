# SRM interface

The different components on the SRM interface provide easy access to different functions. The features and functions that appear depend on your role and permissions.

The SRM interface has the following different sections:

**Note:** If you have other SOW applications, and depending on your assigned roles, that homepage may not be the SRM homepage, as described. It is the SOW homepage instead, with SRM alerts and incidents included in your metrics. If you have other roles, like itil, you may see additional icons in the left navigation pane.

In that case, to view SRM specific areas, select an SRM module.

![Alerts, incidents, and changes](../image/sr-ui.png "Interface sections")
