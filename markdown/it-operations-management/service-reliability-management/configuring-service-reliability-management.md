# Configuring Service Reliability Management

Configuring Service Reliability Management involves installing it from the ServiceNow Store or from the Admin Center. You can assigning administrators, and importing services and teams.

## Configuration checklist

## Using guided setup to implement SRM

Guided setup provides a sequence of tasks that help you configure SRM on your instance. To open guided setup for SRM, navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Operations Workspace** &gt; **Overview** and select **Configure**.

![Service Operations Management tile](../../it-operations-management/image/som-config.png)

1.  [Install SRM from Admin Center or ServiceNow Store](../task/sr-install-sr-store-app.md)  
Install the SRM application from the Admin Center or ServiceNow Store.
2.  [Assign an administrator to Service Reliability Management](../task/sr-assign-admins.md)  
Add people from your ServiceNow instance to administer the \( your service operations.
3.  [Import services for use in Service Reliability Management](../task/sr-import-services.md)  
Filter and import services on ServiceNow's platform to be managed in the Service Operations Management applications.
4.  [Activate teams and services in SRM](../task/sr-activate-teams-and-services-in-srm.md)  
Activate teams and services on ServiceNow's platform to be managed in the SRM application.
5.  [Customize service approval settings in SRM](../task/sr-add-approval-services.md)  
Add an approval process to your services for governance.
6.  [Customize team approval settings in SRM](../task/sr-add-approval-teams.md)  
Add an approval process to your Service Reliability Management teams for governance.
7.  [Import teams to Service Reliability Management](../task/sr-import-teams.md)  

8.  [Edit and publish a team request form for SRM](../task/sr-edit-team-request-form.md)  
Edit a team request form to customize team requests to your organization's preference.
9.  [Configure error budget actions when an SRM SLO is breached](../task/sr-configure-error-budget.md)  
Configure the actions the team can select when the error budget of a service level objective \(SLO\) is breached.
10. [Approve a change request for SRM team approval](../task/sr-approve-change-req.md)  
Approve a change request when the change is reviewed and approved by someone in the Approver role.
11. [Configure Twilio to send notifications in SRM](../task/sr-add-twilio-key.md)  
Establish a connection between your SRM instance and your Twilio account so your teams can receive notifications.
12. [Install the Alert automation rules application](../task/sr-install-aa-store-app.md)  
Install the **Alert automation rules** application and any supporting applications.
