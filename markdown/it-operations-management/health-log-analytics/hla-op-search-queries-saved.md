# Use or modify a saved search

Use a saved search of log data to better understand the causes of an alert. As the owner of a saved search, you can modify the search values and save your changes.

## Before you begin

Role required: evt\_mgmt\_operator or evt\_mgmt\_admin

If you are not the owner of the saved search, save the search with a different name. You can then update search values, save your changes, and share the search with others.

**Important:** From the Vancouver release onward, the **Operator Workspace** is deprecated and replaced with Service Operations Workspace. For the new procedure, see the corresponding topic in the Service Operations Workspace for ITOM documentation: [Use or modify a saved search](hla-op-search-queries-saved-sow.md).
