# Now Assist for Setup \(ITOM\)

The Admin Home page provides a centralized workspace for managing, monitoring, and configuring your ServiceNow instance. From a single view, administrators can access product setup, track operational health, and stay informed about instance status and AI readiness.

The Manage your products section organizes your licensed ServiceNow products into dedicated spaces for focused setup and configuration. Select any product family — such as Core Business Suite, IT Operations Management, Customer Service Management, or Now Assist — to access tailored administrative tools and overviews.

![Admin Home page showing the Manage your products section with cards for Core Business Suite, IT Operations Management, Customer Service Management, and Now Assist.](../image/nowassist-setup-admin-home-page.png)

The shared admin dashboard surfaces real-time operational metrics so you can monitor the health of your instance at a glance. Key indicators include open incidents, request items, problems, pending approvals, change activity, and your hardening compliance score.

![Shared admin dashboard displaying operational metrics including open incidents, open request items, problems, compliance score, and customer actions.](../image/nowassist-setup-shared-admin-dashboard.png)

The Get information about your instance section gives you a snapshot of your instance's current state, including your ServiceNow version and upgrade readiness, the number of installed versus available apps, adoption blueprint progress, and a summary of your Now Assist skills by status.

![Instance information section showing upgrade status, entitled apps count, adoption blueprints, and Now Assist skill summary.](../image/nowassist-setup-instance-info.png)
