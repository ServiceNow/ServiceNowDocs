# ServiceNow keyboard shortcuts

ServiceNow strives to incorporate accessibility features into its AI Platform and products. Keyboard shortcuts play a crucial role in ensuring that users can navigate and utilize our tools efficiently. Explore the links in this topic to learn more.

Keyboard shortcuts are a crucial aspect of digital accessibility, enabling users to navigate and interact with digital products more efficiently. For individuals with disabilities, keyboard shortcuts can be particularly empowering, allowing them to bypass cumbersome navigation and access content with greater ease. By providing alternative ways to perform common tasks, keyboard shortcuts promote independence and equal access to information.

These shortcuts can be designed and implemented to support users with diverse needs. This includes users with mobility or dexterity impairments, visual impairments, or those who simply prefer keyboard navigation.

This page is a helpful resource that collects links to documentation about the keyboard shortcuts available for our products and features. Whether you want to work more efficiently or need alternative ways to navigate due to accessibility needs, you will find useful information here to improve your experience.
