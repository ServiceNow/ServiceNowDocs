# Onboard a Supplier form

Use the Onboard a Supplier form to provide details about the supplier that you want to onboard.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Supplier Name|Name of the supplier.|
|Supplier DUNS|Unique numeric identifier for every supplier.|
|Primary contact first name|First name of the supplier primary contact.|
|Primary contact last name|Last name of the supplier primary contact.|
|Primary contact email|Email of the supplier primary contact.|
|Primary contact title|Title of the supplier primary contact.|
