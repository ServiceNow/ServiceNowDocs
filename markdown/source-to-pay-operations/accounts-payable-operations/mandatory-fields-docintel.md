# Required fields for creating an invoice

The Accounts Payable Operations integration with Document Intelligence application checks whether the required fields are populated on the invoice and invoice lines for creating the invoice.

|Required field for invoice|Required field for invoice line|
|--------------------------|-------------------------------|
|Supplier invoice number|Line description|
|Invoice date|Line amount|
|Invoice amount|Line unit price|
|Supplier|Invoiced quantity|
