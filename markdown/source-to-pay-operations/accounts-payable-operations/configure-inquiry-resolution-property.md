# Configure inquiry resolution property

Configure the inquiry resolution AI agent to extract data from KB articles.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

![APO properties page](../image/apo-properties.png)

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Accounts Payable Operations** &gt; **Properties**.

    The Accounts Payable Operations properties page appears.

2.  In the **Inquiry resolution properties** area, enter the title of the knowledge article that the AI agent uses to extract data and provide resolution for the inquiry case.

3.  Select **Save**.


## Result

The AI agent uses the data from the specified KB article to resolve the inquiry case.
