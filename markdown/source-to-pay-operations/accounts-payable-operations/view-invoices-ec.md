# View your invoices on Employee Center

As a business owner, you can view all the invoices that you own on Employee Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_shop.invoice\_owner

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  In the My active items widget, select **Invoices**.

    The Invoice List page is displayed, which lists all the invoices that you own.

    In the Number column, select the link to an invoice to view its details.
