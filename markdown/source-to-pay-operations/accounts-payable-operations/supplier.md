# Supplier

Field descriptions for the \[sn\_fin\_supplier\] table used to manage supplier records and associated products in primary data.

## sn\_fin\_supplier table

You can add suppliers and their products to populate your primary data. For more information on supplier. see [Manage supplier details](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/source-to-pay-operations/supplier-lifecycle-operations/supp-ws-details-page.md).

|Field|Data type|Description|
|-----|---------|-----------|
|Legal name|String|Legal name of the supplier that corresponds to its operating location.|
