# Customize your top suppliers in Shopping Hub

Personalize the list of suppliers to see on your Shopping Hub homepage. Update your list with the change in your preferences anytime.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_shop.shopper

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **ShoppingHub** &gt; **ShoppingHub Home**.

    You can see the Shopping Hub homepage.

2.  Scroll down on the homepage where you can see the list of suggested suppliers based on your role.

3.  Select the more options icon and select **Personalize**.

4.  From the list of suppliers, select those that you want to see on your Shopping Hub homepage.

    You can select up to 10 suppliers.

5.  Select **Save**.
