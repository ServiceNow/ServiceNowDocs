# Add products to your shopping cart

Use your shopping cart to add multiple products that you have shortlisted and wish to purchase when you are done shopping on Shopping Hub.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_shop.shopper

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **ShoppingHub** &gt; **ShoppingHub Home**.

2.  Browse for the required goods or services from the **Categories** or the **Suppliers** list on the Shopping Hub homepage.

    For more information, see [Order a product](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/source-to-pay-operations/sourcing-and-procurement-operations/order-a-product.md)

3.  Select **Add to cart**.


## What to do next

When you are done adding products to your cart, you can review your shopping cart and proceed to final checkout. For more information, see [Review your shopping cart](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/source-to-pay-operations/sourcing-and-procurement-operations/review-shopping-cart.md).
