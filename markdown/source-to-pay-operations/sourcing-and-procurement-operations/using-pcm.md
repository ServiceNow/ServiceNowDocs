# Using Procurement Case Management

With the Procurement Case Management application, you can enable your employees to submit procurement cases and access your organization's knowledge base.

-   **[Create a procurement case for a service](../task/create-procurement-request.md)**  
Create a procurement case for a service that you wish to procure. The procurement organization receives your details from the request and works toward its fulfillment.
-   **[Create a task from a procurement case](../task/assign-procurement-task.md)**  
Create a task directly from a procurement case. You can also create associated tasks and assign these tasks to fulfill the request.
-   **[Create a knowledge base article about procurement](../task/create-kb-article.md)**  
Create a knowledge base article about procurement so that you can share procurement information and policies with your employees and procurement team.
