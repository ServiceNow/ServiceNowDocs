</td><td>

Specific lever that produced the savings finding. The available values are:-   **Price Escalations &amp; Renewal Caps**
-   **Payment Terms Optimization**
-   **Non-preferred Supplier Spend**
-   **Unconsolidated Spend \(Single Supplier, Multiple BU Contracts\)**
-   **Spend Fragmentation \(Multi-Supplier in a Category\)**
-   **Off-Contract Spend**

</td><td>

Choice

</td><td>

Choice

</td></tr><tr><td>

**Confidence Score**

</td><td>

Numeric confidence value assigned by the AI model to indicate how strongly the recommendation is supported by the underlying data.

</td><td>

Integer

</td></tr><tr><td>

**Complexity**

</td><td>

Effort estimate to action the opportunity, calculated based on factors such as the number of suppliers or purchase orders involved, for example **Low**, **Medium**, or **High**.

</td><td>

Choice

</td></tr><tr><td>

**Spend Categories**

</td><td>

One or more spend categories associated with the opportunity. Access control on the table uses this field to scope opportunities to category managers who own the relevant category.

Glide list

Glide list
