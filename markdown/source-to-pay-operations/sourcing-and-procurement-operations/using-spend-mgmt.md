# Using Spend and Savings Management

Use Spend and Savings Management to import and manage your organization's category taxonomy.

-   **[Map spend categories to product categories using an Excel file](map-spend-product-categories-excel.md)**  
You can map spend categories to product categories using an Excel file. This process enables you to organize and manage your organization’s expenses better by grouping similar goods or services into logical categories.
-   **[Map spend categories to product categories manually](../task/map-spend-product-categories.md)**  
You can manually map spend categories to product categories to organize and manage your organization’s expenses better by grouping similar goods or services into logical categories.
