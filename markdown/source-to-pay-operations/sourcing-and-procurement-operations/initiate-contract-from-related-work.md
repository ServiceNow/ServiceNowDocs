# Initiate a contract request from the related work section

Initiate a contract request for purchase lines, purchase requisitions, sourcing requests, negotiations, or negotiation events from the related work section.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the **Source-to-Pay Workspace**.

2.  Open the entity for which you have to initiate a contract request.

    **Note:** From the related work section, contract requests can be initiated for purchase lines, purchase requisitions, sourcing requests, negotiations, and negotiation events.

3.  Navigate to **Related work** &gt; **Contract Requests**.

4.  Select **New**.

5.  Provide the details about the contract that you have to initiate.

6.  Select **Initiate**.
