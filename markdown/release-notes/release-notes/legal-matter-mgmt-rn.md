# Legal Matter Management release notes

The ServiceNow® Legal Matter Management application enables you to complete legal requests that need cross-departmental tasking and provides a workflow with a mechanism to store supporting documents and track important milestones. Legal Matter Management was enhanced and updated in the Australia release.

## Legal Matter Management highlights for the Australia release

Help ensure that sensitive information remains confidential with stakeholder controls through attorney-client privilege \(ACP\) protection for legal requests and matters.

See [Legal Matter Management](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=legal-matter-management-landing-page&version=australia&pubname=australia-employee-service-management&ft:locale=en-US) for more information.

**Important:** Legal Matter Management is available in the ServiceNow Store. For details, see the "Activation information" section of these release notes.

## New in the Australia release

-   **[Attorney-Client Privilege](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=lsd-acp-landing&version=australia&pubname=australia-employee-service-management&ft:locale=en-US)**

    Protect sensitive legal information by marking legal matters as Privileged &amp; Confidential, helping to ensure access is restricted to only authorized stakeholders.


## Activation information

Install Legal Matter Management by requesting it from the ServiceNow Store. Visit the [ServiceNow Store](https://store.servicenow.com/sn_appstore_store.do#!/store/home) to view all the available apps, and for information about submitting requests to the store. For cumulative release notes information for all released apps, see the [ServiceNow Store version history release notes](https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/store-release-notes/page/release-notes/store/sn-store-release-notes.html).
