# Employee Service Management release notes

Employee Service Management has new and updated features in the Xanadu release

-   **[Employee Center release notes](employee-center-rn.md)**  
The ServiceNow® Employee Center application is a multi-department, dynamic portal for service delivery and employee engagement. Employee Center was enhanced and updated in the Xanadu release.
-   **[HR Service Delivery release notes](../hr-service-delivery/hr-service-delivery-landing.md)**  
The ServiceNow® HR Service Delivery application improves the employee service experience by automating HR interactions and providing a single platform for all HR services. HR Service Delivery was enhanced and updated in the Xanadu release.
-   **[Talent Development release notes](employee-growth-development-landing.md)**  
The ServiceNow® Talent Development application enables managers and employees to identify and track career growth and development opportunities. It supports career growth through conversations, talking points, and ongoing development tracking. Talent Development was enhanced and updated in the Xanadu release.
-   **[Skills Intelligence release notes](skills-intelligence-rn.md)**  
The ServiceNow® Skills Foundation application provides an overview of skills-related data and enables admins to work on Job architecture data from a single location. Skills Foundation was enhanced and updated in the Xanadu release to provide enhanced visibility into employee skill activities across a range of Talent Development products.
