# Healthcare and Life Sciences release notes

Healthcare and Life Sciences has new and updated features in the Xanadu release.

-   **[Healthcare and Life Sciences release notes](healthcare-life-sciences-rn-landing.md)**  
Healthcare and Life Sciences has new and updated features in the Xanadu release.
-   **[Healthcare and Life Sciences Service Management Core release notes](hcls-core-rn.md)**  
The ServiceNow® Healthcare and Life Sciences Service Management application streamlines healthcare operations with automated processes. Healthcare and Life Sciences Service Management was enhanced and updated in the Xanadu release.
-   **[Healthcare Computerized Maintenance Management System release notes](healthcare-cmms-rn.md)**  
The ServiceNow® Healthcare Computerized Maintenance Management System application manages the medical device-related in-service process, alternative equipment maintenance \(AEM\) review process, medical device issues, and out-of-service process. This application is currently supported for the Xanadu release, but it will be deprecated in a future release.
