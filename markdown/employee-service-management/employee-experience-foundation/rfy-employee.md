# View the Recommended for you content

Access all the content recommended for you from from a central location in one-click.

As an employee, you can access the recommended knowledge articles or catalog items from the **Recommended for you** widget.

-   Knowledge articles recommendations are based on the view count of article.
-   Catalog item recommendations are based on the number of times it is requested for.
-   The recommendations associated to the portal taxonomy are displayed on the home page.



For more information, see [Recommended for you](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/rfy-ec.md)
