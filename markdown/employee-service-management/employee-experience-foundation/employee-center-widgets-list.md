# Employee Center widgets

You can use base system widgets as-is in your Employee Center or clone them to suit your own business needs.

This section provides a quick overview of the widgets. To see the full list of widgets, you can navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Portal** &gt; **Widgets**.

## Employee Profile

|Widget|ID|Description|
|------|---|-----------|
|Employee Profile Details|employee\_profile\_details|Displays the basic profile and work- related information of an employee.|
|Employee Profile Header|employee\_profile\_header|Appears across the employee profile page.|
|Employee Profile My Team|employee\_profile\_my\_team|Displays my team information.|
|Employee Profile Overview|employee-profile-overview|Displays the employee profile overview.|
|Public Profile Visibility|public\_profile\_visibility|Shows the visibility setting for the profile. You can modify the visibility settings based on your needs.|

For more information, see [Employee profile](employee-profile-portal.md).

## Employee Center Pro Kiosk widgets

|Widget|ID|Description|
|------|---|-----------|
|Kiosk Header|eck-header|Appears across the kiosk portal and provides quick access to My requests, My favories, Employee profile, and so on.|
|Employee Center Pro Kiosk Portal login|ec-pro-kiosk-portal-login|Enables the login and access to the kiosk portal.|
|Employee Upcoming Shift|employee-upcoming-shift|Shows upcoming events.|
|Timeoff Accrual Balance|timeoff-accrual-balance|Shows the time off and leave balances.|
|Timeoff Accrual Balance - Home Page|timeoff-accrual-balance-home-page|Shows the time off and leave balances home page.|
|Preferences|preferences|Shows the user preferences such as accessibility and language.|
