# Schedule appointment form

Use the Schedule appointment form to select a reason and provide contact information for your appointment.

<table id="table_uvb_bwz_mdc"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Appointment category

</td><td>

Category for the appointment.As an admin, you can enable this field from the instance options.

**Note:** The category might already be selected based on your entry point.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Appointment reason

</td><td>

Reason for scheduling the appointment.You might have to select a category before selecting a reason based on the configuration set by the admin.

**Note:** The reason might already be selected based on your entry point.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Appointment type

</td><td>

Type of appointment that you want to schedule.The selected appointment reason might support only virtual or in-person appointments based on the configuration set by the admin.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Appointment location

</td><td>

Office location for your appointment.You might not be able to select a location if you’ve reached the maximum number of appointments.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Appointment

</td><td>

Time slot for your appointment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Preferred contact method

</td><td>

Contact method that is used to send notifications related to the appointment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Contact detail

</td><td>

Phone number or email that receives the notifications related to the appointment.

</td></tr></tbody>
