# Facebook Workplace content

Create content for your company's Facebook page as another avenue to reach your employees.

**Note:** Facebook Workplace ServiceNow Store will not be available for download from the store starting September 2023.

## Create or edit Facebook content

Create content for your company's Facebook page.

### Before you begin

Role required: sn\_cd.content\_manager, sn\_cd.content\_admin

### Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Content Publishing** &gt; **Content Library**.

2.  Select **New** or existing content.

3.  Select **Facebook** under the **Select the Platform** column.

4.  Select **Facebook Workplace post** under the **Choose a content format** section.

5.  Select **Continue**.

6.  Fill in the fields in the **Facebook Workplace Content** form.
