# Setup browse experience features

Make it easy for employees to navigate the portal and discover content using the browse experience features in Employee Center.

The video provides an overview of the cluster of features available with browse experience in Employee Center. 

|Feature|Description|
|-------|-----------|
|[Unified Taxonomy for Employee Center](config-taxonomy.md)|Unified Taxonomy is a collection of hierarchical topics that brings together different content types, such as requests, articles, quick links, learning content, and employee communications.|
|[Configure Mega menu](../task/config-mega-menu.md)|The mega menu enables easy navigation to a topic and its child topic. The mega menu is comprised of the topics and child topics in theUnified Taxonomy.|
|[Advanced Portal Navigation](adv-portal-navigation-overview.md)|Configure a primary navigation bar of topics and service portal pages.|
|[Guided Self-Service in Employee Center](gss-guided-self-service-overview.md)|Empower employees with easier self-serve capabilities through guided experiences to improve productivity and efficiency.|
|[Dynamic topic pages](dynamic-topic-page.md)|Dynamic topic pages are portal pages accessible from the mega menu or popular topic widgets that bring together different types of content, such as request, articles, and quick links.|
|[Cross-channel favorites](app-launcher-usability.md)|Cross-channel favorites offer quick access to frequently used content for employees, accessible from various widgets and pages, with sorting and customization options|
