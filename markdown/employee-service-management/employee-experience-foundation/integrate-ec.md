# Integrating with Employee Center

Leverage the benefits of Employee Center-integrated solutions to further enhance your employee experience.

-   **[Approval hub integrations](approvals-int-concept.md)**  
The Approval Hub integration with different external applications enable different access points to fulfill organizational approval tasks.
-   **[Employee Profile integration with Microsoft Teams](employee-profile-integration-with-microsoft-teams.md)**  
Employee Profile provides integration with Microsoft Teams that show the current statuses of employees on their respective Employee Profile pages itself.
-   **[Employee Center for Microsoft Viva Connections](../../sn-ms-viva-integrations/concept/sn-ms-viva-integrations.md)**  
The Employee Center for Microsoft Viva Connections integration extends the ServiceNow for Microsoft Teams integration, bringing the ServiceNow employee experience into the Microsoft Viva Connections dashboard.
-   **[Employee Center for Zoom](../task/zoom-ec-create-app.md)**  
Integrate Employee Center with a Zoom account for your organization employees so they can use Employee Center capabilities from within the Zoom client application.
