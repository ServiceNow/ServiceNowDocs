# Setup task management and integration features

Enhance employee productivity and drive portal consolidation.

|Feature|Description|
|-------|-----------|
|Recommended features|
|[My active items widget configuration](my-activity-config.md)|The My Active Items widget displays user activities requiring input or review, organizes items by priority, and provides summary and detailed views.|
|[Employee tasks page](employee-center-to-dos-page-configuration.md)|Enable employees to view and complete all their tasks on a single page|
|[Employee requests page](employee-center-requests-page-configuration.md)|Enable employees to view all their requests on a single page in the Employee Center.|
|[Action framework](action-group-framework.md)|Define actions items for quick access.|
|[Employee profile](employee-profile-portal.md)|An Employee Profile is a comprehensive, unified view of an employee's work-related information that enhances business decisions, workflows, and personalization across applications.|
|[App Launcher](web-application-employee-about.md) \(Employee Center Pro\)|The App Launcher widget provides employees with one-click access to all their web business applications, both SSO-integrated and custom non-SSO applications.|
|Expand portal capabilities with the following features:|
|[Approvals hub](approval-hub-intro.md) \(Employee Center Pro\)|The Approvals hub enables approvers to make informed and prioritized approval decisions by providing in-context information and a consistent layout for different approval types.|
|[Organization chart in Employee Center Pro](employee-profile-org-chart.md) \(Employee Center Pro\)|The organization chart enables employees to find information about other employees and understand the team structure.|
