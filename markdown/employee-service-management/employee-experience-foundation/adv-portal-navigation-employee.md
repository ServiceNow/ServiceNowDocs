# View advanced portal navigation

Use the enhanced navigation experience that helps you with better content discovery.

You can easily navigate to the content that is organized to serve your content needs.

![Standard and expanded layouts](../images/ec-ic-architecture-megamenu.png "Out-of-the-box mega menu architecture options")

-   Navigate to the right content with fewer clicks.
-   View the topics and menu items on the primary navigation bar.
-   Consume the display preferences for the root-level topics or menu items that are custom-served to suit your needs.

For more information on how to configure the advanced portal navigation, see [Configure advanced portal navigation order](../task/config-adv-portal-nav.md).
