# Employee Center Pro features and interface

ServiceNow Employee Center portal is packaged as two separate ServiceNow Store applications: Employee Center and Employee Center Pro.

## Employee Center Pro home page

Employee Center Pro overview 

Employee Center Pro extends the standard Employee Center capabilities and provides additional features, such as:

-   Actionable, targeted campaigns
-   Enterprise search
-   Employee communities

![Employee Center Pro home page with the corresponding functionalities listed out.](../images/ec-pro-home24.png "EC Pro feature summary")

-   **Enhanced home page theme**: Unifies various types of content, such as requests, articles, and quick links. Theme aligns with the latest sets components, a new color scheme, a logo update, and CSS variable adjustments for better browsing experience.
-   **Header enhancement**: Helps users navigate to the tasks, requests, favorites, and search effectively.
-   **Full page search experience**: Offers a conversational search experience in a dynamic dialogue to gather information, provide assistance, or guide through a process using the sparkle icon search.

## Content Experiences widget \(Employee Center Pro\)

Content Experiences widget is designed to support multiple content types, such as banners, announcements, videos, and events. It can be easily configured and adjusted to different layouts. This widget also acts as the unifying interface for multiple content types and announcement configurations for all shapes, sizes, and spaces on a given portal page. For more information, see [Creating campaigns with Content Experiences](ecpro-campaigns.md).

![Content Experiences widget on portal.](../images/content-exp-widget.jpg "Content Experiences widget")

## Integration-powered capabilities \(Employee Center Pro\)

Access all your applications from a central location with a single click with App Launcher. View the entire list of both single sign-on \(SSO\) and non-SSO applications in one place. You can access the applications from the Employee Center home page widget in context from dynamic topic pages and through search. For more information, see [App Launcher](web-application-employee-about.md).

![My applications displayed on the Home page and topic pages.](../images/app-launcher-24.png "App Launcher")

Enable the approver with precise information to make informed and prioritized approvals. Improve productivity with a unified approval task list where you can manage internal and external approvals in one location. For more information, see [Use approval experience](../task/ec-to-dos-use-approval-hub.md).

![Tasks and approvals on the EC portal.](../images/tasks-approvals24.png "Tasks and approvals")

## Enterprise search \(Employee Center Pro\)

Utilize the enterprise AI-driven search that enables employees to find critical information like leave balance, improve content discovery with search on topic pages and learning-based recommendations for self-solving minor issues, accessing and using SharePoint Online Search Connector with Employee Center Pro. For more information, see [SharePoint Online Search Connector](../../sharepoint-online-search/concept/sharepoint-search-overview.md).

![Search results on SharePoint Online Search Connector.](../../sharepoint-online-search/image/sp-search.png "Search results on the SharePoint Online Search Connector")

## Employee Center Pro Kiosk

Employee Center Pro Kiosk enables all the deskless workforce with simplified access to information and services for self-serve and improved productivity.

![Kiosk home page](../images/deskless-homepage-eck.png "Employee Center Pro Kiosk home page")

Using the kiosk portal, deskless workers can manage their time off, schedule, and requests. For more information, see [Employee Center Pro Kiosk](deskless-kiosk-overview.md).
