# View connected Zoom accounts

View the list of Zoom accounts connected to your ServiceNow® instance.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Log in to your ServiceNow instance.

2.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Employee Center for Zoom** &gt; **Connected Zoom accounts**.

    On the Installed Accounts form, the Zoom accounts connected to the ServiceNow instance are listed.

3.  Select any Account ID value to see the associated Zoom account details.
