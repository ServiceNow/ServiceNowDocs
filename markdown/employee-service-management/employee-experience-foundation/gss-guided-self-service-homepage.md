# Use Guided Self-Service

Use the Guided Self-Service experience to self-serve, leading to improved productivity, and operational efficiencies for the organization.​

With an in-context visual way to navigate the complex work scenarios, you can enhance productivity, engagement, and contribute to the overall organizational efficiency.

-   Self-serve with easy access to information, processes, and resolutions for day-to-day work.
-   Discover and use the available GSS process from the search.
-   Navigate to the right article or catalog form through a visual QnA experience.
-   Access information, services, and technology intuitively​.

As an employee, you can select options, view or modify the choices, restart activities, and start entire playbook all over again. Here is the intuitive step-by-step flow for the use cases.

-   Start afresh with context when you return to a partially completed GSS flow.
-   Ensure you complete the entire GSS flow in one-go.
-   Go back, resume, view, and revise your selections.
-   Access a catalog or a KB article based on your permissions.

**Note:** Your incomplete selections are cancelled daily by a schedule job.
