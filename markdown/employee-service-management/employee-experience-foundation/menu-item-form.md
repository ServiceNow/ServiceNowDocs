# Menu Item form

Use the Menu Items form to configure the items for the **More** menu on the Employee Center header.

Add any new menu items to the EC Header under the **More** option.

**Note:** Currently, Filtered or Scripted menu items are not supported as Filtered or Scripted List must be top-level Menu Item.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Label|Name that appears for the item in the menu.|
|Parent Menu|This field should already contain the name of the menu you are adding items to.|
|Order|Value that determines where the item appears in the menu in relation to other menu items.|
|Type|The type of page the item links to. For example, you can link to another page in the portal or an external URL. Form fields vary depending on the option you select from this list.|
|Application|Application for the menu item|
|Page|Name of the portal page the item links to.|
|Condition|Determines what conditions are required for menu items to show in the header.|
|Glyph|Icon that appears next to the menu item.|
