# Configuring Employee Center

Set up and configure Employee Center to provide an employee-centric and multi-department service delivery experience.

Employee Center is available on ServiceNow® instances that are on version Rome or newer. Employee Center includes certain out-of-the-box configurations that can be used as-is or customized. You can install additional plugins and configure features to enhance the employee portal experience.

-   [Configure users and roles](emp-center-personas.md)

    Learn about the roles necessary for using Employee Center features.

-   [Understanding Employee Center plugins](install-plugins.md)

    Discover the Employee Center plugins that are included out-of-the-box on a new ServiceNow® instance, as well as plugins that are installed when you upgrade to Employee Center Pro.

    For the steps to upgrade to Employee Center Pro, see [Install Employee Center Pro](../task/install-ec-pro.md).
