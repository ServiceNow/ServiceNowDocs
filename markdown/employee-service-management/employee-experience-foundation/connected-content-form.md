# Connected Content form

Use the connected content form to associate content to the topics in your taxonomy.

<table id="table_nyf_nvh_sqb"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Content Type

</td><td>

The type of content you want to associate with a topic. The choices are:-   **Catalog Item**
-   **Knowledge**
-   **Quick Link**

</td></tr><tr><td>

Catalog item

</td><td>

The catalog item you want to associate with the topic. This field appears only when **Catalog Item** is selected from **Content Type** field.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Knowledge

</td><td>

The knowledge article you want to associate with the topic. This field appears only when **Knowledge** is selected from **Content Type** field.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Quick link

</td><td>

The quick link you want to associate with the topic. This field appears only when **Quick Link** is selected from **Content Type** field.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Topic

</td><td>

The topic that you want to associate the content with.

</td></tr></tbody>
