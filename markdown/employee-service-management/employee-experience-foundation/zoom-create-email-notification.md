# Use Employee Center from Zoom

Access and use the ServiceNow® Employee Center capabilities from Zoom client.

## Before you begin

Only if your admin has connected the Zoom account to ServiceNow instance, you \(employee\) can access ServiceNow Employee Center in the Zoom application or you can install ServiceNow Employee Center from Zoom App Marketplace.

Role required: Employee

## Procedure

1.  Log in to your Zoom application.

2.  Navigate to **Apps** &gt; **Discover**.

3.  Search for and select **ServiceNow Employee Center**.

4.  Enter your ServiceNow login credentials and select **Log in**.

    ![ServiceNow Employee Center portal in Zoom app](../images/zoom-sn-ec-portal.png)

    The ServiceNow Employee Center homepage is displayed.
