# Opt in to the enhanced Enhanced Requests Experience

Opt in to the Enhanced Requests Experience, as an existing customer, to get the benefits of the updated features.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_sp.esc\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Employee Center** &gt; **Requests** &gt; **Set up**.

2.  Check the **Yes, opt in to enhanced request experience** box and select the **Opt-in** button.


## Result

You have opted in to the Enhanced Requests Experience.
