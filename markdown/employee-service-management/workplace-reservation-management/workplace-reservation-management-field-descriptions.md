# Workplace Reservation Management - Field descriptions

Find the field descriptions of forms used in the Workplace Reservation Management application.

-   **[Add a room form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/workplace-reservation-management/add-room-form.md)**
