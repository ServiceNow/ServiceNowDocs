# Approve or reject a permit

Review the permit and approve or reject it through the employee center.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hs\_rm.permit\_approver

## About this task

Once the permit requestor submits the permit-to-work request, the permit approver can review it through the employee center or the workspace. After assessing the risks associated with the job and validating that the safety parameters are in place, the approver can approve or reject the request.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee center** &gt; **Health and Safety**.

2.  Select **My tasks** to view the permit approvals under the **Open** tab.

3.  Open the permit link to review the permit details.

4.  Add comments on the approve request and approve or reject the permit request.
