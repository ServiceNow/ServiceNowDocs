# Create and manage a safety inspection

Create a safety inspection to manage your workplace inspection of a particular activity, process, or an area. Review the survey results and add findings, root cause analyses \(RCA\), and actions for the inspection.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hs\_rm.safety\_inspection\_manager and sn\_hs\_rm.safety\_inspection\_writer

## About this task

You can also set up an inspection schedule to create safety inspections. For more information, see [Set up a safety inspection schedule](hs-schedule-recurring-safety-inspection.md).

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Health and Safety** &gt; **Health and Safety Workspace**.

2.  Select the risk management icon \(![Risk assessment icon](../image/icon-risk-assessment.png)\).

3.  Select the **Inspections** list and then **All**.

4.  Select **New** to create an inspection.

5.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    Enter details such as inspection format, survey to use for this inspection, risk matrix used for risk assessments for this inspection, and a due date to complete the survey inspection.

    For more information on inspection field descriptions, see [Inspection schedule form](../reference/hs-inspection-schedule-form.md).

6.  Select **Save**.

    -   The **Inspection survey results**, **Findings and RCA**, and **Inspection actions** tabs appear for this inspection.
    -   The associated inspection survey is assigned for completion and appears in the Employee Center and Now Mobile app of the assigned user. The assigned user receives a notification about the inspection survey. For more information, see [Complete a safety inspection survey](hs-complete-safety-insp-survey.md).
7.  If needed, complete the inspection survey in the inspection record by selecting **Open inspection survey**.

    The **Open inspection survey** button appears only when **Inspection format** is set as **Digital**.

8.  In the **Inspection survey results** tab, review the survey responses.

    When completed, the survey results for this inspection are available in the **Inspection survey results** tab.

9.  In the **Findings and RCA** tab, work on the inspection findings, and perform an RCA for this inspection.

    For more information, see [Add a finding and root cause analysis for a safety inspection](hs-add-finding-rca-safety-inspection.md).

10. If applicable, add actions for findings in the **Inspection actions** tab.

    For more information, see [Add an action for a safety inspection or audit](hs-add-action-safety-inspection.md).

11. In the **Details** tab, set the state to Closed Complete after all inspection activities are complete and then select **Save**.

    The inspection moves to the Closed Complete state.
