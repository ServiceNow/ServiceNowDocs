# Create a Mentoring relationship

As a manager, create a mentoring relationship between two of your employees.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ecn.mentoring\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Mentoring** &gt; **Relationships**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the Mentoring relationship form, fill in the fields.

    For a description of the field values, see [Mentoring relationship form](../reference/mentoring-relationship-form.md).

4.  Select **Save**.
