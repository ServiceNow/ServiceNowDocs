# Search for a mentor

Search for mentors by name, job profile, or any other criterion you wish to. You can also use additional filters to search from the list of mentors.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Your career** &gt; **Mentoring** &gt; **Overview**.

2.  On a mentor's profile, in the Suggested mentors section, select **Search mentors**.

    **Note:** You see suggested mentors only after setting your preferences.

3.  On the **Explore available mentors** page, you will see a list of mentors who you can connect with.

4.  Use the **Search by name or keyword** field to find a specific mentor you are looking for.

5.  Select **Filters** and fill in the form to add skills or other requirements.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Job family|Business unit or function of the mentor.|
    |Job title|Role or job title of the mentor|
    |Job seniority level|Role level of the mentor.|
    |Tenure|Range of the tenure of the mentor.|
    |Mentoring relationship|Range of the duration of the mentorship.|

6.  Select **Apply**.

7.  Find a mentor of your choice and select **Connect**.

    For more information, see [Request a connection](request-connection-mentoring.md)
