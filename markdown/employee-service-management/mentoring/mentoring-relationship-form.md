# Mentoring relationship form

Use the mentoring relationship form to start a new relationship between a mentee and a mentor.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Mentee|Name of the mentee.|
|Mentor|Name of the mentor.|
|Duration|Default duration of the program in months.|
|State|State of the program.|
|Start date|Date on which the relationship begins.|
|End date|Date on which the relationship should end.|
|Request details|
|Request date|Date on which the relationship is requested.|
|Withdrawal date|Date on which the relationship must be withdrawn.|
|Request note|Details about the relationship request.|
|Closure reason|Option for auto-close mentoring post due date.|
|Decline details|
|Decline reason|Reason for declining the relationship.|
|Decline date|Date of declination of the relationship.|
|Decline note|Details about why the relationship was declined.|
