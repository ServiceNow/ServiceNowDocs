# Mentoring reference

Reference topics provide additional information about the lists and forms that you use to configure and administer Mentoring.

-   **[Mentoring relationship form](mentoring-relationship-form.md)**  
Use the mentoring relationship form to start a new relationship between a mentee and a mentor.
-   **[Edit mentee preferences form](edit-mentee-pref-form.md)**  
Use this form to record your preferences as a mentee.
-   **[Mentor preferences form](edit-mentor-prf-form.md)**  
Use this form to record your preferences as a mentor.
