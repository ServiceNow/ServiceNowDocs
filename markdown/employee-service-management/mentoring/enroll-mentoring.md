# Enroll as a mentor or mentee

Enroll as a mentor or mentee to learn from your peers in new ways.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Your career** &gt; **Mentoring**.

2.  Select **Get started**.

3.  Select one from the following options:

    -   I want to be a mentee
    -   I want to be a mentor
    **Note:** You can do both, but you will have to enroll as a mentee and mentor separately.

4.  Select **Next** and answer the questions that appear in the wizard.


-   **[Add mentee preferences](add-preferences-mentee.md)**  
As a mentee, add your preferences to find the right mentor to guide you through your career.
-   **[Search and add skills](search-add-skills-mentoring.md)**  
As a mentee, search for a skill name or a keyword to find a mentor who specialises in that skill to help you.
-   **[Add mentor preferences](add-mentor-preferences.md)**  
As a mentor, add your preferences to help mentees find out if you're the right match for them
