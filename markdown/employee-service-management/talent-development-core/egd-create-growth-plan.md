# Create a growth plan

Create a growth plan to give your career goals guidance and structure.

## Before you begin

Role required: None

## About this task

Growth plans are based on your individual needs and aspirations and help you make the most of your potential and reach your career goals.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  At the **Your Career** drop-down list, select **Growth plans**.

3.  Select the **Create a growth plan** button.

4.  Select **Get Started** if this is your first time creating a growth plan.

    **Note:** If you have any open aspirations or recent conversations, they might be displayed as potential **focus** areas. If you don't have any open aspirations or conversations, you can build a plan from scratch by selecting **Build a plan from scratch** option.

5.  Enter a plan name in **Growth plan name**.

6.  Enter Description

7.  Select the **Next** button.

    **Tip:** Creating a plan will start to process and find some activities for your plan.

8.  Select the **Next** button.

    **Tip:** If learning and activities are installed, Growth plan might show some suggested learnings, or you can search learning to add things to your Growth plan.

9.  Select **Create plan**


## Result

The draft growth plan is created and will be shared with your manager once the plan is launched.

## What to do next

-   Launch plan so it can be shared with your manager.
-   Review your progress.
-   Continue to edit your growth plan.
