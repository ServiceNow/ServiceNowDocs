# Integrate with the Talent Development Core application

Integrations enable the expansion of features and services among available apps. Integrations can include internal \(ServiceNow®\) and external \(third party\) applications.

Talent Development Core is closely integrated with several ServiceNow applications and plugin features.

## Key apps

Talent Development is integrated into the Employee Center portal along with Manager Hub enabling you to navigate through the different Talent Development features and applications.

-   **[Employee Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/employee-center-landing-page.md)**

    One of the key features of the Employee Center is the Employee Profile. Talent Development applications and features access data from employee profiles, see [View employee profile](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/update-employee-profile.md). You can view, update, and manage information about your career from the [Career tab in Employee Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/talent-development-core/manage-skills-profile.md).

-   **[Manager Hub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/manager-hub-overview.md)**

    Integrates with Talent features used by mangers to help manage and proactively engage with employees, see [Manager Hub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/manager-hub-overview.md).


## Talent Development applications

There are several applications developed to extend and improve your experience with Talent Development.

-   **[Skills Foundation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/talent-development-core/skills-intelligence.md)**

    The ServiceNow® Skills Foundation application is an AI-driven platform that you can use in your organization to develop a workforce that is based on skills.

-   **[Career Conversations](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/career-conversations/egd-activities-landing-page.md)**

    Supports an end-to-end process for individuals and managers to create and manage career growth conversations.

-   **[Learning](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/learning-core/exploring-learning-exp.md)**

    The Learning application enables you to discover, manage, and engage with career development content. It enables the configuration of learning catalogs that contain course items from both internal and external learning systems.

    The [Career Assessment](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/talent-development-core/exam-engine.md) application is part of the Learning application. The Career Assessment application works as an exam engine that provides the ability to create exams that help with career advancement and help test a learner's knowledge of a subject.

-   **[Mentoring](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/mentoring/mentoring-egd-overview.md)**

    The ServiceNow® Mentoring application is a platform that connects individuals seeking mentorship with experienced mentors. It creates a seamless experience for both mentees and mentors.

-   **[Employee Goals](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/employee-goals/egd-goals-landing.md)**

    The ServiceNow® Employee Goals application lets employees view and track their career goals from the Employee Center. This application is a tool that enables employees to stay focused and aligned with your company's strategic objectives, resulting in improved overall organizational performance.

-   **[Leader Hub](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/td-lh-landing.md)**

    Leader Hub empowers organizational leaders to understand the strengths of their teams, identify gaps in skills, and facilitate changes that are simultaneously conducive to their workforce and the achievement of desired business outcomes.

-   **[Opportunity Marketplace overview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/talent-development-core/egd-opportunity-marketplace-ovrvw.md)**

    Opportunity Marketplace enhances career mobility. It provides employees an intuitive experience to explore career opportunities. Customizable match preferences are seamlessly integrated with your Applicant Tracking System \(ATS\) to enhance employee engagement with transparent and efficient career development.
