# Initiating a contract request

As a case owner or fulfiller, initiate a self-served or non-self-served contract for the case you are working on.

-   **[Initiate a self-served contract request](cncore-initiate-self-served-contract.md)**  
As a case owner or fulfiller, initiate a self-served contract for the case you are working on. Self-served contracts are submitted when you want own paper based contract documents to be sent for review.
-   **[Initiate a non-self-served contract request](cncore-initiate-non-ss-cnt.md)**  
As a case owner or fulfiller, initiate the submission of contracts when you want third-party based contract documents to be sent for review.
-   **[Initiate an amendment request](cncore-initiate-amedment.md)**  
Initiate an amendment request from your workspace.
