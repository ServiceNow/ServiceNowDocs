# Supporting information for Now Assist in Contract Management

Get a quick overview of the important information that is related to the Now Assist in Contract Management application.

## Supported Versions

Now Assist capabilities are supported in Contract Management Pro starting with Xanadu \(Patch 3\).

## Application information

Activate the Now Assist in Contract Management plugin \(sn\_cm\_gen\_ai\) to analyze contract revisions and extract metadata from signed contracts.

The application has the following dependencies:

-   Now Assist for Platform \(sn\_genai\_platform\)
-   Document Intelligence for Contract Management Content pack \(sn\_docintel\_cm\_cp\)

**Note:** Ensure that Contract Management Pro \(sn\_cm\_pro\) and Contract Workspace \(sn\_cm\_workspace\) plugins are installed before installing Now Assist in Contract Management plugin \(sn\_cm\_gen\_ai\).
