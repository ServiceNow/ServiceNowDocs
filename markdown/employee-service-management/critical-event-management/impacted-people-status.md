# Impacted people status

Descriptions of the possible statues for impacted people.

|Status|Description|
|------|-----------|
|Unknown|Default status. No information is available about the impacted person.|
|Accounted|Impacted person is traced and confirmed safe.|
|Need Help|Impacted person is traced and needs medical attention or other assistance.|
