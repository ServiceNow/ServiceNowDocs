# Conduct a roll call as an on-site responder

Mark the status of the impacted people in a critical event in the responder portal. You can also manually add more impacted people in the list.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_critical.responder

## Procedure

1.  Log in to the responder portal through the email sent by an agent.

2.  On the critical event, select **Show Details**.

3.  Mark the status of the impacted people.
