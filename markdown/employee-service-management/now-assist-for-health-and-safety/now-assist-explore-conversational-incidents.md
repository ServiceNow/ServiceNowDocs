# Conversational AI Health and Safety Incident Report

Use the Now Assist panel conversational interface to report Health and Safety incidents that enables you to engage with live agents, virtual agents, or both.

A basic Health and Safety incident can be reported using the conversational language through the Now Assist panel. Using appropriate keywords such as health, safety, incident, or injury generates functional responses. These keywords can be configured by an admin on the Health and Safety workspace by navigating to **All**&gt;**Service Catalog**&gt;**Catalog definitions**&gt;**Record producers** and then editing the **Meta** field on the **Report Health and Safety Incident-Basic** record. For more information on record producers, see [Record Producer](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=c_RecordProducer&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-servicenow-platform&ft:locale=en-US).

Employees can use the Now Assist panel icon ![now assist panel](../../../reuse/icons/brand-icons/bus-ai-sparkle.svg) in the **Employee Center** home page to open a chat panel to report a Health and Safety incident easily.

![conversational incident reporting in now assist](../image/na-conversational-incident-reporting.png "Guided conversational Health and Safety incident reporting")

Once the incident is recorded, the Now Assist chat panel provides the Health and Safety incident record number and the employees can view it by selecting **My Requests** or selecting the record number.

## Benefits of Conversational AI Health and Safety Incident Report

Conversational incident reporting simplifies the safety incident-reporting process.

-   Eliminates the need to complete lengthy forms.
-   Provides real-time interaction and an immediate feedback mechanism.
-   Provides a user-friendly interface with Instant response and guidance.
-   Provides a responsive and supportive environment and promotes proactive incident reporting.
-   Enables document uploads \(maximum 10 mb\) through Now Assist panel.
