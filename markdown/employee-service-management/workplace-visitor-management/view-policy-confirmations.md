# View visitor policy confirmations

View policy confirmations that were submitted by the visitors.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_visitor.staff

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Visitor Management** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Visitor policy confirmations**.

    You can also navigate to the Visitor registration record of the visitor.

2.  From the Visitor Policy Confirmations list, select the record that you want to view.

3.  View the details about the Visitor Policy Confirmation.

    **Note:** For policies that require a signature, the signature is attached to the record as an image. If the admin anonymizes the visitor, the signature is removed from the record.
