# Cancel the signature process from the Legal Counsel Center

Cancel a signature process for a legal request from the Legal Counsel Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_cnt.contract\_fulfiller and sn\_cm\_core.contract\_fulfiller

## About this task

You can cancel the signature process from the Legal Counsel Center when the legal request is in Awaiting signature state.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  Select the List icon \(![List icon](../../legal-request-management/image/lsd-lcc-list-icon.png)\).

3.  Open the legal request for which you want to cancel the signature.

4.  Select the contract request from the **Contract Requests** tab.

5.  On the Standard Ticket page, from the **Actions** menu, select **Cancel signature**.

6.  On the confirmation window, select **Cancel signature** to confirm the cancellation.


## Result

The contract status updates to Signature cancelled, and a notification email is sent to all signatories.
