# Resubmit third-party contract request

As a legal user, resubmit contract request in draft state.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user and sn\_cm\_core.contract\_user

## About this task

For a custom record producer, if no documents are attached, the legal request will be in Draft state. You need to upload document and resubmit the contract request.
