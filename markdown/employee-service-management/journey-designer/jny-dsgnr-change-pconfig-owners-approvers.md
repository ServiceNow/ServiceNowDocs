# Change Journey designer version 2 plan configuration owners or approvers

Administrators can change a plan configuration owner or approver.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## About this task

Administrators may need to change owners or approvers for Journey designer plan configurations if people change roles or leave their job.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Journey designer** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Manage Plan Configurations**.

2.  Open a **Plan Configuration** to change an owner or approver.

    Double-click the **Plan Configuration** to open it.

3.  To to the **Plan Config Owners and Approvers** tab.

4.  Double-click the **Name** of the owner.

5.  Change the owners, co-owners, or approvers.

6.  Select **Update**.
