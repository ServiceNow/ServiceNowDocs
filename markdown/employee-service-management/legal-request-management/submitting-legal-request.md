# Using legal services

Employees can submit a legal request to seek services from the legal department and track its status.

-   **[Submit a legal request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/submit-legal-request.md)**  
Submit a legal request for yourself or on behalf of another user to seek services from the legal department.
-   **[Viewing and tracking legal request details](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/view-legal-request-details.md)**  
View the details of a legal request after it has been submitted and track request activities.
-   **[Update a submitted legal request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/update-legal-request.md)**  
Update request details, add comments, or upload documents in a legal request you submitted while it is still in the New or Assigned state. You can cancel a submitted request irrespective of its state.
-   **[Reopen a closed legal request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/reopen-legal-request-requester.md)**  
Reopen a request if you're not satisfied with the resolution given by the legal team.
