# Review your vaccine responses

If you would like to review your recently reported COVID-19 vaccinations or add an attachment to a record, access your previous vaccine responses.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_imt\_core.privacy\_consent\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Vaccination Status** &gt; **My vaccinations**.

2.  Select the vaccine response that you would like to add an attachment to or review.

3.  Modify the record by adding an attachment or review the record.

    -   If you are adding an attachment, such as proof of vaccination, add the attachment and then click **Update**.

        **Note:** For information on managing attachments, see [Manage attachments](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=t_ManagingAttachments&version=yokohama&pubname=yokohama-platform-user-interface&ft:locale=en-US).

    -   If you are reviewing the record, review the record and click **Back** to return to the list of vaccine responses.
