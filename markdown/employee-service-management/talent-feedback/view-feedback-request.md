# View feedback requests

You can view and feedback requests platform under the Talent Feedback module.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Talent Feedback** &gt; **Feedback requests**.

2.  Select the feedback request you want to view.

3.  View the information on the **Talent Feedback Request** form.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Number|Number allotted to the feedback request.|
    |Requested for|Employee for whom the feedback is requested.|
    |Opened by|Manager who opened the feedback request.|
    |State|Current state of the feedback request.|
    |Assigned to|Employee from whom the feedback is requested.|
    |Due date|Date by when the feedback is due.|
    |Short description|A description of the nature of skill or feedback requestd.|

    **Note:** You can also view the **Talent Feedback Request Skills** in the related links section of the form.


-   **[Begin a discussion from a Talent Feedback request](discussion-skill-feedback.md)**  
You can choose to begin a discussion regarding a talent feedback request using the Discuss option.
