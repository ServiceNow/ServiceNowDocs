# Accessing the Return to Workplace campaign

View and edit the campaign content and campaign bundles in the Return to Workplace campaign.

## Before you begin

You may choose to clone a campaign before making edits.

For more information on cloning, editing, and publishing a campaign, see[Campaigns for Content Experiences](../../employee-center/concept/ecpro-campaigns.md).

Role required: sn\_ca.campaign\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Content Automation** &gt; **Campaigns**.

2.  Select **Return to Workplace**.

    You may choose to clone the campaign before editing. Scroll down to see the **Campaign Bundles** and **Campaign Content** tabs.

3.  Click a campaign bundle or campaign content to edit.

4.  After making edits, click **Update**.
