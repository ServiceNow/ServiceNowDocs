# Accessing the Return to Workplace knowledge articles

Create or modify knowledge articles to share information across your organization.

## Before you begin

Role required: knowledge\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Knowledge** &gt; **Articles** &gt; **All**.

2.  Select a knowledge article to edit.

3.  Click **Checkout**.

4.  Make your edits.

5.  Click **Update**.
