# Close a legal request through Mobile Agent

Close a legal request assigned to you when you've resolved it.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  In the Mobile Agent, navigate to the **Assigned Requests** list.

2.  Tap the request item that you want to close.

3.  Tap **Close Complete**.


## Result

The legal request is closed and the state changes to Close Complete.
