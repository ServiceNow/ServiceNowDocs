# Integrations for Hiring Experiences

Integration enables expansion of features and capabilities among the applications.

-   **[Integrate with your ATS](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/integrate-ats.md)**  
Integrate your Hiring Experiences environment with your preferred ATS to create a seamless hiring workflow.
-   **[Configure Microsoft Outlook integration](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/configure-microsoft-outlook-integration-ta.md)**  
Set up Microsoft Outlook to integrate meeting invites from Hiring Experiences with your Outlook account.
-   **[Configure Zoom integration](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/configure-zoom-integration-ta.md)**  
Configure Zoom to seamlessly integrate meeting invites originating from Hiring Experiences with your Zoom account.
-   **[Configure Virtual Agent notifications in Microsoft Teams](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/config-va-ta.md)**  
Configure the Virtual Agent notifications to provide prompt updates for the hiring managers and recruiters in Microsoft Teams.
