# Reopen a legal hold matter

Reopen a closed legal hold matter when a legal hold is either revoked or newly issued in relation to the same closed legal matter.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_hold.legal\_hold\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  Select the List icon \(![List icon](../../ohs-incident-management/image/list-icon-hs.png)\).

3.  In the **Default lists** tab, navigate to **Matters** &gt; **All**

4.  Select the legal matter from the list.

5.  In the legal matter page, select **Reopen**.

6.  In the **Comments** field of the Response matter pop-up window, enter the reason to reopen the legal hold notification matter.

7.  Select **OK**.


## Result

The closed legal hold notification matter is reopened, and its state changes from Closed complete to Work in Progress.
