# View user metrics related to Listening Posts surveys

View user experiences while taking pulse surveys to better understand overall employee interaction with a survey.

## Before you begin

-   Role required: sn\_lp.admin
-   Plugins required: Listening Posts \[sn\_lp\], User Experience Analytics \[com.glide.appsee\], and Service Portal Analytics plugin \[com.glide.service-portal.analytics\]. For more information, see [Integrating Listening Posts with User Experience Analytics](../concept/lp-apps.md).
-   Ensure that the application scope is set to Listening Posts. For more information, see [Listening Posts](../concept/listening-posts-1.md).

## About this task

The User Experience Analytics dashboard provides information about an employee's interaction with the **Listening Posts My Survey** and **Listening Posts Take Survey** widgets. For more information on widgets, see [Widgets for Listening Posts](../concept/listening-posts-widget.md).

**Note:** On the User Experience Analytics dashboard, the data is updated 30 minutes after the actions are recorded on the Listening Posts survey widget.

For more information about the information displayed on the User Experience Analytics dashboard, see [User Experience for HR Service Delivery](../concept/user-exp-hrser.md).

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **User Experience Analytics** &gt; **Dashboard**.

2.  On the User Experience Analytics dashboard, select **Events** in the left panel.

3.  Click **Expand** to the right of the names of the events relevant to survey widgets: Successful submissions at survey response and Unsuccessful attempts at survey response.

    For more information about the details that are displayed for these events, see [Events related to the Listening Posts surveys on User Experience Analytics dashboard](../concept/lp-user-exp-analytics-events.md).
