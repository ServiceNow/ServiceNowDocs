# Configure HR Playbook activity actions

Use playbook actions to enable users to complete \(add buttons and lists\) and skip activities.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_ws.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Playbook Experience** &gt; **Activity Actions**.

2.  Enter or edit the form fields.

3.  After editing or creating, click **Update** or **Submit**.
