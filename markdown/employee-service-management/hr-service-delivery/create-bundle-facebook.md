# Create a campaign bundle for Workplace from Facebook

Use campaign bundles to group your campaign content within a campaign. This process allows you to determine when the content must be published to the groups on Workplace from Facebook.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ca.campaign\_admin, sn\_fb\_wp\_spoke.workplace\_fb\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Content Automation** &gt; **Campaigns**.

2.  Select the required campaign.

3.  In the Campaign bundle related list, click **New**.

4.  On the form, fill in the fields:

## What to do next

Add the campaign content to the campaign bundle.

1.  In **Campaign Content** related list, click **New**.
2.  On the form, fill in the fields, and click **Submit**.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Content|Option for adding content that is created for Workplace from Facebook.|
|Active|Option for indicating that the content is active and available.|
|Campaign bundle|Name of the campaign bundle.|
|Facebook Workplace groups|Workplace from Facebook groups to which content is posted. You can select one or more groups, including open and closed groups.|
