# Properties for Alumni Service Center

Use properties to control the behavior of the Alumni Service Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_asc.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Alumni Service Center** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Properties**.

2.  In the Personal email domains \(sn\_asc.self\_registration.personal.email.domains\), select the domain names that are considered when alumni is self-registering.

3.  Select **Save**.
