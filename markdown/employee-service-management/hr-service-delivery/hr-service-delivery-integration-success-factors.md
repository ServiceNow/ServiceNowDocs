# HR Service Delivery Integration with SuccessFactors

The ServiceNow® HR Service Delivery Integration with SuccessFactors application is built by Bristlecone, Inc.

Using HR Service Delivery Integration with SuccessFactors you can enable employees to fulfill related tasks at a single location in ServiceNow and also synchronize the employee records.
