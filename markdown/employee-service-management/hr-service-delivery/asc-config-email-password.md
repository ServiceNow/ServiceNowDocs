# Configure email and password resets for the Alumni Service Center

Use **Notifications**, to configure the emails that send the user ID and password reset for your alumni.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  **System Notification** &gt; **Email** &gt; **Notifications**.

2.  Click **Invite Alumni Email - Username** and edit the **Message HTML** to reflect your company's message on providing an alumnus with a user ID.

3.  Click **Preview Notification** to preview your message.

4.  Click **Update**.

5.  Click **Invite Alumni email - Temp Password** and edit the **Message HTML** to reflect your company's message on providing a temporary password to your alumni.

6.  Click **Preview Notification** to preview your message.

7.  Click **Update**.
