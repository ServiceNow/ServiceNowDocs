# Take a Listening Posts voluntary survey

Share your feedback anytime by accessing the voluntary feedback survey on Employee Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Employee Center**.

2.  Click the **Give feedback** button.

    A predefined questionnaire appears: **I like something**, **I don't like something**, and **I have a suggestion**.

3.  Submit your response.
