# Enable a Listening Posts voluntary survey

Enable users to share their feedback anytime by letting them access the voluntary feedback survey on Employee Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: pulse\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Listening Posts** &gt; **Voluntary feedback** &gt; **Voluntary survey**.

2.  Enable the **Active** check box.

    -   The **Give feedback** button is displayed on Employee Center to let an employee take a voluntary survey.
    -   After an employee takes a voluntary survey, the responses are recorded in the **Pulse users** related list in the Voluntary Survey page. To view the voluntary survey responses in a dashboard, navigate to **Listening Posts** &gt; **Voluntary feedback** &gt; **Voluntary feedback report**.
