# Complete a Listening Posts pulse survey

Provide your rating for the questions asked in a pulse survey. Your feedback is used to gauge overall employee experience.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Complete a pulse survey using one of the following options.

    -   Open the pulse survey email that you have received. Click the **Take Survey** link to answer and complete the pulse survey.
    -   Click **Take Survey** on the Listening Posts widget in Employee Center.
    -   Take a pulse survey from other communication channels such as Virtual Agent, Slack, Microsoft teams, and chat web client in Employee Center.
    -   Take a pulse survey via an SMS.
