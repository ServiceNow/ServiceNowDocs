# Integrating Listening Posts with User Experience Analytics

Listening Posts integration with User Experience Analytics provides an interpretation of an employee's journey on the Listening Posts survey widgets. It provides an implicit insight on employee's behavior and their response to pulse surveys.

## Activation information

To start using Listening Posts with User Experience Analytics, you must activate the Listening Posts \[sn\_lp\], User Experience Analytics \[com.glide.appsee\], and Service Portal Analytics \[com.glide.service-portal.analytics\] plugins.

## Evaluating Listening Posts surveys using events in User Experience Analytics dashboard

As a pulse administrator, you can evaluate the successful or unsuccessful attempts of an employee in taking the Listening Posts My Survey and Listening Posts Take Survey using the **Successful submissions at survey response** and **Unsuccessful attempts at survey response** events. For example, understanding the average time taken by an employee to respond to a survey, or the number of resignations in the survey process could be used to improve the design of future surveys or indicate whether more encouragement is needed to increase employee participation.

For more information about the details that are displayed for these events, see [Events related to the Listening Posts surveys on User Experience Analytics dashboard](lp-user-exp-analytics-events.md).
