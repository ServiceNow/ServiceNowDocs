# Create a pulse survey as content in Listening Posts

Use the Content Type Pulse Survey to create pulse surveys that can be delivered via ServiceNow Content Delivery or Content Automation.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lp.creator, sn\_cd.content\_manager, sn\_ca.campaign\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Content Delivery** &gt; **Pulse Content**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields:

<table id="table_kt1_rmd_knb"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Title

</td><td>

Name of the pulse content.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Theme

</td><td>

Theme with which you want to associate the pulse content.**Note:** Only the themes of type Group Pulse can be selected in the pulse content.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Active

</td><td>

Option to indicate that the pulse content is active and available for use.

</td></tr></tbody>
</table>4.  Click **Submit**.

5.  In the **Questions** related list:

    -   To add a question to any content, click **New**. For more information, see [Create a pulse question in Listening Posts](create-pulseq.md).
    -   To add a question from Question Bank, click **From Question Bank**.
