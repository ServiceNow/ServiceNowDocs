# View your workplace floor plan update logs

After updating your workplace floor plan, you can view all changes made.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_core.workplace\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Safety Management** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Floor Plan Update Logs**.

    Logs appear for each floor plan updated with date and timestamps.

2.  Select the **Created date** link to view the log record.

3.  Select the **Floor** link to view the floor record.
