# Environmental data form for Workplace Connectors

Environmental sensor data form has pre-filled environmental data values and units of measure. Select the MetricBase value to visualize the time-series data in different transforms.

|MetricBase value|Description|Units|
|----------------|-----------|-----|
|Carbon dioxide value|Carbon dioxide \(CO2\) concentration level in a workplace location.|Carbon dioxide units.|
|Light value|Light value in a workplace location.|Light units|
|Ozone value|Ozone level unit in a workplace location.|Ozone units.|
|Location|Workplace location where the environmental data sensor is installed.|NA|
|Temperature value|Temperature levels to measure extreme heat or cold in a workplace location.|Temperature units.|
|Sound value|Sound levels to measure the level of noise in a workplace location.|Sound units|
|Total volatile organic compounds \(TVOCs\) value|Unit for Volatile organic compounds \(VOCs\) in a location. Helps in analyzing the air quality and identifying the sources of pollution to reduce environmental exposure.|Total volatile organic compounds \(TVOCs\) units.|
|Relative humidity value|Humidity levels in a workplace location. High humidity promotes growth of mold that can cause respiratory issues.|Relative humidity units.|
|Particulate matter 2.5 value|Particulate matter \(PM\) for fine air particles measuring PM 2.5.microns or less in diameter.|Particulate matter 2.5 units.|
|Particulate matter 1.0 value|Particulate matter \(PM\) for fine air particles measuring PM 1.0.microns or less in diameter.|Particulate matter 1.0 units.|
|Particulate matter 10 value|Particulate matter \(PM\) for microscopic matter suspended in air measuring PM 10 mu or micro in diameter.|Particulate matter 10 units.|
|Pressure value|Atmospheric pressure value at the workplace location.|Pressure units|
