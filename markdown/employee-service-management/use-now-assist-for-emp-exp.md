# Using Now Assist for Employee Experience

Use the Now Assist in Virtual Agent to quickly check your tasks and approvals.

-   **[Check your to-do tasks by using the Now Assist in Virtual Agent](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/employee-service-management/check-todo-tasks-using-the-now-assist-virtual-agent.md)**  
Check your to-do and pending tasks by using the Now Assist in Virtual Agent AI chat window. By checking these tasks, you can stay organized, prioritize the most important tasks on your list, and monitor how you're doing against your deadlines.
-   **[Check your approvals by using Now Assist in Virtual Agent](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/employee-service-management/check-your-approvals-using-now-assist-virtual-agent-emp-exp.md)**  
Check the approvals that are assigned to you by using the Now Assist in Virtual Agent AI chat window. By checking your approvals before you begin your tasks, you avoid working on tasks that weren't approved and focus on what needs to be done.
