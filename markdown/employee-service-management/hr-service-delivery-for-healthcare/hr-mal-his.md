# Malpractice history form

Access, update, and validate the Malpractice history details of a healthcare professional in the Malpractice history form.

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Professional profile

</td><td>

Professional profile of the healthcare professional.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Date of occurrence

</td><td>

Date when the malpractice occurred.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Date of claim filing

</td><td>

Date when the malpractice claim was submitted.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Profession liability insurance involved

</td><td>

Insurer selected for the claim.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Description

</td><td>

Additional details about the malpractice claim.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Active

</td><td>

Option indicating that the malpractice claim history record is active.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Is in NPDB

</td><td>

Option indicating that the claim history is in the National Practitioner Data Bank \(NPDB\) database.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Status

</td><td>

Status of the malpractice claim history record:-   New: When profile intake form is sent to the healthcare professional for filling.
-   Pending validation: When data is filled in by healthcare professional and sent for credentialing and validation to HR agents.
-   Invalid: When HR agents review the data and reject due to insufficient or invalid details.
-   Valid: When HR agents validate and approve the information provided by the healthcare professional.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Claim status

</td><td>

Current status, whether the claim is in progress or resolved.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Resolution method

</td><td>

Type of resolution provided for the claim, for example, settled with prejudice, mediation, or arbitration.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Amount of settlement

</td><td>

Amount at which the claim got settled.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Amount paid

</td><td>

Total amount paid by the insurer for claim submitted.
