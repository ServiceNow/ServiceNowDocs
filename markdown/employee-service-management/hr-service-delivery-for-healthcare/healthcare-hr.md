# HR Service Delivery for Healthcare

The ServiceNow® HR Service Delivery for Healthcare application creates a standardized, digitized, and automated physician onboarding workflow using the Healthcare Professional data model. The application enables you to see the full picture for all the required onboarding information during and after physician onboarding. HR Service Delivery for Healthcare is a new application in the Xanadu release.
