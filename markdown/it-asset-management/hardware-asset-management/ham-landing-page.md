# Hardware Asset Management

The ServiceNow® Hardware Asset Management \(HAM\) application is a licensable application that on activation provides advanced workflow, automation, and mobile capabilities to maintain your assets.

Watch this short video for an introduction to the Hardware Asset Management application.

Hardware Asset Management overview 

**Note:** Beginning with the ServiceNow AI Platform® Washington DC release, we will provide limited support for the Hardware Asset Management Core UI interface. While it remains active in your instance, including when you upgrade to a new ServiceNow AI Platform release, we encourage you to move to the new workspace experience. For more information, see [KB1584544](https://support.servicenow.com/kb?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB1584544), [Hardware Asset Workspace](../concept/using-ham-workspace.md) and [Next Experience UI](https://www.servicenow.com/docs/access?context=next-experience-landing-page&version=xanadu&pubname=xanadu-platform-user-interface&ft:locale=en-US).
