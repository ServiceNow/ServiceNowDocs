# Using Hardware Asset Management

Use the Hardware Asset Management application to manage your hardware and consumable assets.

-   **[Work with hardware normalization](Work-with-hardware-normalization.md)**  
Asset Management Hardware Model Normalization enables users to normalize the details, such as manufacturer, product, model, and device type, of your hardware and consumable models. Data from the models is compared against the data in the Hardware Model Normalization Content Service.
-   **[Work with Asset and CI](work-with-asset-ci.md)**  
Asset and configuration item \(CI\) management refers to creating assets, setting appropriate states and substates, synchronizing assets and CIs, managing consumables, and retiring assets.
-   **[Consumables life cycle](../../asset-management/concept/c_FollowLifeCycleConsumbl.md)**  
Consumables are assets that are not tracked individually, but as a group of the same model.
-   **[Manage asset bundles from your inventory](../task/create-bundled-assets.md)**  
Create asset bundles from existing assets in your inventory to track, reserve, or deploy a group of assets as a single entity.
-   **[Manage various assets through asset classes](../../asset-management/concept/c_AssetClasses.md)**  
The default asset classes are Hardware, Software License, Consumable, Bundle, Software Entitlement, and Facility. These general classes can be used to manage various assets.
-   **[Manage your inventory through pallet assets](pallets-for-inventory-management.md)**  
Manage your inventory effectively using pallet assets.
-   **[Create fixed assets](../../asset-management/concept/c_CreatingFixedAssets.md#)**  
Fixed assets are containers that can hold multiple assets. Fixed assets are commonly tracked at the corporate level by a finance or accounting department, but may contain IT assets such as hardware and software.
-   **[Manage pre-allocated assets](manage-preallocated-asset.md)**  
Manage pre-allocated assets, which are the assets that the vendor still owns, but has agreed to store in a customer stockroom for just-in-time procurement.
-   **[Manage loaner assets](../task/manage-loaner-asset.md)**  
Request a loaner asset or consumable for a short period. You can also prepare the loaner asset or consumable for deployment and reclamation by using loaner asset tasks. Deploy the loaner asset or consumable for a specific period, and reclaim it on the return date.
-   **[Donate assets to charity organizations](donate-asset-to-charity-organizations.md)**  
Use the Asset Donation flow to donate hardware and consumable assets of your organization to charity organizations.
-   **[Exclude assets](../task/exclude-assets.md)**  
Exclude an asset for which you don't want to use Hardware Asset Management licensed features.
-   **[Use Advanced Shipment Notification](../task/advanced-shipment-notification.md)**  
Use Advanced Shipment Notification \(ASN\) to automate and create asset records when your assets are in transit.
-   **[Use Organization Management](../../asset-management/reference/org-mgmt.md#)**  
The Organization Management application provides an easy way to perform such asset management tasks as updating users and adding vendors.
-   **[Manage RMA requests](manage-rma-req.md)**  
Initiate a Return Merchandise Authorization \(RMA\) process by submitting an RMA request to initiate an RMA process with your vendor. You can repair or replace a faulty asset.
-   **[Create an inventory stock order request](../task/create-inventory-stock-order.md)**  
Create an inventory stock order request so that you can order new hardware in bulk for your stockrooms. After your request is approved, the Hardware Stock Order flow takes you through the process of requesting, sourcing, and receiving your order.
-   **[Transfer orders for Asset Management](../../asset-management/concept/transfer-orders-for-am.md#)**  
Transfer orders move assets between company stockrooms.
-   **[Create a transfer order for Asset Management](../../asset-management/task/t_CreateATransferOrder.md#)**  
Create a transfer order in order to transfer assets from one location to the other.
-   **[Transfer order line asset tracking](../../asset-management/reference/r_TransferOrderLineAssetTracking.md#)**  
As transfer order line actions are triggered, the stock information and states of any affected assets are updated. Consumables and non-consumables are tracked differently.
-   **[Create a disposal order](../task/create-disposal-order.md)**  
Create a disposal order for hardware or consumable assets. You can create a disposal order for an asset reaching its end of life cycle or an asset that is no longer functional.
-   **[Use a hardware asset request flow](../task/hardware-request-flow.md)**  
Use a hardware asset request flow for requesting, sourcing, and deploying hardware catalog items from the Service Catalog application.
-   **[Audit your inventory](../task/audit-your-inventory.md)**  
Perform scheduled or blind audits of asset stockrooms and other locations such as offices or datacenters.
-   **[Request a Hardware Asset Refresh](../task/hardware-asset-refresh.md#)**  
Use the Hardware Refresh request flow to track the aged hardware assets that are nearing the end of their life cycle. Replace them with new hardware assets.
-   **[Manage your expiring contracts for leased hardware assets](../task/manage-your-leased-hw-asts-expiring-contract.md)**  
Use the lease contract expiration flow and decide on what to do before your leased hardware asset contract expires. Get notifications about the lease contracts that are nearing their expiration dates.
-   **[Reclaim hardware assets](manage-asset-reclaim.md)**  
Reclaim hardware assets efficiently and store them in the inventory, reassign, send it for repair, or dispose of as required.
-   **[View RFID information of assets](../task/view-rfid-info.md)**  
View the Radio Frequency Identification \(RFID\) information of assets to manage and locate important assets easily.
-   **[Manage the lifecycle of hardware models with calculated lifecycle templates](manage-ham-lifecycle-temp.md)**  
Manage the complete lifecycle of hardware models by creating calculated lifecycle templates and associating these templates with the models.
-   **[Receive asset warranty details from Lenovo](receive-warranty-details-lenovo.md)**  
Connect to the Lenovo Warranty API and get the warranty details of your hardware assets.
-   **[Manage stockrooms](manage-your-stockrooms.md)**  
Manage the assets in the stockroom by using appropriate stock rules, service locations, and distribution channels.
-   **[Track shipments using the integration framework](tracking-shipments-using-integration-framework.md)**  
Track your shipments in real time by integrating your ServiceNow instance with your third-party carrier's application through the integration framework provided by the IT Asset Management application.
-   **[Track asset location using indoor maps](../task/track-asset-location-using-indoor-maps.md)**  
Locate and track the consumables and hardware, bundle, and pallet assets in your organization by using indoor maps. Indoor maps provide an interactive interface that enables you to visualize the location of your assets within your campuses, buildings, floors, and places.
-   **[Assess performance of Hardware Asset Management](suc-goal-act-hw.md)**  
Track the success of the Hardware Asset Management application in your instance by creating success goals. You can also create a success goal category for a success goal and track the success of your goals by creating success activities.
-   **[Manage refresh of assets using Zero Touch Refresh](refresh-hardware-uisng-ztr.md)**  
Enable your employees to request hardware refreshes through an external vendor by using the Zero Touch Refresh flow on your ServiceNow instance. This process enables you to eliminate having to maintain a local inventory of new assets.
-   **[Configure the Total Cost of Ownership of assets](configure-ham-tco.md)**  
Configure the Total Cost of Ownership \(TCO\) of assets by creating rate cards for each task in a Hardware Asset Management workflow.
-   **[Manage and monitor hardware asset performance](manage-and-monitor-hardware-asset-performance.md)**  
Configure settings for asset KPI tracking and monitor asset KPIs using performance reports in the Hardware Asset Workspace.
-   **[Manage Hardware Asset Management subscriptions](managing-ham-subscriptions.md)**  
Save on Hardware Asset Management licensing cost by choosing only the Hardware Asset Management Resource Categories that you use.
-   **[Manage repair of defective assets in your stockroom in the Hardware Asset Workspace](manage-repair-of-defective-ham-assets.md)**  
Optimize your inventory by getting the defective hardware assets in your stockroom repaired using the Repair flow.
-   **[Manage picking hardware assets within your stockroom for Hardware Asset Management workflows](manage-asset-picking-stockroom-ham-ws.md)**  
Enable the Asset pick task explicitly for your stockroom and provide granular location details of the asset to facilitate asset picking within your stockroom.
-   **[Manage asset put away using the Hardware Asset Workspace](manage-asset-putaway-stockroom-hardware-asset-workspace.md)**  
Enable the Asset put away task explicitly for your stockroom to put away the assets from the receiving bay in the designated aisle-space. View the open Asset put away task and close it after specifying the aisle-space value to move the asset in the stockroom.
-   **[Manage hardware asset tasks using the Mobile Agent application](manage-hardware-asset-tasks-mobile-agent.md)**  
Manage hardware asset tasks such as repair tasks and asset pick tasks using the intuitive Mobile Agent application.
-   **[Acknowledge receipt of assets on the Employee Center portal](../task/receive-assets-employee-center.md)**  
After receiving hardware or consumable assets that were in transit and reserved for you, confirm their receipt on the Employee Center portal.
-   **[Update associated Decision tables for HAM flows](../task/trigger-flow-ham.md)**  
Update associated Decision tables for Hardware Asset Management \(HAM\) flows to trigger a new or customized HAM flow.
