# Configuring Enterprise Asset Management by enterprise asset managers

The enterprise asset manager role can configure your Total Cost of Ownership \(TCO\) to track your assets.

The enterprise asset managers have the capability to configure the following:

-   Create sources for classification
-   Create a TCO report and a TCO report source
-   Create task rate and labor rate cards
-   Create expense lines for closed tasks

-   **[Create sources for classification codes](../task/create-class-source-eam.md)**  
Create sources that you can then assign to classification codes.
-   **[Configuring Total Cost of Ownership for enterprise assets](confifure-eam-asset-tco.md)**  
Configure your Total Cost of Ownership \(TCO\) to track your assets.
