# Managing enterprise assets and models

Create and manage your enterprise assets and models throughout their life cycle.

-   **[Create and manage enterprise assets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-manage-enterprise-assets.md)**  
Create and manage enterprise assets throughout their life cycle.
-   **[Create and manage enterprise models](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-manage-enterprise-models.md)**  
Create and manage your enterprise models to keep track of your enterprise assets.
-   **[Managing enterprise asset shipments](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/manage-shipments-eam.md)**  
Track and manage your enterprise asset shipments in real-time by integrating the Enterprise Asset Management application with third-party shipping carriers using the IT Asset Management integration framework.
-   **[Create a multi-asset onboarding process](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-multi-asset-onboarding.md)**  
Onboard multiple assets at one go by creating a multi-asset onboarding playbook.
-   **[Managing enterprise asset move orders](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/managing-enterprise-asset-move-orders.md)**  
You can use move orders to move in-use or consumed enterprise assets from one location to another.
-   **[Onboarding multiple enterprise assets in the Enterprise Asset Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/onboarding-multi-asset-from-ws.md)**  
Onboard multiple enterprise assets at one go by creating onboarding orders in the Asset operations view.
-   **[Normalizing enterprise models](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/xanadu/markdown/xanadu/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/normalizing-enterprise-models.md)**  
To standardize enterprise models, the data must be normalized. The normalization process enables you to normalize your manufacturer, model name, model number, and model type data of your enterprise models.
