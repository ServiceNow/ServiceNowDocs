# Create success goals for Software Asset Management

Create success goals to track the success of the Software Asset Management application in your instance.

## Before you begin

Role required: sam\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Software Asset Workspace** &gt; **Success Portal**.

2.  Select **Create success goal**.

3.  On the form, fill in the details.

    For a description of the field values, see [SAM Success Goal Details](../reference/sam-success-goal-details.md).

4.  Select **Save**.

    The success goal appears in the License operation view under the Success goals list. You can cancel or delete a success goal from the License operations view.
