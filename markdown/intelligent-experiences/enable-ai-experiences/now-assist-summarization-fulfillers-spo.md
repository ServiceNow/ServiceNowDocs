# Fulfiller summarization for Sourcing and Procurement Operations

Summarize requests and their related records to quickly update fulfillers on their progress and open action items.

These skills provide a fulfiller with a summary of procurement-related records. This enables them to get visibility into the status of the record in the procurement process and consequently enables them to take the necessary actions quickly.

The fulfillers can get a summary of the following procurement records using the Source-to-Pay Workspace.

-   Sourcing request summarization
-   Purchase requisition summarization
-   Procurement case summarization
