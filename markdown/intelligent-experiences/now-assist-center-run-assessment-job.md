# Run the AI readiness assessment job in Now Assist Center

Run the AI readiness assessment to analyze your instance and evaluate its readiness for AI adoption.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_na\_center.nac\_admin

## About this task

Follow these steps to run the AI readiness assessment job. The job performs an analysis of your instance data and produces readiness assessments. The assessments enable you to identify configuration gaps, measure readiness over time, and accelerate the path to AI implementation.

The AI readiness assessment is performed with the Now Assist Readiness Evaluation application. For more information, see [Now Assist Readiness Evaluation in Now Assist Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/intelligent-experiences/now-assist-center-readiness-evaluation.md).

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Now Assist Center** or **Workspaces** &gt; **Now Assist Center**.

    The Now Assist Center opens to the home page.

    The card in the AI readiness section displays a **Start AI readiness assessment** button if the assessment has not yet been run.

    \[Omitted image "now-assist-center-home-ai-readiness.png"\] Alt text: AI readiness shown in Now Assist Center.

2.  Select **Start AI readiness assessment** to run the assessment of your instance.

    The AI readiness assessment may take several minutes to run.

    After the assessment is complete, the results display in a card showing your overall AI readiness status.

    \[Omitted image "now-assist-center-home-ai-readiness-overall.png"\] Alt text: Card showing the summary results of the overall AI readiness assessment.

3.  Re-run the overall AI readiness assessment at any time.

    1.  Select AI readiness assessment \(\[Omitted image "icon-now-assist-center-nav-readiness.png"\] Alt text: AI readiness assessment icon.\) in the side navigation bar.

        The AI Readiness Assessment tab opens.

        \[Omitted image "now-assist-center-ai-readiness-page.png"\] Alt text: AI Readiness Assessment tab showing a general assessment summary and a list of assessments.

    2.  Select **Re-run assessment**.

4.  Re-run the individual AI readiness assessment for specified area of your instance at any time.

    1.  Select AI readiness assessment \(\[Omitted image "icon-now-assist-center-nav-readiness.png"\] Alt text: AI readiness assessment icon.\) in the side navigation bar.

        The AI Readiness Assessment tab opens.

    2.  Select **View assessment** in the card of an individual assessment.

        The View Assessment tab opens showing the detailed results of a readiness assessment for the specified area of your instance.

        \[Omitted image "now-assist-center-ai-readiness-details.png"\] Alt text: View Assessment tab showing the detailed results of a readiness assessment.

    3.  Select **Re-run assessment**.

        **Note:** The label of the button includes the assessment name. In the example of running the assessment for AI Search, the button label is **Re-run AI Search assessment**.


## What to do next

Review the readiness assessments that the Now Assist Readiness Evaluation has identified for your instance. For more information, see [View your AI readiness assessment in Now Assist Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/intelligent-experiences/view-ai-readiness-now-assist-center.md).
