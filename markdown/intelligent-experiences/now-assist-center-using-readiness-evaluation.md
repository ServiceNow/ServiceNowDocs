# Using Now Assist Readiness Evaluation in Now Assist Center

Use Now Assist Readiness Evaluation to help you prepare to launch generative AI and agentic AI for your organization.

The Now Assist Readiness Evaluation features in Now Assist Center help you find actionable items in your implementation preparation and provides direct links to improve those areas.

-   **[Run the AI readiness assessment job in Now Assist Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/intelligent-experiences/now-assist-center-run-assessment-job.md)**  
Run the AI readiness assessment to analyze your instance and evaluate its readiness for AI adoption.
-   **[View your AI readiness assessment in Now Assist Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/intelligent-experiences/view-ai-readiness-now-assist-center.md)**  
Review the readiness assessments that the Now Assist Readiness Evaluation has identified for your instance.
