Primary connectors are developed, made available, and supported by ServiceNow.

## Primary connectors in preview

Some primary connectors are considered in preview. These connectors are developed and supported by ServiceNow, but are still being enhanced to include all planned functionality. While in preview, a connector may have limitations in platform support or available features.

Primary connectors in preview are fully functional for their documented scope and receive the same ServiceNow support as other primary connectors. For details on specific functionality limitations, see [KBB0010487](https://support.servicenow.com/kb?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KBB0010487).
