# View the XmlTemplate methods

After configuring the XmlTemplate connector, you can expose its methods.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Data Modellers &gt; XMLTemplate**.

2.  Drag the XmlTemplate connector under Global Objects in Project Explorer.

3.  Expand the Global Objects node.

4.  Double-click the XmlTemplate connector.

    The methods appear under the Object Explorer pane.
